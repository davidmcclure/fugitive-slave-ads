Transcript: Ran away<BR>FROM the subscriber on the 10th of December last, a Negro Woman named SYLVIA, about 35 years of age; she is very black, low set and chunkey made; has rather a down look when spoken to, and a very palavering tongue. She makes use of her left hand mostly when at work. It is likely she has attempted to pass as a free woman.<BR>Any person that will take her up and lodge her in Jail so that I get her again or give me any information of her, shall be well rewarded for any trouble they may be at, &c.<BR>SAMUEL SUGG.<BR>Wake county, near Raleigh.<BR>May 25    60eo3t
