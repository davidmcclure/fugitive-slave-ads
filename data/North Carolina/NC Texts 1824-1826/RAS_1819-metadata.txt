Transcript: Stop the Runaways.<BR>$20 REWARD.<BR>ON the night of the 6th inst. two negro men ranaway from the Subscriber: one named MANUAL, about 45 years of age, 5 feet 7 or 8 inches high, somewhat bow-legged, with a scar over his left eye, and one on his upper lip, probably occasioned by fighting; very intelligent and artful; has some knowledge of the blacksmith's business; plays on the violin.<BR>DANIEL is 18 or 19 years of age, pleasant countenance, round shouldered, his legs somewhat crooked.<BR>Manual was purchased by me from John S. Nelson, in Newbern, and Daniel from James G. Cuthbert, of that place, and they will no doubt endeavor to return to Newbern or vicinity.<BR>The above reward and all reasonable expenses will be paid on their delivery to me or confinement in jail so that I get them again, or 10 dollars for either.<BR>A. McLAUGHLIN.<BR>Stewartsville, Richmond county, July 9--4t-pd.
