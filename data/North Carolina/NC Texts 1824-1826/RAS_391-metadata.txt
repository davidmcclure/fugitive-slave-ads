Transcript: $50 Reward.<BR>RAN away from the farm of the subscriber not long since, two negro Boys of the following description: AARON, about 20 years old, 5 feet 8 inches high, and stout in proportion to his height; a dirty black skin, wide face and small eyes.<BR>DAVID , 18 years old, about the height of Aaron, but not so square built. He is a dark mulatto, nearly black; his eyes are above the common size.<BR>They will probably take up about Raleigh, or in the neighborhood of Colonel Philemon Hawkins, in Warren. The above reward will be given for their apprehension and delivery to me, or 30 dolls, for Aaron and 20 for David.<BR>WILL.[William] A. BLOUNT.<BR>Beaufort county, May 15.    60tf
