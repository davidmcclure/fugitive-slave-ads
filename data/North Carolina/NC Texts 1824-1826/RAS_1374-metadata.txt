Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY, on Monday the 29th of November, a Negro Boy named DAN, about 18 years of age, 5 feet 4 or 5 inches high, black complexion, of a pleasing countenance, and stammers when accused of any thing improper;--had on new trowsers of Sagatha, and Jacket of the same--considerably worm.  He has a brother at the plantation of John F. Smith, Esq. on Brice's Creek, where he is probably lurking.  The above reward and reasonable expenses will be paid for his delivery to the Subscriber.<BR>JAMES G. CUTHBERT.<BR>December 4, 1824--'50tf.
