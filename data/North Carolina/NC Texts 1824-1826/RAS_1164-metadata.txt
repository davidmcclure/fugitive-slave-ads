Headline: Forty dollars reward
Publishing newspaper: Elizabeth City, N.C. : Elizabeth City Star and North Carolina Eastern Intelligencer
Date of advertisement: 1826-05-27
Page: 3
Time period (decade): 1820-1829
Person placing ad: Samuel Nixon
Presumed or intended destination: Perquimans County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Harry
Slaveholder: Samuel NIxon
Transcript: Forty Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN away from the Subscriber last Feb'ry twelve months ago, without any provocation, negro man<BR>HARRY,<BR>about 25 years old, five feet 8 or 9 inches high, very stout and well made, little yellow, very thick lips, and is inclined to be bow-legged. He has a father and mother living near Nixon's Bridge, in Perquimans county, where I have been informed he is harbored. The above reward will be paid if delivered to me, or secured in jail so that I get him.<BR>Samuel Nixon.<BR>May 27.
