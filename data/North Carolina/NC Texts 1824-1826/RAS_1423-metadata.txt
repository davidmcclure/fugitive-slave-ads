Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber's plantation, about four miles from Fayetteville, on the 1st inst. a Negro Boy named SHADRACK; black complexion, about 17 years of age, and about 5 feet high; had on, when he went away, a dark striped round jacket, blue striped pantaloons, and a straw hat.  The above reward will be given, and all expenses paid, on delivering him to me, in Fayetteville, or to Edward Mooney, at the plantation, or to any Jailor in the State.<BR>JOHN KELLY.<BR>Jan. 20.--98--3tp
