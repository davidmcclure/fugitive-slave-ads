Transcript: Stop the Runaway.<BR>MY old man ABRAM went off this morning--he is the same fellow that has been out for several years and passed as a free man by the name of Reuben Wiggins--he is a stout fellow, brown complexion, fifty-three or four years old, his hair a good deal white, and a scar on the nose that I gave him when brought home. A generous reward will be given for securing the said negro in any jail, or delivered to me.<BR>J. BISHOP.<BR>The said fellow may wish to get to Plymouth, and pass again as a free man.<BR>Dec. 1, 1824.<BR>37-tf
