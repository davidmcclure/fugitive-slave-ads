Transcript: Runaway<BR>FROM the subscriber on the 1st day of January last, a certain negro boy by the name of ISAAC, about 5 feet 9 or 10 inches high, yellow complected, long straight nose, piques up his forehead, broad square shoulders, hollow-footed.  No other marks, except his back well marked with hickory.  It may be that he has procured a free pass.  Any person taking up said negro and confining him in Jail and contriving me word so that I get him again, shall receive Fifty Dollars.<BR>WM.[William] BRIDGE.<BR>Jackson's Creek, Randolph} county, N.C.
