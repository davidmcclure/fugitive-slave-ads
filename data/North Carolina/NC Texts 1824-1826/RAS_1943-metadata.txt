I will give the above reward, if caught in my county, or fifteen dollars if caught in either of the counties of Wake, Franklin, Chatham or Caswell, and pay all reasonable charges<BR>SIMMONS SOUTHERLAND.<BR>March 24, 1825.<BR>14-3tp
