Transcript: 10 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 24th ultimo, his negro man ABRAHAM. He is about five feet ten inches high, dark complected, stout built, weighs upwards of 200 lbs, and has a plausible countenance. He was raised in Gates county, and will probably aim to get there again. He was brought from there by Abraham Harrell and William W. Stedman, Esqr's, and I purchased him at Pittsborough, Chatham county. I will give ten dollars reward to any person who will apprehend the said fellow, and lodge him in Jail, or deliver him to me in Chatham county.<BR>ABNER BUCHANAN.<BR>Dec. 6, 1824.   50-3tp
