Transcript: Runaway<BR>LAST NIGHT FROM THIS CITY,<BR>A bright Mulatto Man, about 25 years of age, about 5 feet 6 inches high, well built, black straight hair, named Charles, belonging to the Estate of the late Dr. J. Battle--had on blue Broad Cloth, and had other good clothes with him, and will probably pass for a freeman.  Said Negro carried with him a yellow Sorrel Horse, belonging to said Estate, 6 years old this Spring, about five feet high, in good order, his hind feet white, has a star in his forehead and thin switch tail.  His Ears pretty large; walks and paces well.  He also carried with him an old saddle and a good Bridle.<BR>A handsome Reward will be given to any person who shall apprehend said mulatto, and lodge him in any Jail, and give notice thereof to the subscriber.  A Reward will also be given for the Horse.<BR>GREEN BOBBITT.<BR>Raleigh, March 21,    41
