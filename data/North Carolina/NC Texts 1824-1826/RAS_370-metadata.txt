Transcript: $25 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, living in Chatham County, North-Carolina, near the Gulf on Deep River, about the first of April last, a negro man by the name of Israel. He is black, about twenty-eight years old, and about five feet three or four inches high. He has a narrow face with a very large mouth--two of his upper foreteeth being out, one on each side, leaves two in the middle very large; he has a down look with a jarring voice, is eyes inclined to be red, his clothing when he left me was a white cotton shirt and pantaloons.  The above reward will be given to any person that will deliver said negro to me.<BR>THOMAS JINKINS.<BR>June 12th, 1826.     70 8wp.
