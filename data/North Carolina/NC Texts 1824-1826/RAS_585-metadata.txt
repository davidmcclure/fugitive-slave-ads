Transcript: Twenty-Five Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber about 5 months since, a negro man named JERRY.  He formerly belonged to the estate of the late Richard D. Spaight, Esq.[Esquire] and is well known in Newbern.  He is about 27 years of age, well made, 5 feet 8 or 10 inches high, and has a large scar on one of his shoulders.  It is supposed he is lurking about the plantations of John R. Donnel or of Richard D. Spaight, Esqrs [Esquires], where he has connexions.  The above reward will be given to any person who will confine said negro in Jail so that I get him.<BR>ZACHARIAH DAVIS.<BR>Lenoir County, June 11, 1825--'78 21.
