Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 17th instant, a Negro man named CHARLES, about 5 feet 8 inches high, and near 40 years of age, inclined to baldness; dark complected, one of his little fingers injured by a burn when small, has a scar on his face by the bite of another negro; he is a great singer; has with him various suits of clothes, among which are a plaid coat and gray cloth pantaloons; he has some notion of spelling.  The above reward will be given to any person apprehending the said negro, or lodging him in any jail so that I get him<BR>SION ALFORD.<BR>Alfordsville, April 26, 1826--655t<BR>The Editors of the Raleigh Register will please publish this for one month, and forward their account to Alfordsville, N.C. for payment.
