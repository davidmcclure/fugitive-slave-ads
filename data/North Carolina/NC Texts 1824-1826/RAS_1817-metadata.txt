Transcript: Runaways.<BR>A FEW days ago SAM and SILVA his wife, ranaway from the subscriber.  Sam, 23 years of age, is of common stature, has a face quite black, and is apt to smile when he is spoken to.  Silva, 27 years of age, is not tall, but corpulent.  They went off at the same time and are probably together; they carried the chief part of their clothes; Sam had a coat of green baize.  Whoever will apprehend them and give information thereof, will be well rewarded by their owners, who reside in the western extremity of Moore county, N.C.><BR>RODERICK McAULEY,<BR>KENNETH CLARKE.<BR>August 21, 1825--30tf
