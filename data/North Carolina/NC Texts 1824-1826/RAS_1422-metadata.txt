Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber's Plantation on Cape Fear, in June last, a negro man named BEN, aged about 40 years.  He is a very large man, of black complexion, and has no particular marks about him that I recollect.  If Ben will come in and go to his work, he will be forgiven: if he is taken and lodged in jail, or delivered to Frederick Moore, the above reward will be given.<BR>J.A. CAMERON.<BR>Fayetteville, Sept.[September] 12--32tf
