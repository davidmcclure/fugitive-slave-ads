Transcript: Runaway.<BR>FIVE DOLLARS will be given as a Reward, for apprehending and delivering to me, living near Reves's Cross Roads, in Wake County, N.C. a Mulatto Boy, named RANSOM BURNS.  He had on when he went off, a dark mixed home spun coat and waistcoat, a copperas coloured pair of pantaloons, a wool hat, and carried with him a yellow coloured pair of pantaloons.  I hereby forewarn all persons whatsoever, from employing or harbouring him, under the penalty of the law.<BR>JOHN CHAVIS.<BR>Dec. 25, 1824
