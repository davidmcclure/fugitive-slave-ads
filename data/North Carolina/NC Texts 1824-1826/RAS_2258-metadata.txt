Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward. <BR>  WILL be given for the apprehension, and securing in Jail, so that I get her again, negro woman EDITH, aged 21 years or thereabouts. Said negro is a stout, well set woman, rather inclined to be yellow, with a small scar over her right eye; she has the marks of the whip on her shoulders and arms. She eloped some time in September last, but was seen in and about Raleigh, not more than two weeks since, where she pass as a free woman. At which time, report says, she went off in company with a certain free man of color named Frank Anderson. It is believed that they shaped their course towards Franklin, where F. Anderson formerly resided. <BR>  GREEN ALFORD. <BR>  Wake county, 18 miles east of Raleigh, Dec. 14, 1824. } 51-tf
