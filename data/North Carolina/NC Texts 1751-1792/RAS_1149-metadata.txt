Transcript: June 25, 1753
RAN away from the Subscriber, in Johnston County, about the Middle of April last, a Negroe Fellow named Sam, a well set Fellow, speaks good English, about 5 Feet 4 Inches high: He has been taken up lately, and committed to Newbern Goal[sic], from which he was delivered to Mr. John Smith, in order to be carried to his Master; but broke out of Custody of the said Smith, near the South-We[?] Bridge.
Whoever apprehends the said Slave, so that his Master may have him again, shall have Twenty Five Shillings Reward, besides what the Law allows.
John Dickson.
