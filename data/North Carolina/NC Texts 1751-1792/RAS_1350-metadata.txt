Transcript: THIS is to give all Persons Notice, that there is a Negro Fellow named HOLLOW, detained from me; he is about 30 Years of Age, a shot well set Fellow, has a scald Head, is a good Cooper, likewise a Corker, and is a sensible Fellow. Whoever will take the said Fellow and bring him to me, shall have FIVE POUNDS reward, paid by <BR> BENJAMIN BLOUNT.<BR> March 10, 1775.
