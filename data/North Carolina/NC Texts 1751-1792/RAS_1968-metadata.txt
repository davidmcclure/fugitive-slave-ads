Transcript: FIVE POUNDS REWARD,<BR> RAN away from the Subscriber, about the first of Februry, a Multatto or Mustee Slave, called Tony, about 24 Years of Age, with long stiff black Hair, and greatly the Looks and Colour of an Indian: He is about 5 feet 9 Inches high and strong limbed, and is used to work at the carpenter Trade: Had on when he went away, a homespun Jacket, and old Coat grit around him with a broad buff Belt, and a large Brass Buckle. Whoever brings the said Slave to the subscriber in Newbern, or procure him so as to be recovered, shall receive Five Pounds Reward, and all reasonable charges.<BR>William Powell.
