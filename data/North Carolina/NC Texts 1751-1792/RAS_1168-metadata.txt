Transcript: NEWBERN, April 3.<BR> TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR> RUN away from this place a negro fellow named SALEM, about 5 feet 9 inches high, lusty and well made, speaks good English, and is used to the river trade; had on when he went away a green jacket, dirty trowsers, blue stockings, new shoes, white metal buckets, and a chip has; he is supposed to have down the river, as he took with him a small boat marked on the stern, H.S. Whoever brings the said slave and back to Newbern, shall have the above reward.<BR> FRANCES BATCHELOR.
