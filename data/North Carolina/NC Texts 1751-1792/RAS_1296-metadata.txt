Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber, on the 11th inst. (August) a likely Negro Man by the name of Will, formerly the property of Mr Hance Bond of Halifax.  He is about six feet high 20 years old, spare made, black complexion, his hair grows very low down his forehead; he can read and write tolerable well, and it is expected he will forge a pass and endeavour to pass as free.  Also runaway about the same time, a Negro Boy, by the name of Jack about 16 years old, well made, black complexion.<BR>Any person apprehending and securing or delivering them to me, shall receive the above reward<BR>JOHN PONS
