Transcript: Three Pounds Reward.<BR>RUN AWAY from the subscriber, the 27th of October last, a Negro Boy, named CHARLES; had on when he went away a red jacket, oznabrigs shirt and trowsers: he bends his knees to his right side as he walks, and appears as if his right leg was the shortest.  He has been once to sea and will probably endeavour to slip himself on board some vessel.  Whoever will secure said boy so that he may be got again, shall receive the above reward.<BR>HENRY HARRAMOND.<BR>Bertie county, Dec. 27.  60
