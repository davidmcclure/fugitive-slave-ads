Transcript: Run away, on the 11th of September last, from the Subscriber living in Bladen, a Negro Fellow named WILLIAM, aged about 25 Years, and about 5 Feet 8 Inches high; is well made, has lost some of his Upper fore-Teeh, this Country born, Sensible, and speaks English very well; he is a good Cooper and very handy at Carpetners Work. Whoever secures the Negro WILLIAM, so that his Master may have him again, shall have Five Pounds Reward, paid by <BR> JOHN LUCAS.
