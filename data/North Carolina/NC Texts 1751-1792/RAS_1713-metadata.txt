Transcript: RUN-AWAY, or KIDNAPED, <BR> FROM the subscriber, on Friday the 13th instant, a mulatto   fellow named CHARLES, who is well known, in, and about Wilmington, that his person requires no   particular description.  The subscriber has great reason to believe that CHARLES has got on   board of Capt. Stewart's vessel, bound to Jamaica, in which went passenger, a Mr. Barnes,   merchant, who has resided in this town for some considerable time past. Any person that will   give such information as will enable me to prosecute the captain, or his securties to effect,   shall receive a reward of TWENTY POUNDS. <BR> James Geekie. <BR> June 16, 1788.
