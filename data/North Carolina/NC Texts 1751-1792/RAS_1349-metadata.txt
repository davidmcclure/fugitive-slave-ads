Transcript: Thirty pounds reward.<BR>THE subscriber having been informed, that some evil minded white person upon White-Oak river, in Jones County, harbours out a Negro fellow named PIT; who ran away about six months ago.  The above reward shall be paid to any person who will give information so that the person harbouring the said Negro may be prosecuted to conviction thereof--and whoever will deliver the said Negro to the subscriber, at his plantation near Wilmington, shall be paid ten pounds and all reasonable expences in conveying said negro.<BR>July 10.    W.H. HILL.
