Transcript: RUN away from the subscriber, a Negro Wench, about 35 years of age, of a yellow complexion, with a blemish in one of her eyes, occasioned by a cancer wart; her name is Easter. She will try to pass for a free woman.  I forewarn all persons from harbouring her; and any person who will apprehend said wench and bring her to me, shall receive satisfaction.<BR>DEVOTION DAVIS.<BR>Pasquotank, Feb. 11, 1790.
