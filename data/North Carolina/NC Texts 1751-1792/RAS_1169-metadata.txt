Transcript: NEWBERN, JULY 25, 1777.<BR>Three Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN away from the Subscriber, a Negro Man named Surry, about 5 Feet 6 Inches high, about 30 Years of Age, well made is rather yellowish, and carried with him many different Suits of Clothes. The said Fellow is a new Negro, that speaks pretty good English. He was formerly the Property of Govenor Tryon, and now belongs to the Estate of Isaac Edwards, deceased. Whoever secures him, so that I get him again, shall have the above Reward.<BR>RICHARD BLACKLEDGE, Jun.
