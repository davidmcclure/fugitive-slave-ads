Transcript: FIVE POUNDS REWARD.<BR>RUN away from the subscriber, on the 19th inst. a negro fellow named Peter, formerly the property of John Hall, who lived near Edenton. He is about six feet high, slim made, dark complection, about 50 years of age, had a brown great coat, much worn.  It is supposed he may have got a free pass by some means or other. Whoever will secure said negro, so that his mistress may get him again, shall receive the above reward, and all reasonable charges.<BR>THOMAS CHARLTON.<BR>Chowan County, April 22.    72
