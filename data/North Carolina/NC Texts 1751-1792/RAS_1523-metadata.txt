Transcript: RAN away from the Subscriber, a Negro Fellow, named Frank; he is a short thick Fellow, speakes good English, and is a fennble [?] Negroe. Had on when he went away, a very good thick Jacket, and carried a Womans Gown with him; is branded on the left Buttock with a P. is an old Offender, and a great Thief. Whoever brings him to me, than have Thirteen Shillings and Four Pence, Proclamation Money, Reward, besides the Country Allowance, paid by <BR> Samuel Johnston.
