Transcript: FIVE POUNDS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, in Jones County near Trenton, on the 21st of July: A Negro Wench named CLOE, aged 23 years, 5 feet 7 or 8 inches high, very black, her fore teeth somewhat detected[?]: Had on when she went away a home-spun Woollen Petticoat, a calico jacket, a hat covered with checker'd silk, a blue and white strip'd gown: It is likely she will change her name in order to pass for a free wench: The above reward will be given to any person who will bring her to me, or secure her so that I may get her again.<BR>JOSEPH REASONOVER.<BR>August 10, 1787.
