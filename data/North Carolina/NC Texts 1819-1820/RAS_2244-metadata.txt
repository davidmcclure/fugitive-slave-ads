Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber in April last, a negro woman by the name of Phoebe, who carried off her child a female; she carried off two dutch blankets and two homespun suits of clothes. She is a tall slender yellow complected woman, large eyes, thin visage, and long feet and hands, her child is about sixteen or seventeen months old. --She has been lurking this summer, about Philip Silers's in the west end of Chatham county, who owns her mother. I will give the above reward to any person who will confine her in any jail in North Carolina, and all reasonble expences paid.<BR>38-4t.    JOHN A. RAMSEY.
