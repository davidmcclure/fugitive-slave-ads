Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, in September last, a Negro woman named JENNY, of a middle size, yellow complexion, and has lost some of her foreteeth.  She formerly belonged to Genl.[General] Croom of Lenoir county, and has a husband by the name of Dave, lately the property of Wm.[William] Palmer, of Batcheller's creek, Craven county.<BR>The above reward will be given to any person who will secure said wench so that the subscriber gets her again.<BR>All persons are forwarned from harboring, employing, or carrying her away, under the penalty of the law.<BR>VALENTINE RICHARDSON.<BR>March 13, 1819.--5tf
