Any person apprehending said fellow and delivering him to me or so confining him in jail so that I get him shall receive the abvoe reward, and all reasonable expences paid by me.<BR>SBRAH[SARAH] BONNER,<BR>Granville county, near the}<BR>Indian Grave Hill, Oct. 28} 51
