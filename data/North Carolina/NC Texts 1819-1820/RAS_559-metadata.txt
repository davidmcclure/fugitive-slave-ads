Slave name(s): Hanna
Slaveholder: Andrew Mathieu
Transcript: 25 Dollar Reward.<BR>  RAN AWAY from the subscriber, a short time since,a Negro Woman, named HANNAH, 27 or 28 years old, middling size. It is deemed unnecessary to describe her more particularly, as she is generally known in this town and the adjoining settlements, in some of which she is supposed to be lurking. Five dollars will be given to any one who will apprehend said negro woman and secure her in gaol; and for information of the person or persons who harbor her, twenty dollars will be paid, on conviction.<BR> ANDREW MATHIEU.<BR>Salisbury, July 3, 1820.
