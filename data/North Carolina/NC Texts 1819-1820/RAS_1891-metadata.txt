Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR> RUNAWAY on the 20th inst. my negro Anthony, about twenty two years of age, very black, wide mouth, strongly made went off well dressed in which I am not able to describe, I suppose him to be about Five feet eight or nine inches high, I suppose he has made for the lower part of this state; as there some waggons going to Fayetteville about the 22d instant, and a negro were seen a lurking after the same, which I suppose to be the same one I have lots[lost], any person taking up said negro and confining him [in] any jail so I get him again shall be entitled to the above reward.<BR> FREDERICK MILLER<BR> Rowan County, N.C. 15 miles east of Salisbury Abotts[?] Creeks.<BR>F. MILLER.<BR>22 4w.
