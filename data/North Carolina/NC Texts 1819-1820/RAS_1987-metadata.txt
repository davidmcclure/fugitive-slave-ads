Date of advertisement: 1820-04-11
Page: 3.
Time period (decade): 1820-1829
Person placing ad: Thomas Granberry
Date of departure: 1829-03-31
County of residence: Perquimans County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Peter
Slaveholder: Thomas Granberry
Transcript: 50 Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber on Friday night the 31st March last, my Negro Man Peter. He is about 27 years old, 5 feet 8 inches high, very black, a pleasing countenance, and when spoken to, is apt to smile. The above reward will be paid if taken out of this State, or 25 Dollars if delivered to me or secured in any jail in the State so that I get him. All persons are cautioned against harboring or carrying him away.<BR>Thomas Granberry.<BR>Perquimans County, April 6, 1829.
