Transcript: 100 Dollars Reward<BR>WILL be given for apprehending and delivering to me, or confining in jail so that I get him, my Negro Boy<BR>JOE, commonly called Joe Benton. He is about 19 or 20 years of age, 4 feet 8 or 10 inches high, tolerable black, speaks quick, and is very knowing and artful. He went off on Friday night last without even the shadow of provocation. I will give the above reward if taken up without the limits of the the State, and secured as above, or 25 Dollars if taken up in any of the adjacent Counties, and 10 Dollars if taken up in this County, and all reasonable expences paid. Masters of Vessels and others are strictly forbidden from harboring, or carrying him off at their peril.<BR>James Coffield.<BR>Chowan, Aug. 20, 1820.
