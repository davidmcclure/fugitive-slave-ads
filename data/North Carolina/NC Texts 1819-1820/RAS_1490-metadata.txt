Transcript: One hundred dollars<BR>REWARD.<BR>RAN-AWAY from the Subscriber, in September last, two Negro Men,<BR>KIT & JOE,<BR>KIT is about 32 years of age, 5 feet 6 or 7 inches high, yellowish complection, with large freckles-- his teeth are broad and far apart, and he has a smiling countenance.<BR>JOE is a stout made fellow, nearly 6 feet high, of a yellowish complexion, a little knock-kneed, walks very wide--has lost some of his toes by the frost, and is a sulky looking fellow.<BR>The above reward will be paid for apprehending and securing in Jail the above named Negroes, so that I get them, or Fifty Dollars for either.<BR>Edmund Hatch.<BR>Craven County, April 27, 1820--110 5.
