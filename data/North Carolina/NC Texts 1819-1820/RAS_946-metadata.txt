Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, two negro fellows, named BILL and NACE, belonging to Edward P. Hall, and hired by the subscriber for the present year. Bill is about 35 years of age, nearly 6 feet high, black complexion and slender made. Nace is about 50, 5 feet 6 inches high, of a yellowish complexion. They have both lived sometime in Wilmington. The above reward will be given for their delivery, or Ten Dollars for the delivery of either, to the subscriber, or to the jailor of New Hanover.<BR>J. H. SIMMS.<BR>January 23         3tp
