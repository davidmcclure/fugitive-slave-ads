Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY in October last my negro woman Chaney, 25 years old tall and likely, of a Copper Colour, wears her hair platted around her head. Chaney was purhcased by Mr. Green Davis of Augustin Alston in Warren county where she probably may be lurking. I would be thankful to any person that will give information of said Negro and will pay $20 for confining her in Jail or delivering her to me.<BR>W. CRENSHAW, 16 miles North of Raleigh.<BR>N.B. I will take $550 for Chaney. If no Expences attend her.
