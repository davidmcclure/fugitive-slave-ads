Date of advertisement: 1819-09-24
Page: 4
Time period (decade): 1810-1819
Person placing ad: Leonard Craglow
Date of departure: 1819-08-15
County of residence: Rowan County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Gilbert
Slaveholder: Leonard Craglow
Transcript: Stop the Runaway.<BR> RANAWAY from the subscriber on the night of the 15th inst. my negro man GILBERT, aged between twenty-five and thirty years. He is about six feet high, straight built, very dark complexion, black gums, and very white teeth, and in common of grinning countenance when spoken to.--About the time that the negro man Gilbert ran off, an idle Girl of the name of Sally Henderson, left this neighborhood; and there are atrong[sic] reasons to believe that she seduced the boy away with her: Sally Henderson may be easily known, her eyelieth[?] are double, she of common women height, somewhat of a tawny complexion (tho' healthy) dark brown hair, straight and tolerable delicately built. A reward of ten dollars will be given to any one who will apprehend the said negro, so that I get him again: -And if delivered to me in Rowan County, Wethrows Creek: all reasonable expences shall be paid.<BR> LEONARD CRAGLOW.<BR>August 31, 1819.
