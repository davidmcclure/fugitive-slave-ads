Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY, on the 14th instant, a young black fellow named <BR>DICK,<BR>or he may call himself REDDICK, about twenty-four years of age, five feet, six inches high.  He wore a Cotton Homespun Shirt and Trowsers; has large fleshy hands and feet--a small scar on one cheek, under the eye.<BR>He was enticed away, and went in company with a fellow known in Newbern by the name of George Fewclothes, a noted bad fellow, and is hired by me of Mr. John Fonveille, and is also runaway.-- They will probably harbour among George's acquaintance in Newbern, and above and below that place, on Neuse River.<BR>S. FOSCUE.<BR>Jones County, August 16th, 1820.<BR>126--tf.
