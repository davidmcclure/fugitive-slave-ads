Transcript: Five Dollars Reward. <BR>RAN away from the subscriber, about the 20th of March last, a Negro man, named ISAAC, about twenty-two years of age, yellow complected, five feet six or eight inches high, two of his under fore teeth out.  Had on when he went away, a round-about homespun coat and homespun woollen pantaloons.  The above reward will be given, if taken up in this county, or ten dollars if out of the county, or confined in any jail, and all expenses paid. <BR>Alexr.[Alexander] Borland <BR>Orange County, April 28 13--3
