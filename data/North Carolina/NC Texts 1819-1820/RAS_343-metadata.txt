Transcript: $25 REWARD<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber living in Caswell county, N C in th emonth of June, 1818, a Negro Boy named CHARLES.? He is black complected, about 20 years of age, 5 feet 4 or 5 inches high, well made, and inclined to be slim, and has an open, prepossession countenance.? The last time I heard of him was his having broken jail in Martin county, N.C. in April last, where he was apprehended--He had a free pass, and had been passing as a free man for some time--there was another fellow in company with him, who also had a free pass.? it is more than probable they will continue together and obtain a new set of passes.? Charles was purchased of Mrs. Little of Sussex county, Virginia, where it is probable he may be--or he may remain lurking about in the lower part of this state near Newbern and Edenton, where he as been for some time.<BR>Any person who will secure said fellow Charles so that I get him again, shall have the above reward, and all reasonable expences paid.? Any letters addressed to me at Leasburg, Caswell, N.C on the above, will be received and attended to by<BR>WILLIAM FULLER.<BR>June 17.??? 83
