Any person who will apprehend said negro, and deliver him to the subscriber, living in the town of Williamsborough, N.C. or secure him so that he can get him again, shall be entitled to the above reward, and all reasonable expenses paid.<BR> JOHN REEKS.<BR>January 15, 1819.--tf.
