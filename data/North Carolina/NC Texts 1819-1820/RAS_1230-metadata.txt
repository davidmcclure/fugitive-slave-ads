Transcript: Five Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, a Negro man named NOAH, belonging to the estate of Hyman, and hired to me by Mr. Samuel Oliver.  Noah is a very impudent fellow, five feet 9 inches high, a great dancer, & called by some, Godbold.  I will give the above reward to any person who will bring said Negro to me, or secure him any jail so that I get him, and will pay all reasonable expenses.<BR>Masters of vessels and other, are forwarned from harboring or employing said Negro, under the penalty of the law.<BR>George Reid.<BR>Newbern, May 15, 1819.--60tf
