Transcript: NOTICE<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, on Tuesay evening the 22d of March, instant, one Negro man named JAMES, about thirty-three or four years old, five feet eight or ten inches high, very black complection [sic] with remarkably red eyes.? He carried off with him a Blue Broad Cloth Coat and a pair of White Dimity Pantaloons, and was cloathed with Green mixed Homespun ; he was lame in one of his heels, occasioned by a burn, has a scar on the left side of his Neck.? This fellow has made a like attempt before, and was apprehended in Washington, N.C. If any person will stop said Negro, and confine him so that I get him again, shall have a Reward of TEN DOLLARS.<BR>LOFTIS PIPEN.<BR>Franklin county, N.C. March 27.--71-Stp
