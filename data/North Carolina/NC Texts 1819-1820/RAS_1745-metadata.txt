Transcript: Runaway,<BR>FROM the subscriber, a negro fellow named JIM, or Jim Gundy, aged about 30 years, about 5 feet 10 inches high, well made. I purchased him from Hinton Curtis, and he once belonged to Edward Tansil, of Franklin county, who now ownes[sic] his wife. I will give a reward of twenty dollars for his apprehension and delivery in Wake jail, and ten dollars if he is confined in any other jail so that I get him. He went off this morning and is now probably in Raleigh, or the neighborhood of Mr. Tansils.<BR>M. MORDECAI.<BR>Raleigh, February 17.<BR>7-tf
