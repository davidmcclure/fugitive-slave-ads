Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward<BR>WILL be given for apprehending and securing in jail so that I get her again, a Negro Woman named MOLLY, late the property of Miss Sophia Niell, of Edenton. She is about 24 or 26 years of age, tolerable black and lively. She left me on the road between Williamston and Tarborough, on Saturday evening the 15th inst. I will give the above reward, if secured as aforesaid, and all reasonable expences paid.<BR>William D. Smith<BR>Buncombe County, July 21,<BR>1820.
