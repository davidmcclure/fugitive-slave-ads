Transcript: 10 DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 3d inst. his Negro Woman SILVEY.  She is about 25 years old, 5 feet 2 or 3 inches high & one of her little fingers is draw'd up by fire.  The above Reward will be given for her alive, or fifty dollars for her head.  I do hereby forwarn all persons from harboring, employing or carrying her away, as I am determined to enforce the law against all offenders.<BR>EDEN T. JONES.<BR>Slocumb's Creek,<BR>March 13, 1820.   2w104.
