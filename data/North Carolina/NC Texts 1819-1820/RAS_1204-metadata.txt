Transcript: NOTICE<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, living in Johnston county, N.C. on the 12th inst. a Negro Man by the name of JACK He is of a black complexion, with a very flat nose, which was occasioned by a hurt when small, has some scars on his back from the whip, is about five feet six or eight inches high, stout built, quick spoken when spoken to. I suppose that he will try to get to Petersburg, Va I suppose that he will give a reward of ten dollars, if taken within the state and confined so that I get him again, and twenty dollars if out of the state.<BR>JOHN T.
