Transcript: $25 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 7th inst. a negro man named DICK, about six feet high, very dark complected, rather flat faced, heavy built, and about thirty years of age. This fellow formerly belonged to the estate of Henry G. Lewis, dec'd[deceased]. He is quick spoken, and affects much politeness.<BR>Any person apprehending and delivering said boy to the subscriber in Buncombe county North Carolina, shall be entitled to the above reward, or twenty dollars if secured in any jail so that I get him.<BR>BENJAMIN BRITTAIN.<BR>Ashville, N. C. 29th July, 1820.<BR>53-4ts.
