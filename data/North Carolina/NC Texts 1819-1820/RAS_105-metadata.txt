Transcript: RAN AWAY<BR>FROM the subscriber on Sunday night the 1st inst. a mulatto fellow named ISAAC, but it is likely he will go by the name of John--He is stout made, and about 20 or 25 years old, has large white eyes, and is about 5 1-2 feet high. Any person that will deliver said fellow to me near the public road leading from Salisbury to Charlotte, say 10 miles from Charlotte, shall be reasonably rewarded for their trouble, and all necessary expences paid, or if they will seucre him in any Jail so that I get him again.<BR>HUGH GALLWAY.<BR>August 3, 1819 39 3w
