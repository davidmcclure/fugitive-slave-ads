Transcript: $10 Reward<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 10th day of November past, a Negro Man named HARDY, about 25 years of age, of small stature, is lame in the right hip which makes his right leg appear shorter than the other; has a scar near the mould of his head about the size of a dollar.  I purchased said fellow of Mr. May, who bought him of Mr. Umphries in Onslow county--for which place I expect he is making his way.<BR>The above reward of ten dollars will be given to any person that will apprehend the said negro and confine him in any jail within this State, or twenty dollars if delivered to the subscriber near Miltonsville, Anson county, N.C.<BR>JOHN SINCLAIR, Sen [Senior]<BR>January 1.    15 4t
