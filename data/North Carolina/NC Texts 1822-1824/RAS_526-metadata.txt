Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber on the 19th of October last, a Negro Man named GEORGE, (commonly call'd George Fewclothes.) He is a stout well built fellow, and very black, 32 or 33 years old.  Also, on the 24th ult. a Negro Man named PROVIDENCE, of common size, and yellow complexion, --about the age of George.<BR>I will give the above reward of Twenty Dollars for the apprehension and delivery to me or securing the said Negroes in Jail so that I get them again, or Ten Dollars for either, if taken separately.<BR>LEWIS BRYAN.<BR>Bloomfield, Lenoir county, Dec. [December] 15, 1822
