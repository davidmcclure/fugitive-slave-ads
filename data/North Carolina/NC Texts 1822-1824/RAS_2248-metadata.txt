Transcript: Fifty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, on the 21st Feburary last, a dark mulatto fellow named BEN; five feet eight or nine inches high, twenty one or two years of age; very quick spoken; with a scar on one of his hams, occasioned by a wen, cured by rowelling; it is said he has a scar on one his cheeks, which was occasioned by the bite of a dog. It is expected he is passing for a free man. Any person apprehending the said fellow, shall receive the above reward.<BR>G. WIMBERLY.<BR>Johnston County, Oct. 1, 1823.  40-tf
