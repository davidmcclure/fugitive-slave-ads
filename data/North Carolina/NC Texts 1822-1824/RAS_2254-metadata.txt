Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, living in Chatham county, on the 28th of August last, my boy DEAN, about 32 years old, five feet ten or eleven inches high, dark complected, thick lips and very large feet. He had on when he left me, white cotton pantaloons and shirt, and cool dyed coat. The balance of his clothing not recollected. The above reward wil be given, if delivered to me, or lodged in any Jail, so that I get him again. <BR>  GILLIAM UPCHURCH. <BR>  September 8, 1824.  37-tf
