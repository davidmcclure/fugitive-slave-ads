Transcript: $5 reward.<BR>RAN AWAY in February last a negro man named<BR>HARDY--<BR>He is about five feet 7 or 8 inches high; a scar on either the right or left cheek, which not recollected; dark complexion--as he is well known a further discription[sic] is deemed unnecessary, he is probably lurking in the vicinity of this town or about Goose Creek, where he has a wife--HARDY was the property of Samuel Young dec'd.<BR>The above reward will be paid any person that will apprehend and deliver the said fellow to me or secure him in jail so that I get him again.<BR>All persons are cautioned against harboring, employing or carrying him off under the penalty of the law.<BR>J. CHERRY.<BR>Washington, April 16, 1824--3w438.
