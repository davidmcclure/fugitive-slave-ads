Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the night of the 16th inst. his negro man PETER, twenty-two years old, about five feet eight or ten inches high, stout built, tolerably black, board faced, with high cheek bones. It is probable he has a pass, and may attempt to go to Caswell county, where he has some acquaintances. The above reward will be paid for the apprehension and delivery of said boy to me, or secured in any Jail so that I get him again.<BR>AMBROSE BULLARD.<BR>Chatham County, Oct. 22, 1824.  44 4w
