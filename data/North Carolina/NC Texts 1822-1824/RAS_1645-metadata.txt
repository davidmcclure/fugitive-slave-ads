Transcript: RANAWAY<BR>FROM the Subscriber's Salt Works at Cape Look Out, a Negro man named GLOSTER, about 5 feet 7 or 8 inches high. Ten Dollars Reward will be given if he is taken within the county of Carteret, and Twenty Dollars if apprehended without the limits of the county, and lodged in Jail. When apprehended, it is requested that notice be given to Elijah Pigot or M.C. Stephens.<BR>JOHN D. HAWKINS.<BR>Nov. 6, 1823.
