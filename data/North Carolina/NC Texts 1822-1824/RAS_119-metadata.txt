Transcript: RAN AWAY<BR>FROM the Subscriber, on the 9th inst. a Negro Man named MARK, alias Mark Ralph.  He is about 6 feet high, 50 years old, a Carpenter by trade, and belongs to the estate of William Shepard, deceased, late of Newbern, in which place and its neighbourhood he is well known, and where he is probably lurking.--He has a wife in Washington.<BR>All reasonable expenses incurred in apprehending and committing him to the jails of Newbern or Beaufort County, or delivering him at Belle Vue, Pitt County, will be cheerfully paid by<BR>SAMUEL LATHAM.<BR>Nov. [November] 21, 1822.--'46 3w.
