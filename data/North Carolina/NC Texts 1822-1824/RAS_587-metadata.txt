Presumed or intended destination: Newbern (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Stepny;Sarah
Slaveholder: Fred'k [Frederick] Staton
Transcript: Twenty-Five Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber living on Lane's Creek, Anson county, on the 15th of August last, a negro man named STEPNY, about 40 years of age, 5 feet 2 inches high, has a large scar on the side of his belly, supposed to have been cut by a knife.  And a negro woman, (his wife,) named SARAH, about 25 years of age, 5 feet 3 inches high, very black.  The above negroes were raised in Newbern, and I suppose will endeavor to return to that place.<BR>A reward of $25 will be paid for apprehending and confining in jail the above mentioned negroes, or twelve dollars and fifty cents for either of them.  Or if delivered to me at my plantation, all reasonable expenses will be paid, in addition to the above reward.<BR>FRED'K [FREDERICK] STATON.<BR>Anson county, Sept. 25, 1824--3t85
