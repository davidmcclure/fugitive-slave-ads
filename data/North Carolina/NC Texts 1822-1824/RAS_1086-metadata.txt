Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS<BR>REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY, from the Subscriber, on the 8th of April last, a Negro Man named BACCHUS, and called by him Bacchus Hill, of light complexion, about 30 years of age, 5 feet 6 or 7 inches high, and by trade a Carpenter.<BR>Fifty Dollars will be paid for his apprehension and delivery, if taken out of the State, or Twenty-five Dollars if taken in the State and out of the County, and Twenty Dollars if taken in the county.<BR>Masters of vessels are cautioned against carraying[sic] away or harbouring the said runaway, as the law will be rigidly enforced against them.<BR>ABNER PASTEUR.<BR>June 11, 1822.--'21tf.
