Transcript: ONE HUNDRED DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the service of WM.[WILLIAM] P. FERRAND, a Negro man named VIRGIL.  He is about 20 years of age, tall and slender, and a very intelligent fellow.  It is probable, that he will endeavour to get to the North, and pass as a free man.  The above reward will be paid to any person who will deliver said negro at Swansonborough, or secure him in the Newbern jail. Masters of vessels, and all others, are forwarned from harbouring, employing, or carrying him away, under the penalty of the law.<BR>JAMES WADE.<BR>Swansborough, Sept. 17th, 1824. '39tf.
