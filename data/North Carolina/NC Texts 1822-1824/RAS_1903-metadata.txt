Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber a negro man named CAESAR,  formerly the property of Mrs. Strudwick; he is about fifty-five years of age, six feet high, stout and well made; he is inclined to be bald of the head and perhaps has lost some of his front teeth; he is humble and somewhat insinuating in his manners, and will no doubt endeavor to pass for a free man, and perhaps has papers to that effect.  Caesar's complexion is a dark mulatto.  It is expected that he is in or near the vicinity of Hillsborough.  Ten dollars reward will be given to any person who may apprehend and confine said negro in any jail in the state.<BR>Geo.[George] H. M'Millan.<BR>South Washington, Aug 10.  33-5w
