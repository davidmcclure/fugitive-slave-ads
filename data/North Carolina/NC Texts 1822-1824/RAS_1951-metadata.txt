Slave name(s): Sylva; Kimber
Slaveholder: John Daniel
Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, on the night of the 17th inst. two negroes--SYLVA, aged twenty-nine or thirty years and her son KIMBER aged about thirteen years.  The wench is spare and well made, of a yellow complexion, hangs her head down when spoken to, and is four or five months advanced in pregnancy. Her clothes are yellow filled in with copperas colour. The boy is large of his age, his complexion almost white; has also a down look; his dress, tow pantaloons and cotton shirt. Took with them a bundle of clothes and probably may change. The above reward with all necessray expenses will be paid to any person who will apprehend said negroes and deliveer them to the subscriber on the waters of Newhope, twelve miles east from Hillsborough, or confine them in any jail so that he can get them.<BR>John Daniel.<BR>Orange county, Aug. 29  R6-3wn
