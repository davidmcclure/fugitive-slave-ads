Transcript: $25 Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber, on the 18th ult. dark complected negro man named<BR>PLUTO;<BR>He is about twenty-six years of age, five feet ten inches high, spare built and is well known as the property of John Barrow;--It is supposed that he is lurking about Durham's Creek. The above reward will be given to any person that will apprehend him, if taken in the County of Beaufort, and secure him in jail so that I get him again, or deliver him to me. If taken out of the County aforesaid, the above reward, and in addition thereto, all reasonable expenses in conveying him to my plantation or lodging him in the jail of this County.<BR>Masters of vessels and others are hereby cautioned against harboring, employing or carrying him off under the penalty of the law.<BR>JOHN BARROW.<BR>Beaufort County, Dec. 5, 1823--tf421.
