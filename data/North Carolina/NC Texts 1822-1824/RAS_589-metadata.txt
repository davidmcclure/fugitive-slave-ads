Transcript: Twenty-five Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, about three years ago, a negro man named JACOB.  he is about thirty-five years of age, five feet six or seven inches high, about the common colour, toleraly active, has narrow feet, and a small scar over one of his eyes.  It is probable he has altered his name, as he frequently passes from Stantonsburg to Newbern.  He has a free wife by the name of Rancy Artis, living near Statonsburg, and it is likely he attempts to pass for a free man.-- The above Reward will be given to any person who shall deliver said Negro to me (living five miles above Stantonsburg,) or confine him in Jail, so that I get him again.<BR>JOEL NEWSOM.<BR>Wayne County, August 7, 1824--'34 36*
