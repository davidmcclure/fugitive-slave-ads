Transcript: $10 Dollars Reward <BR> RAN AWAY from the subscriber, living in Rockingham county, N.C. a negro fellow named ISAAC, about six feet one inch high, stout made, but inclined to be small at the waist, or rather a yellow complexion, and, as well as recollected has a small scar about his left temple. Took off with him a new home made twilled blanket; had on a new tow shirt, a new pair of thread and cotton pantaloons, and an old white hat. Any person taking up the said fellow and will deliver him to me, or lodge him in any jail, so that I get him, shall receive the above reward. <BR> ALEXANDER MARTIN. <BR> July 14, 1823.
