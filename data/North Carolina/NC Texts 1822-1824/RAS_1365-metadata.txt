Transcript: Thirty Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, two young Negroe[sic] men, to wit:--<BR>DAVE, formerly the property of Mr. Raiford of Fayetteville--and LEWIS, formerly the property of Mr. Adam of the same place. It is supposed that they are about Fayetteville where they were raised. Any person lodging the said Negroes in Fayetteville Jail, will receive the above reward by calling on Mr. Peter Mallett, or fifteen dollars for either of them.<BR>JOHN DEWITT.<BR>Nov 22, 1822.      26 9wpd
