Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber's Platnation in Hyde county, on the 25th of December last, a Mulatto Boy, named DANIEL, aged about 16 years.--He has had his thigh broke, and limps, particularly when he attempts to run.  He is the son of Tignor's Levin, who is well known in Newbern, or Mr. Benners' Plantation on Wilkinson's Point.  The above reward will be given to any person who will confine him in Newbern Jail.  All persons are forwarned from harbouring said boy under the penalty of the law.<BR>ELI SMALLWOOD.<BR>Jan. 24, 2824---5tf.
