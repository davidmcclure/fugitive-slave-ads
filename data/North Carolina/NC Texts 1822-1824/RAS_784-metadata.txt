Transcript: 50 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, on the 30th ultimo, two Negro fellows, CHARLES & HUMPRHEY.<BR>Charles is about forty years of age, six feet high, stammers a little, and had on when he left home a pair of twilled mixt negro cloth trowsers, and a jacket of negro cotton, purple dye.  He is probably lurking in the neighborhood of John Fonveille, in Craven County, where he has a wife.<BR>Humphrey is about 25 years of age, six feet high, has a down look, and had on an old green cloth surtout, twilled mixt negro cloth trowsers, and a jacket of purple negro cotton.  It is believed that he is lurking about Mr. Blackledges Plantation, in Lenoir County, where has a wife.<BR>The above reward will be given for said Negroes, or twenty-five Dollars for either of them, if they delivered to me, or secured in any jail so that I get them.<BR>Thomas H. Kean.<BR>Granville County, April 12, 1823.--64tf
