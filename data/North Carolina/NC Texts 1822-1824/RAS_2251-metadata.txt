Transcript: Thirty Dollars Reward.<BR>WILL be given for the delivery of NAT, a negro about about 20 years of age, who ranaway from me on the 7th ultimo, and is supposed to have gone to Hertford county, where he was raised. I purchased him from Solomon Shephard, who had him of a Mr. Jennings, of Hertford county, in December last. The above reward will given for his delivery, or 15 dollars for securing him in any jail in this State, so that I get him.<BR>WILL.[WILLIAM] POLK.<BR>March 24, 1824.   13-tf
