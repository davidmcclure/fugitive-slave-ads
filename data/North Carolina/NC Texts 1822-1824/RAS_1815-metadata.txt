Whoever will apprehend said runaway, and deliver him to me, or secure him in any jail, and give me information so that I get him again, shall be reasonably rewarded, and all necessary expenses paid.  He was raised in or near Lancaster District, S.C. and it is probable he way[may] make his way thither.<BR>NEILL SHAW.<BR>Big Rockfish, Robeson Co. Aug. 11, 1822.    2w
