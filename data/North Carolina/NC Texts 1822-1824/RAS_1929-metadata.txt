Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, a few weeks past, his Negro Man LEWIS.  He is of the common size, very black, his eyes small, speaks slow, and is parrot toed.  It is understood that he is in the neighborhood of Newbern, and his object may be to effect his escape by water; masters of vessels and others, are therefore cautioned against carrying him off.  The above reward and all reasonable expenses will be paid for his delivery to me, or confinement in jail.<BR>W.P. BIDDLE.<BR>Craven County, February 12, 1824--8
