Transcript: TWENTY-FIVE CENTS<BR>REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY, from the Subscriber, about the first of June last, a Mulatto slave named JERRY, 21 or 22 years of age, and 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high.  There is reason to believe that he is lurking about the Plantation of Thomas Battle, on New River, Onslow County, where he has a wife and other connexions [sic].  The law will be enforced (should proof be obtained,) against any person harbouring or employing said runaway.<BR>THOMAS H. HILL.<BR>July 19, 1823--78 79
