Page: 3.
Time period (decade): 1820-1829
Person placing ad: John Paine
Date of departure: 1829-04-12
Presumed or intended destination: Edenton (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Tom;Sophia
Slaveholder: John Paine
Transcript: $25 Reward.<BR>The above reward will be paid to any person or persons who will apprehend and deliver to me my Negro Man TOM, and Negro Woman SOPHIA, or Fifteen Dollars for Tom and Ten Dollars for Sophia if taken seperately; who took themselves off on Tuesday the 12th inst. Tom is about twenty five years of age five feet 8 or 10 inches high; dark complexion, quite stout and will weigh 180 or 190 pounds. He had on, a fur HAT, a black cassimere pantaloons both considerably worn. Sophia is almsot 20 years ofd 5 feet 4 or five inches high, they are both known in the neighborhood and also in the Town of Edenton, where it is probable they have bent their course. The above reward, as stated, will be paid for their delivery to me at my farm 12 miles about Edenton, near Mr. James Coffield's fishery. Masters of Vessels and all others are forbid harbouring or converying them off, as the law will be rigidly inforced [sic] against any who may offend.<BR>JOHN PAINE.<BR>April, 19th 1829.
