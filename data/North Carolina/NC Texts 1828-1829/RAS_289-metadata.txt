Transcript: $25 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber in February last, Negro JESSE, about 30 years of age, quite black, about six feet high, slender built, knockneed, and stutters somewhat when talking fast. This fellow's parents live in Jones County, this State, and he may probably be lurking there.  A reward of twenty-five Dollars will given for the delivery of him to me, or for his confinment in Jail, so that I get him.<BR>W.B. GRIST, <BR>Near Washington, N.C. <BR>April 28, 1820--30tf.
