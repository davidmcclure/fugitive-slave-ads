Slaveholder: Wm. [William] H. Borden;Alice Ward
Transcript: $20 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, sometime since, Negro Man TOM, and GRACE, his wife. TOM is about 5 feet 6 or 7 inches high, 30 years of age, dark complexion, and when spoken to, has rather a down look. He was purchased of Wright C. Stanly, Esq. and it is more than probable that he is lurking in the neighbourhood of Spring Garden, or Newbern. GRACE is the property of Mrs. Alice Ward, of this County. She is between 4 and 5 feet in height, about 30 years of age, and yellow complexion. The above reward, and all reasonable expences will be paid for their apprehension, and confinemnt in jail, so that I get them, or $10 for either of them. <BR>WM. [WILLIAM] H. BORDEN.<BR>Bordensville, Carteret Co. Aug. 23d, 1829.--pt 5
