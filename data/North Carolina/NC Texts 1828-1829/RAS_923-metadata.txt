Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>Ran away from the subscriber, a negro man, by the name of RANSOM, of a dark complexion, his thumb is down in his left hand, occasioned by the white swelling. I suppose he is in the neighbourhood of Benj.[Benjamin] Morgan's, in Franklin county, North Carolina. I will give a reward of twenty dollars if any person will deliver said negro to me, or confine him in the Jail of Louisburg or Oxford, or in any Jail in the State so that I get him again.<BR>GEORGE KITTRELL.<BR>Granville county, N.C.}<BR>19th May, 1829.}<BR>22-4w
