Transcript: $25 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber in August, 1827, a negre[sic] man, ISAAC. between thirty-five and forty years old, five feet 8 or 10 inches high, stout built[?}, very sensible, rather slow spoken. Isaac was raised in Edgecombe county, N.C. by Frederick Philips, and by him sold to the subscriber. I suppose that he is lurking in Edgecombe. The above reward will be given for his apprehension, if secured in any of the jails of this State so that I get him.<BR>JOHN PURCELL.<BR>Robeson County, N.C.}<BR>Jan. 26, 1829.}24-13
