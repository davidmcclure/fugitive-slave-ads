Transcript: $25 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the plantation of Arch'd[Archibald] Smith, in Robeson county, my Negro Boy JERRY, about [1]6 or 17 years of age, dark complected.  It is supposed, as his mother belongs to Mr. John Ford, of Marion District, South Carolina, that he may be lurking in that neighbourhood.  Any person who will deliver the said Boy to me, in Robeson county, or lodge him in Lumberton Jail, shall be entitled to the above reward, and all reasonable expenses paid.<BR>DANIEL SMITH.<BR>September 12, 1829.    41tf.
