Transcript: FIVE DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber on the 19th inst.a Negro woman named MINERVA, between 35 and 40 years of age: about 5 feet 5 inches high, and very black. She is probably lurking about Newbern; or in the neighbourhood of Fort Barnwell, as she has a husband living on the plantation of John Whitefield Esq. All persons are forewarned from harbouring or employing her, under penalty of the law. The above reward will be paid to any person who will apprehend and deliver said negro to me, or secure her in jail at Newbern.<BR>JOHN B. SMITH.<BR>Tivoli Grove, Jan. 31. -- 26tf
