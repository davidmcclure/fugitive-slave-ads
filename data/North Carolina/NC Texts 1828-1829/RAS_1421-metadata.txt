Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber's brickyard, at the Canal, on Tuesday the 1st of April, a negro man about 22 years old--about 5 feet 5 inches high, named BATE.  He has a scar near the right elbow, on his arm, from a burn.  He has a wife in Newbern--was formerly owned there, and is probably lurking about that Town.  The above reward will be given if he is taken in Craven or Carteret County, and secured in Jail, or Twenty Dollars if he is taken in another County, and secured in Jail.<BR>JAMES MANNEY.<BR>Beaufort, April 10, 1828--'26 '27.
