Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber on Wednesday evening last, a Negro Boy named BOSTON, about 15 years old, 4 feet 2 or 3 inches high, complexion very black, and has an impediment in his speech when frightened. Also, a negro woman named MARIA, about 18 years old, 5 feet 4 or 5 inches high, smooth skin, and very black. All persons are forwarned from harbouring or employing them under the penalty of the law. The above reward will be given to any one who will apprehend and deliver them to the subscriber, or secure them in the Newbern Jail.    SPRATLEY M. DOYLE.<BR>March 7--31
