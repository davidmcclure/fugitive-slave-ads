Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber on or about the 15th December last, Negro woman SILVIA: she once bleonged to Mr. Henry Luton, and is so well known in Edenton, (where she no doubt may be found,) that a description of her is thought entirely unnecessary. I will pay the above reward to any person who will deliver her to me, or confine her in jail, so that I get her.<BR>DAVID SMALL.<BR>Jan. 39.
