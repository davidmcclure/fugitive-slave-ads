Transcript: 40 DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, the first July last, a Negro boy by the name of PETER, supposed to be 21 or 22 years old. A small scar on one of his cheeks; also on a close examination, his ears is not alike--his height is five feet, nine or ten inches--when weighed last, he weighed from 180 to 190--stout built, inclined to be of a yellow complexion--rather slow spoken--he left me when living on Rocky river. Any person or persons hearing of or confining the said Negro will be handsomely rewarded.--Direct your letter to Wadesborough, N.C.<BR>JOHN WATSON.<BR>June 3, 1829.<BR>t[illegible]
