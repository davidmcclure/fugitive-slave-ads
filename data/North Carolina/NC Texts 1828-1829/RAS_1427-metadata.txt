Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, on Friday the 11th inst, his negro girl MOLLY, stout built--very black--and about five feet, three or four inches high.  Said girl formerly belonged to Wright C. Stanly, Esq.[Esquire]--I forwarn all persons from harboring her under the penalty of the law, in all such cases made and provided.  The above reward and all reasonable expenses will be paid for her delivery to me in Newbern.<BR>JOHN DUGUID.<BR>April 19, 1828--'25tf.
