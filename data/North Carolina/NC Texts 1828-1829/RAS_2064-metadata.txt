Andrew is a very likely fellow about 30 yearsf of age and dark complexion, and is well acquainted in Harvey's Neck where he is known to be lurking. Masters of vessels, and others are forbid harbouring or carrying him off.<BR>JAMES H. HARVEY.<BR>Pembroke, Chowan. CO. Feb 23.
