Transcript: $30 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from Lenoir County, on the 16th of July last, a negro man named NEW, and a negro woman named MILLY.  They are of yellow complexion.  New is about forty years of age, and Milly about thirty-two.  Twenty dollars reward will be given for the apprehension of New, and Ten dollars for Milly; or thirty dollars for the confinement of both, in any jail in the State, so that I get them again.  All persons are forewarned from harbouring or employing said negroes under the penalty of the law.<BR>CHRISTOPHER R. GREEN,<BR>Agent for H.H. NICHOLSON.<BR>Trenton, Oct. 30th, 1828--52 56
