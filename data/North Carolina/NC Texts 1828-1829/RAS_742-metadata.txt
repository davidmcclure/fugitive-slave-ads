Transcript: 30 DOLLARS<BR>REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, on Friday, 11th April, Three Negro Men, viz.--<BR>Tom, Dave, and Jesse.<BR>TOM is 26 years of age 6 feet high, black complexion, small whiskers, and has a slow, but good delivery of speech.<BR>DAVE, or DAVID, is 25 years of age, 6 feet 2 inches high, slim, black complexion; and, in order to cloak his villainy, pretends to be religious; is fond of singing psalms, and, being the greatest rogue, will no doubt take the lead in all conversation.<BR>JESSE is a Virgina Mulattoe, 35 years of age, 5 feet 10 inches; has lost several of his upper teeth; is hard of hearing, and is generally whistling or laughing.<BR>Tom and Dave are brothers, came from Craven County, North Carolina, and it is probable they will attempt to return there; and that Jesse will accompany them.<BR>The above reward and all reasonable expenses will be paid for apprehending the above mentioned slaves and securing them in any Jail, so that I can get them.  I will pay in proportion for either of them.<BR>WM.[WILLIAM] BELL.<BR>Pensacola, 14th April 1828--'28'31
