Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>I will give Ten Dollars reward to any person, who will apprehend and deliver to me, or confine in Jail so that I get them, my negro women CLARISSA and MARIAM, who ranaway about a fortnight past. Mariam is well known in the Town of Edenton, where no doubt they are both lurking. The above reward will be paid for both or $ 5 for either. ELIZ [ELIZABETH] PETTIJOHN.<BR>Chowan Co. March 24.
