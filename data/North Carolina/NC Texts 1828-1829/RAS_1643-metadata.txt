Transcript: Ranaway<BR>FROM the Subscriber, on the 2d instant, a dark mulatto boy about 18 years old, by the name of Calvin Danson.  He is knock-kneed, has large feet, with a slit in his right ear.  He had on when he went away, a black yarn coat, much worn, with white cotton pantaloons.  I suppose he is gone to the neighborhood of Tarboro' where his uncle Enphraim Dunson lives.  He had a writing with him, certified by Ann Brown, wife of Jesse Brown, dec'd[deceased] that he was about 22 years old, which certificate was signed also by Doctor Boddie to any one who will apprehend and confine him in Jail, so that I can get him again.<BR>HARBARD ROBERTSON.<BR>Wake C'ty[county], Oct. 12, 1829.    47 2p
