Slaveholder: Thomas Battle
Transcript: $30 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on Monday night the 5th inst. four Negroes, viz.<BR>ISAAC, aged 24 years, yellow complexion, has a scar on the cheek, having the appearance of being cut by a knife--there is also a white spot on one of his eyes.<BR>PETER, aged 18 years, dark complexion.<BR>BETTY, aged 25 years, dark complexion, and her child HANNAH, a mulatto, 7 or 8 years of age.<BR>These negroes are believed to be lurking either in Jones or Carteret Counties.<BR>I will give the above reward of Thirty Dollars for the Negroes herein named, or ten dollars each for the men, and ten dollars for the woman and child if delivered to me or secured in any jail so that I get them.<BR>THOMAS BATTLE.<BR>Onslow County, July 21st, 1829.
