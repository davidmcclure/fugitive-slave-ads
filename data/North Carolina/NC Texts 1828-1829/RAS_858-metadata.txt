Transcript: FIFTEEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 1st instant, his negro man SIGN;  he is about  thirty-six years old, five feet ten inches high, quite black, and his right hand is crippled, the middle finger stands straight, and the others bend inward. Sign was lately bought of Wm.[William] Gaston, Esq.; and he has relations in Newbern and at Mr. Gaston's Mills. His mother belongs to Capt. Bradley, of Beaufort, where he will probably go. He carried away with him, one suit of domestic cloth, for winter, one coat and trowsers of pale blue cotton cloth, a pretty good drab great coat, a new fur hat, an old wool hat, and an old suit of deep blue negro cloth. I will give the above reward to have said fellow safely lodged in Jail in Newbern. <BR>HENRY BLACK,<BR>Near Newbern<BR>March 7--31
