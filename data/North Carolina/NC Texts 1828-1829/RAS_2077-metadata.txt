Person placing ad: John M. Woodward
Date of departure: 1828-09-01
County of residence: Chowan County (N.C.)
Presumed or intended destination: Tyrell County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Affa
Slaveholder: John M. Woodward
Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber, on the 1st September last, a negro woman AFFA, aged from 40 to 45 years, of small stature; it is believed that she is lurking in Tyrell County, where he has connexions [sic] and from which place she was brought. The above reward will be paid for apprehending and delivering said negro to the subscriber if taken without the County, or Five Dollars, if apprehended within the County. Any person wishing to purchase said woman, that can get her in, may do so on reasonable terms; which will be made known on application to the subscriber or at the Gazette Office.<BR>JOHN M. WOODWARD.<BR>Chowan, Co. Dec. 9.
