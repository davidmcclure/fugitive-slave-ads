Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward<BR>Ran away from the subscriber, living near the Falls of Neuse, Wake county, on the 7th instant, a negro man named JIMBOY, about 34 years old, about 5 feet 10 inches high, stout and strong built; his complexion as black as common; he has long keen whiskers, has a small long scar on the side of his face, near his mouth; he has another on the top of one of his feet; which makes it thicker than the other, and also many other scars about him. He may probably be lurking about Raleigh, as I understand he was in the city on yesterday.<BR>The above reward will be given for the apprehension of said negro so that I get him again.<BR>JAMES ALLEN.<BR>Wake county, June 12, 1828.<BR>24-tf.
