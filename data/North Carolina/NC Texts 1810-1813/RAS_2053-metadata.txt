Transcript: STOP THE RUNAWAY.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber or seduced away some designing person, on or about the 15th of April last, a Negro boy by the name of DAVID, about 16 years old and remarkably well grown of his age, of a yellow complexion with a scar on one of his cheek bones, about the size of a quarter of a dollar, of a darker colour than the other part of his face, and of rather a down or heavy look, and slow spoken. Any person who will apprehend and deliver said Negro to me in Franklin County, or secure him in goal, so give such information so that I get him again, shall be handsomely rewarded for their trouble and all reasonable expences paid.<BR>JAMES YARBOROUGH.<BR>May 6, 1811.
