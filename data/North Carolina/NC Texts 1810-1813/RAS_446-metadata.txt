Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward. <BR> RUN away from the subscriber about 6 weeks past, a negro fellow named MANUEL. He is a tall likely looking fellow, of a yellowish complexion, and considerably knock-kneed. He is about 20 years of age, and stutters very much when spoken to. Any person who will deliver said fellow to me, or confine him in any goal so that I get him again, shall receive the above reward. <BR> WILLIAM SAUNDERS. <BR> Chowan, Nov. 1, 1810.  tf 244
