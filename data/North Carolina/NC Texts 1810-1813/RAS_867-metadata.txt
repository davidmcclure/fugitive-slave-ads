Transcript: FIFTEEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN-AWAY from the Subscriber, about the 23rd of January last, a Negro man by the name of<BR>JIM.<BR>He is about 35 years of age, five feet six or eight inches high, of a dark complexion, large eyes, and very talkative.--It is supposed he is lurking about Newbern, as he has a wife there, at which place he is well knowu[known] by the name of JIM REW.--I will give the above Reward for him if taken in this County, and Twenty Dollars if taken out of it and secured so that I get him again. <BR>All masters of vessels, and others, are forbid employing said Negro, or carrying him away under the penalty of the law.<BR>SOUTHY REW.<BR>Garbacon, Feb. 15, 1812.
