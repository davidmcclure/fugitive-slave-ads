Transcript: 50 Dollars Reward. <BR>RUN-AWAY from the Subscriber, five Negroes--FAIN, LYDIA, ALLEN, PLEASANT and child--Fain and Lydia run-away on the 7th of last September. Fain was formerly the property of Mr. Thomas M'Lin--Lydia is her daughter. Allen, Pleasant & child were formerly the property of Abner Everett, Esq. of Jones County. I have reason to believe that they are all about Newbern. <BR>I will give the above reward for all of them, or in proportion for any part of them being delivered to me, or secured in Newbern Jail;--and double the reward if killed and proved to my satisfaction, and no questions asked concerning them. <BR>WM.[William] H. CONNER. <BR>July, 27th 1812.
