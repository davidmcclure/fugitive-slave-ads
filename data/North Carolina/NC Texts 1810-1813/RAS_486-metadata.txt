Time period (decade): 1810-1819
Person placing ad: Mary Howe
Date of departure: 1812-01-20
County of residence: Bertie County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Mark;Peter
Slaveholder: Mary Howe
Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR> RUNAWAY from the subscriber about the 20th of January last, two Negro Men, by the name of MARK and PETER. <BR> Mark is about 5 feet 9 or 10 inches high, of a yellowish complexion, down look when spoken to, has a small scar over his right eye, thick lips, small slim hands and feet, and about 30 years of age. <BR> PETER is about 5 feet 10 or 11 inches high, dark complexion, very well made, walks remarkably proud, and is about 19 years of age. The above reward  will be given to any person who will apprehend and deliver the above mentioned fellows to the subscriber, living in Bertie County, or secure them in any jail so that I get them again; or 15 Dollars for either of them. All masters of vessels and others are hereby forewarned from harboring, employing, or carrying them off under the penalty of the law. <BR> Mary Howe. <BR> Bertie County, March 9, 1812.  3w 31[illegible]
