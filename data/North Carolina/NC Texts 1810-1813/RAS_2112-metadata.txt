Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber living 6 miles East of Hillsborough, a likely young negro man Slave by the name of WILLIS about 5 feet 6 inches high, about 22 years of age, slender made, walks nimbly and has a small scar on his right jaw. He took away with him a suit of light coloured mixed purple Clothes; he is a tolerable Shoemaker, and can read tolerably. It is likely he will attempt to preach or Extort among Negroes, as he carried away a bible with him, & has been in that habit for some time past. I will give the above reward and pay all reasonable Expences to any person that will deliver him to me if taken out of the limits of Orange County, or for Securing him in Jail as aforesaid; or Ten Dollars if taken in the County of Orange or secured in Hillsborough Jail.<BR>Hillsborough, 20th March 1811.<BR>JOHN DOUGLAS.
