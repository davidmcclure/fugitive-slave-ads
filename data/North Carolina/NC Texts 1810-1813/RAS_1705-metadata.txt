Transcript: RUN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 30th of September last, a negro man named MOSES, between seventeen and twenty years of age, yellow complexion, five feet six or eight inches high, plays on the violin, and will attempt to pass for a free-man. Any person stopping said negro and delivering him to Henry B. Hunter in Williamston, Martin county, or to the subscriber in Tarborough, Edgecomb county, shall receive a reward of Tweny-Five Dollars, or if confined in any jail within this State, Fifteen Dollars. <BR> Henry Hunter. <BR> Tarborough, Oct. 4, 1811.  3w 593
