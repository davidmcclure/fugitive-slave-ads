Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward. <BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber on the 29th of June last, a Negro man by the name of <BR>ABRAM. <BR>Commonly called by the way of distinction, Stevenson's Abram;--lately the property of Gardner Childs.--He is about six feet high, thin visage, dark complexion and large whiskers. It is supposed he is lurking about Newbern or its vicinity, where he is well known. <BR>I will give the above reward to any person who will apprehend said fellow and deliver him to me, or secure him in the jail of Newbern--and Twenty dollars for his head, as he is legally out-lawed. <BR>WILLIAM MOORE. <BR>August 8, 1812.
