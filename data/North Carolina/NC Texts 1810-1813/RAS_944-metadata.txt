Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the Subscriber, some time in the month of March, a negro woman by the name of <BR>LOUISA. <BR>She is about twenty two or three years of age, rather of a yellow complexion ;--she carried with her a girl child about fifteen months old---It is supposed she is lurking about Washington as she formerly belonged to Mr. JOEL DICKENSON of that place, and has a husband at Mr. JAMES REDMOND's of Washington, by the name of Frank belonging to the heirs of THOMAS BLACKLEDGE.--I will give the above reward to any person who will bring said negro woman and child to me, or secure them so that I may get them again. <BR>JOHN HOUSE. <BR>Greenville, Pitt County, May 30, 1812.
