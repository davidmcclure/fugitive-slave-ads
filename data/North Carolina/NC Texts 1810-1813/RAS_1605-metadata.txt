Transcript: RANAWAY,<BR>FROM the Subscriber, living near Mr. Bennehan's, on Flat River, Orange county, on the 4th instant, a NEGRO MAN, named PETER, and a WOMAN called POLLY -- The former about 35 years of age, somewhat yellow--The latter a small woman, about 24 or 25 years of age, of a darker complexion than the Man.<BR>The Man took away a suit of double woven cloth--coat, jacket and pantaloons, a mixed suit striped with red cotton, and a quite new double wove great coat dyed purple.  It is expected that he has obtained a pass.  The Woman had also a number of good clothes not particularly recollected.<BR>Twenty-five Dollars Reward will be given for apprehending and securing the said Runaways--or a proportionate part for either of them.<BR>Temperance Wellborn,<BR>William Horton<BR>Jan. 17, 1811
