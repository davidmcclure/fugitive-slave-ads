Transcript: Ranaway, <BR>FROM the subscriber, living near the falls of Neuse, in Aug. 1810, a negro woman named Alsey, about 35 years of age, large, of dark complexion and full faced. She was purchased of Duncan Lemmon, formerly residing near the falls of Tar river, Nash county, where her father and mother live, and where she is supposed to be now lurking. Ten dollars reward will be given to any one who shall deliver said wench to me. <BR>CASWELL POWEL. <BR>Wake county, Oct 4, 1811.
