Transcript: STOP THe RUNAWAY!<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber in Person County, on Tuesday the 8th inst. a Negro man named Abram, rather of a yellowish complexion, about 35 or 36 years of age, has on only a pair of pantaloons when he absconded; but has to his clothing it is very uncertain what he has on now. The said Negro has lot of his middle toes or parts of them, also the forefinger on his right hand is stiff, and the nail on his finger deformed, occasioned by a felon or rising and his back very much scarred by whipping.<BR>RICHARD DUTY.
