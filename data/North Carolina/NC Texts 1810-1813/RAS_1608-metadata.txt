Transcript: Ranaway,<BR>FROM the subscribers, living near Mr. Bennehan's on Flat river, Orange county, on the 4th instant, a NEGRO MAN named PETER, and WOMAN named POLLY. The former about 35 years of age, somewhat yellow; the latter a small woman about 24 of 25 years of age, of a darker color than the man. The man had a coat, jacket, & pantaloons of double wove cloth in a mixed suit, stripped with red cotton; and a quite new double wove great coat, dyed purple. It is expected that he was obtained by a pass. The woman had likewise a number of good clothes not particularly recollected. Twenty five dollars reward will be given for the apprehnesion and securing of the above runaways, or a proportion part for either of them.<BR>TEMPERANCE WILBORN,<BR>WILLIAM HORTON.
