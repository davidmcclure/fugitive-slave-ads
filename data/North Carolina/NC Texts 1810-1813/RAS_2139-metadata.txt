Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY on the 29th of October last, a Negro man by the name of JOE, between 25 and 30 years of age, he has lost a toe off of the right foot, and the others are very crooked, his fore teeth are open before, has a down look when spoken to, and is about five feet six or eight inches high. I will give 20 Dollars Reward to any person who will deliver him to me, or secure him in any Jail so that I get him again.<BR>THOMAS BURNS.<BR>George's Creek, Chatham County, April 28, 1812. }
