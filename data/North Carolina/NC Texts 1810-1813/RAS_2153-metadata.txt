Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY on the 6th of April last, a boy named Aaron, who is in the habit of calling his name Frederick; he is seventeen years of age, about five feet high, has large eyes with a bald look, and rather inclined to be bow-leged, he will probably change his name and endeavour to pass for a free boy. I will give the above reward of $10 to any person that will secure him in any Jail so that I get him again, and if delivered at my house in Warren will pay any reasonable expences.<BR>MICAJAH T. HAWKINS.<BR>Warren County, N.C. June 6th 1812.
