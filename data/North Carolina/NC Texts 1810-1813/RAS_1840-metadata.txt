He has a free wife who was called Revel, and she has in possession a certificate of her freedom, signed by Jospeh Moore.--Caesar is well known about WIlmington, N.C. was the property of the late William F. Strudwick, dec. <BR> JAMES WEBB. <BR> Hillsboro N.C. Jan 20, 1813.
