Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward.<BR> WILL be given to any person who will apprehend and deliver in any gaol, negro man JERRY, who lately ran away, belonging to Thomas Benbury, orphan of Richard Benbury, dec. He is about 5 feet 10 or 11 inches high, of a yellowish complexion, thin visage, 27 or eight years of age, has been branded with letters on each cheek, but being entirely defaced by scratching and nearly covered with hair, are not legible. He is a fidler, had on when he went away a pair of half boots, light coloured pantaloons, and blue coat. He was hired this year by Ebenezer Paine, Esq.[Esquire] of Edenton. It is expected he will endeavor to get to the Northward. <BR> All masters of vessels and others are strictly forbid from carrying the above fellow out of the State, or harboring him under the penalty of the law. <BR> JOS.[Joseph] C. BENBURY. Guardian.
