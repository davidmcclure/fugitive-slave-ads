Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward. <BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on White-Oak, Jones County, on the 12th of December last, a Negro Fellow by the name TOM, about 25 years of age, not very dark, about 5 feet 8 or 10 Inches high, has a scar on one of his cheeks just below his eye; formerly belonging to the estate of Joseph Sanderson, deceased, and was hired last year by Mr. William Mitchell of Newbern, and it is supposed said fellow is lurking about that Town or in its vicinity, for he has a wife there.--I will give the above reward to any one who will secure him in the Jail of Newbern, or deliver him to me at my Plantation. <BR>All persons are forwarned from harbouring, employing, or carrying him away, under the penalty of the Law. <BR>JOHN SIMMONS. <BR>Jones County, Jan. 1st, 1813.
