Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber some time in the month of January last, a Negro man named <BR>CATO. <BR>He is about 5 feet, 8 or 10 inches high, of a black complexion, round shouldered, and the lower part of his right ear cropped off.--It is supposed he is lurking about Newbern, or it's vicinity. I will give the above reward to any person or persons, who will apprehend said fellow and deliver him to me, or lodge him in the jail at Newbern. <BR>Masters of vessels, and all others, are forwarned from harbouring, employing or carrying him away, under the penalty of the Law. <BR>WILLIAM S. SPARROW. <BR>Dawsons Creek, February, 29th, 1812.
