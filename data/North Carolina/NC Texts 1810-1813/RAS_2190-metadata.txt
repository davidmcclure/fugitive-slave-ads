Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, on the 3d day of Oct. last, a negro Woman named MOLLY, about 37 years old, very yellow complexion, long thin visage; carried off one store habit, blue striped, and sundry homespun clothing. She is a slender made negro, round shoulders and very sensible, 5 feet or seven inches high. I will give twenty Dollars to any person who will secure her in any Jail within the State so that I get her, or fifty Dollars if out of the State.<BR>Lewis Ticer.<BR>Chatham County, near Brandy's ferry, Deep River, 12th March, 1811}
