Transcript: RUNAWAY,<BR>FROM the Subscriber, on the 6th of July, a Negro Man, by the name of BRUNSWICK, about six feet high, well made, of a dark complexion, and about 35 years of age.  He is rather slow of speech.  He took  with him a black Homespun suit : the rest of his cloaths are not recollected.  He had on a white hat turned up with green.<BR>Twenty-five Dollars Reward will be given to any person who will deliver him to the subscriber ; or for information of his being harbored by any one, or taken out of the State.<BR>WM. BROWN.<BR>Warren County, N.C. Sept. 20, 1812.
