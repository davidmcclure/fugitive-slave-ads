Slave name(s): Rose
Slaveholder: Curtis Snelling
Transcript: NOTICE.<BR>RANAWAY, or Stolen, from the Subscriber, on the 24th inst. a Negro Woman by the name of ROSE, seventeen years of age, has had one child, is low and dark, and has three round scar on her right arm above the elbow, the largest one is next to the elbow, occasioned by a burn. When she went off she carried no other clothing but a stripped homsepun habit, stripped with blue and cooperas. I will give ten dollars to any person who will secure her in any jail so that I get her again, provided she has runaway, and if stolen I will give twenty-five dollars for the apprehension of the theif.<BR>CURTIS SNELLING
