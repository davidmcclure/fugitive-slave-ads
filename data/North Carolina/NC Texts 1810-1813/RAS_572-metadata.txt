Slave name(s): Ben
Slaveholder: Thomas Trotter
Former slaveholder: Elizabeth Bonner
Transcript: TWENTY-FIVE DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RUN AWAY from the Subscriber, on the 6th of July last, my Negro man <BR>BEN, <BR>Formerly the property of Elizabeth Bonner, dec. He is about 26 years of age, about 6 feet high, stout made, and a very coarse voice. He had on a jacket and trowsers of twilled cotton cloth. I have heard of him being at Swifts-Creek towards Newbern, some time ago. The above reward will be given to any person delivering said negro to me, or 20 dollars for lodging him in any goal so that I may get him again. <BR>All masters of vessels, and others, are forbid employing said negro, or carrying him out of the State, under the penalty of the law. <BR>THOMAS TROTTER. <BR>Near Washington, Beaufort County, OCt. 24, 1811.
