Transcript: $50 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, on or about the 14th inst. a light mulatto negro man named ROBBIN, about 24 years of age, about 5 feet 4 or 5 inches high, stout and well made, had on a light mixed round jacket, with white cotton pantaloons, and black fur hat. It is believed that he has a forged free pass, with which he will no doubt attempt to pass for a free man, and make his way to a free State. I will give the above reward for his apprehension out of the State, or $25 in the State, so that I get him again.<BR>LEWIS COGDELL.<BR>Near Waynesborough, Wayne County,}<BR>N. C. April 24, 1835.}<BR>34-4w.
