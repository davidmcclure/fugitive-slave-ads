Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, about the 15th ult. a negro woman named<BR>HANNAH;<BR>formerly the property of Col. Edward Williams, of Onslow County, but more recently owned by Richard Saunders, Esq. Hannah is above the ordinary size, black complexion--about Forty years of age.--She has relations in Onslow county and at Rocky Point.<BR>She has, for some years past, been living with a free colored man named Isaac Scott, who has left this place, and she might have gone off with him.<BR>I will give the above reward for her apprehension, upon delivery to me at Wilmington, or confinement in any jail, so that I may recover her.<BR>ANN GUTHRIE.<BR>Wilmington, Sept. 11th, 1835.<BR>36-3t
