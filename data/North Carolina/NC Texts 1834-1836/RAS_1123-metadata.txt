Transcript: FORTY DOLLARS REWARD.  <BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 5th of May last, a Negro Woman named CARER, formerly the property of of George A. Thompson. It is likely she is in Carteret county, White Oak, or the neighborhood of Swansborough, in Onslow county. I will give the above reward for her confinement in any jail within this State, or for her delivery to me at my residence, Richlands, Onslow county.  <BR>JOHN COOK. <BR>February 8th, 1836.
