Transcript: Stop the Runaway.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber in July last a negro woman named Macy. She is about 40 years old--dark complected--deficient in her front teeth--of common stature--slow spoken--quite an intelligent countenance--had on when she eloped a dark calico dress.<BR>In all probability said negro is aiming for Southampton county, Va. as she was purchased in that county 19 months since and bartered to me. She said that she belonged to, Dr. Goodin of the above named county, when first purchased. Any person apprehending said negro and commiting[sic] her to jail, and giving me due notice of the same, or delivering her to me, shall be liberally rewarded<BR> NICHOLAS MICHAEL.<BR>Lexington, August 1836.
