Transcript: RAN AWAY <BR>FROM the subscriber, his Negro Man HECTOR, a stout, able bodied fellow, about 20 years of age. He will probably endeavor to reach Newbern, where he has relations, and may attempt to effect his escape by water. A generous reward will be given for this apprehension and confinement in jail, so that I get him. <BR>EDWARD B. DUDLEY. <BR>Onslow County, Sep.[September] 8th, 1835.
