Transcript: $25 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, a few weeks ago, a negro man named WASHINGTON. Said Washington is about 5 feet 6 or 8 inches high, about 25 years of age, dark complected, and is thought to be in the neighborhood of the Harris Mine. He was purchased from William A. Harris. The above reward will be given if delivered to me, or secured in any jail, so that I can get him.<BR>GREEN HUIE<BR>Sept. 7, 1836.       10tf
