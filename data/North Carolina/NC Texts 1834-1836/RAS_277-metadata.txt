Date of advertisement: 1835-08-08
Page: 3
Time period (decade): 1830-1839

County of residence: Rowan County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Gabriel
Slaveholder: G. & C. Partee
Former slaveholder: John Carson
Transcript: $25 REWARD.<BR> The Subscribers will give the above reward for the apprehension and delivery to them, or for his confinement in any Jail within the State, a Negro man named Gabriel, about 35 years of age, 6 feet high, very black and teeth very white. It is supposed the above runaway is in some of the neighborhoods in which he has been hired, then belonging to John Carson, his clothing not recollected. He took with him, for amusement, a first rate fiddle, and plays it tolerable well. He has been out about  2 months.<BR> G. & C. PARTEE.<BR> Rowan County, July 25, 1835.--p--4'--
