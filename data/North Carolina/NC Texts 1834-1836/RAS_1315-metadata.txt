Transcript: Ten Dollars.<BR>WILL be given for apprehending and securing a fellow named<BR>DICK,<BR>who ran away in September last, and has been frequently seen in Wilmington. He is rather under ordinary height, carries his head erect, full face, black smooth skin, turns out his toes when walking; about 20 years of age, plausible in conversation--had on grey satinet Jacket and Trowsers[sic]. I apprehend he ranges in the vicinity of this Town.<BR>SAMUEL BLACK.<BR>Nov. 12.<BR>97-tf.
