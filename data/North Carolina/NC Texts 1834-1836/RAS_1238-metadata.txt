Transcript: FIVE DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY, from the subscriber, on the 5th instant, a negro girl named NANCE. She is about 16 years of age--4 feet 7 or 8 inches high, light black complexion--and has a scar on her right cheek, resembling the letter R. Had on when she went away a woollen coat, striped with black, white, red and gray. Masters of vessels and all other persons are forewarned from harbouring, employing or carrying her away, under the penalty of the law. The above reward will be paid to any person who will deliver her to the subscriber, or confine her in Mr. Gildersleeve's jail.<BR>LEM'L[Lemuel] C. BRINSON.<BR>Craven County, 15th Nov. 1834. 325 tf.
