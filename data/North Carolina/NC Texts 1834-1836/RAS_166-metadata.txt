Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on Thursday the 18th inst. a Negro man by the name of JO: HARRIS, or Jo. White. Said fellow is about thirty two years old, about five feet ten or eleven inches high of a dark copper color, almost black, thin face and high cheek bones, and rather stooping in the shoulders. He has a variety of clothing with him, and amongst his clothes are jacket and pantaloons of country homespun; also an old drab Surtout overcoat, considerably worn and soiled. I will give the above reward for his delivery to me, or to the jailer in this place.<BR>N.B. Should Jo not come in in the course of ten days, I will then outlaw him.<BR>ISAAC NORTHROP.<BR>September 25.<BR>[illegible]-3t
