Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>BROKE Jail, on the 13th inst. a negro man named Luke, committed to the Jail of Johnston county on the 18th January last, as a runaway. Said Negro reported himself to belong to a man by the name of Gee of Halifax county, N.C. Luke is between 20 and 30 years of age, very black; of athletic form, about 5 feet 10 or 11 inches high, very low forehead, and covered with thick hair.  I will pay the above sum for the apprehension of said negro, so that I get him again.<BR>A.S. BALLENGER, Sheriff.<BR>March 14, 1835    73
