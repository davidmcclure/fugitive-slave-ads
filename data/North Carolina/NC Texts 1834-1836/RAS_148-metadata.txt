Transcript: $100 Reward!<BR>STOLEN OR RUNAWAY, from the subscriber, on the 28th August, a negro man named JACOB. Said negro is about 6 feet high, and about 22 years old.  I have no doubt he has made for Georgia, as he stated that he had been persuaded to go there, and intended doing so.<BR>I will pay one hundred dollars for the delivery of said boy to me, at Sandy Grove, Chatham county, N.C. or for his confinement in any Jail, so that I get him again.<BR>ALSON GRAY.<BR>Sandy Grove, Chatham co., Oct. 1, 1836--6t.<BR>The Augusta, Ga. Sentinel will publish the above advertisement 3 times, and foreward the account to the subscriber at Sandy Grove, Chatham co., N.C., for payment.    A.H.
