Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on the 4th of September, a negro man named TONY. He is of black complexion, strong built, five feet six or eight inches high, about 28 or 29 years of age. The said negro has a wife at Mr. James Simpson's, and may be lurking in the neighborhood of Moore's Creek or Wilmington. The above reward will be given for his apprehension and delivery to me, or confinement in the jail at Wilmington.<BR>JAMES LEWIS.<BR>September 14th, 1835.<BR>[illegible]
