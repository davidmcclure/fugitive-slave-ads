Headline: $30 reward
Publishing newspaper: Washington, N.C. : The Whig
Date of advertisement: 1835-08-01
Time period (decade): 1830-1839
Person placing ad: John McWilliams
Slave name(s): Moses
Transcript: $30 REWARD. <BR> RANAWAY from the subscriber, on the 19th inst. negro man MOSES, aged about thirty years, five feet 6 or 7 inches high--stout built, large feet, short white teeth--has on his breast several whelks [welts], which grew there, nearly the size of a child's little finger.<BR> The above reward will be paid if said fellow is apprehended without the limits of Beaufort county, and $20 is taken within said limits and secured in the jail of this town.<BR> Masters of vessels, and all others, are cautioned against harboring, employing or carrying him away, under the severest penalty of the law.<BR>JOHN MCWILLIAMS.<BR>Washington, July 25th, 1835.
