Transcript: Stop the Runaway.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, living near Liberty Hill, in Iredell county, N.C., a negro man named<BR>PETER,<BR>formerly owned by James Cunningham. He is between forty and fifty years old, of a yellowish complexion--round face and small eyes. He is marked with a scar in one of his ears, which has not grown together; also with a scar on the underside of his heel, which has not filled up; he has also a small scar on one of his cheeks, and is about five feet, five or six inches in height.--Any one taking up this negro and lodging him in jail or delivering him to me, shall be reasonably compensated.<BR>HUGH CUNNINGHAM.<BR>Liberty Hill. Iredell co. N. C.}<BR>June 11th, 1836--t147
