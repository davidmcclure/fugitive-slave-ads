Transcript: TWENTY-FIVE DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RANWAY from the Plantation of the subscriber, in Onslow County, his Negro Woman LUCY and YORK, her son. <BR>LUCY is of a small stature; rather spare made, has a small scar on her face, a down look and is about 27 years of age. <BR>YORK, is about 13 years of age, appears sullen when spoken to, and is doubtless in company with his mother. They are supposed to be lurking about the plantation of General Dudley, in Onslow, or they may have gone to the plantation of Hosea Murray, in New Hanover County. <BR>The above reward will be paid for their apprehension and safe confinement in jail or for their delivery to me. <BR>All persons are forbid harboring or concealing said Negroes under the penalty of the law. <BR>LEWIS M. WILLIAMS.  <BR>Richlands, Onslow county, Sept. 7th, 1835.
