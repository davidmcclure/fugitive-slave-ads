Transcript: $15 REWARD.<BR>I WILL give the above reward for the delivery to me, near Rockingham, Richmond County, N.C my boy JACK, or ten dollars, if lodged in any Jail so that I get him. He absconded on the 11th inst., is a dark Mulatto, about 21 years of age, common size, and has a scar a little above one corner of his mouth; had on when he left me a Homespun roundabout, and purple cotton Pantaloons. He may attempt to pass as a free negro.<BR>STEPHEN COLE.<BR>April 13, 1836.<BR>85 4w
