Transcript: Fifty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away, on the 5th inst. two likely Negro Men, Needham and Summer: the former is about the age of 35, with a small bald place on the back of his head, he has an aqueline nose, with a scar on his right eye, or a dark swarthy color, talks rather slow, of middle stature, about 5 feet 11 inches in height, and has a very slow motion, one of his legs is apparently shorter than the other: the latter is about the age of 46, of a sallow complexion, low and chunky, has only one eye, very pleasant in conversation, two upper teeth out, his height is about 5 feet 6, and has a very thick upper lip. The above reward will be promptly forwarded to any one who will apprehend them.<BR>E. MITCHELL.<BR>University of N.C., March 7, 1835     8t21
