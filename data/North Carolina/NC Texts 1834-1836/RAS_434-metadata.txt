Transcript: 15 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber some time in June last, a Negro girl named CAROLINE; she is 15 years of age, about five feet high, has a light black complection[sic], and talks very sensibly. She is so well known in Newbern, that a further description is deemed unnecessary. It is understood that she has been lurking in the suburbs of the town for some time. The above reward will be given for her apprehension and delivery to the subscriber, or for her confinement in any jail.<BR>JOSEPH F. ANTHONY.<BR>25th Dec. 1835. 82tf
