Transcript: Ran Away,<BR>FROM the Subscriber, on Sunday the 24th day of January, 1836, a Negro Man named HARRY, about 40 years of age, who has a wife at J'n[John] Walker's plantation. He was dressed in Homespun, mixed blue and copperas, somewhat worn--had on a tarpaulia hat, the tar off the top, leaving the straw bare. He has lately been cutting wood at Nathaniel Potter's plantation, in Brunswick county, and may be in that neighborhood. I will give TEN DOLLARS reward for his delivery to me or in Jail, and all reasonable expenses paid.<BR>EDWARD NEWTON.<BR>Jan. 29<BR>Federal Point.<BR>[illegible]--tf.
