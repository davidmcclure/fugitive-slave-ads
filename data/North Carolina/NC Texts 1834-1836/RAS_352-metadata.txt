Transcript: $30 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, from Point Petre, about the 15th inst. his negro boy<BR>JIM FRINK,<BR>about 28 years old; short stature; thick set; black complexion; speaks slow and plain.<BR>Also, about the 23d, from the same place,<BR>NED and GILBERT.<BR>NED is a small fellow; yellow complexion; about 25 years old; very intelligent; and speaks slow and plain. GILBERT is about 19 years old, small for his age; very black complexion; short and well set, and is also, very intelligent; speaks slow and distinct. The above reward will be paid for their delivery to me, or at Wilmington, or Smithville Jail, or $10 for either of them.<BR>SAMUEL POTTER.<BR>July 29.<BR>[illegible]
