He is well known about Long Creek and South Washington. All persons are forewarned from harboring said negro, trading with him or transacting business with him in any shape whatever. I will give FIVE DOLLARS reward for his delivery to me, if caught out of the neighborhood of Long Creek.<BR>JAMES GARRISON<BR>Long Creek, Nov. 6.<BR>44-4t.
