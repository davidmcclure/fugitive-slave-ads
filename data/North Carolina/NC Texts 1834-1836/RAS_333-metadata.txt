Transcript: $25 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY, in the early part of last month, from the neighborhood of Elizabeth, Bladen county, a negro woman named<BR>SALLY SNEED;<BR>belonging to Mr. Prudhomme. She is about 25 years of age; dark mulatto; had on when she went away, a yellow cotton dress. She was purchased of Mr. JAMES NIXON, and lived formerly at the Sound. I will give the above reward, if the said Negro Woman is delivered to me in Wilmington; or $10 to any person who will secure her in any jail, so that I can get her.<BR>RICHARD BRADLEY.<BR>Dec. 11.<BR>49--tf.
