Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, living in the county of Mecklenburg, a negro man named ELLICK, about five feet six or eight inches high, 30 years old, black complected, and has rather a down look when spoken to, and speaks rather broad and slow for a negro; some of his front teeth are out. He had on a light mixed coat, and copperas pantaloons, and white wool hat,--he has other good clothing with him. The above reward of $20 will be paid if caught without the county of Mecklenburg, or $10 if taken within the county.<BR>MARGARET A. HARRIS<BR>Nov. 24, 1835.     60-tf
