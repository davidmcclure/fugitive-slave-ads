Transcript: RUN AWAY,<BR>FROM the subscriber, on the 11th of July, my negro man named<BR>DAVE.<BR>He is about six feet high; between 35 and 40 years of age; light complected, for a negro; one of his hands has been burn, leaving a scar, and his little finger is bent out; one of his upper fore teeth is out; tolerably quick spoken, with a somewhat lively look.  I will give Twenty Dollars for his confinement in any Jail, and Twenty Five for his delivery to me at my house, near Rolesville, Wake County.<BR>He may have a free pass, and attempt to make his way to Tennessee.<BR>WILEY MITCHELL.<BR>Wake co. July 21, 1836.    90--tf 3 p.
