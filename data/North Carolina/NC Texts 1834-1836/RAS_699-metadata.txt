Transcript: Twenty FiVe DOllars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, living ten miles south of Raleigh, on the 12th Nov'r[November] last, a negro man by the name of ISAAC, about 26 years of age, dark skin, 5 feet 9 or 10 inches in height; he is well proportioned, white eyes, and speaks remarkably low, and wore large whiskers. He is probably lurking about the vicinity of Raleigh, where he has a wife.  I will give the above reward of twenty-five dollars for his delivery to me, or secured in jail in the city of Raleigh, so that I get him again.<BR>JONATHAN UTLEY.<BR>February 2, 1835    14
