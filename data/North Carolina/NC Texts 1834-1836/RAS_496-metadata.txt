Transcript: 25 DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, in March, 1834, his negro woman FAME, of about the common size and complexion. She is supposed to be in or about Newbern. Her sister Sophia, belongs to Mr. Ann Ellis, in Newbern. Her aunt Ginney, old Dick's wife, belongs to John C. Stanly, and lives on his farm a few miles from Newbern, up Neuse road. Col. Washington's man Phill, who lives at his Piney Grove plantation, is said to be her husband. The above reward will be given to any person delivering her to me or securing her in jail so that I get her again.<BR>SAM'L[Samuel] SIMPSON.<BR>Fort Barnwell, Aug. 25, 1835. 566 pt.
