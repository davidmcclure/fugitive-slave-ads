Transcript: $40 REWARD.<BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber, in the county of Bladen, during the Christmas Holliday's[sic] two Negro men, named<BR>JOE & ADAM.<BR>Joe is a low and stout built fellow, has a small scar on his forehead, one knee crooked, and is about 27 years of age. Adam is somewhat taller than Joe, well made, and has a scar on the left side of his face, near the eye and temple and is seventeen years of age. All persons are forbid harboring, concealing or employing said Negroes, as I am determined to prosecure any person who may do either. I will give the above reward for the delivery of said Negroes to me, at my house in Bladen county, and an additional reward of $10 for procuring testimony which will convict any person of harbouring said Negroes.<BR>JEREMIAH NORMAN.<BR>Bladen Co., N.C., Feb. 1, 1836. 51t3
