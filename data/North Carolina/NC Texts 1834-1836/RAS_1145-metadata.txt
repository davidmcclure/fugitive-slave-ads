Transcript: FORTY DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber's Plantation on Bay River, on the 11th of Sept. last, a Negro Man named SIMON, about 28 years of age, 5 feet 6 or 7 inches high, of dark complexion, and has an impediment in his speech, occasioned by the loss of some of his front teeth. He has connexions on the plantations of George Wilson and Thos[Thomas] J. Pasteur, Esqrs.--at one of which places he is probably lurking. The above reward will be given for his apprehension and confinement in jail, or for such information as will enable me to recover him. <BR>DAVID D. FRATER. <BR>Bay River, Craven County, Oct. 5, 1835.
