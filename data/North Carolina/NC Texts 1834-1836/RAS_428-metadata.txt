Transcript: 15 Dollars Reward!<BR>WILL be paid for the apprehension and delivery to me, or lodged in any Jail, of my negro Man<BR>ANDREW.<BR>He is about 22 years old; a light complected negro. He is well known in the neighborhood of the sound, and is probably lurking in the neighborhood of Mr. McClammy's, where he has a mother and brother. All persons are forwarned from harboring said negro, under the severest penalty of the law.<BR>W. S. WILLKINGS.<BR>If ANDREW should come in within a month he will not be punished, otherwise he will be outlawed.<BR>W. S. W.<BR>Feb'y 25.<BR>8-tf.
