Transcript: Twenty-five Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from my young man, Pleasant Bullard, on the first day of November, 1835, in Cabarrus county, North Carolina, 17 miles from Salisbury, on the Road from Salisbury, to Charlotte, a Negro man by the name of GRIFFIN, aged about 28 or 30 years old, and about five feet 8 or 9 inches high, a little stooped shouldered, of a black complexion.  Said man, was purchased by Reubin R. Hawkins, of a Mr. Parker, of Essex county Virginia, for me.  I will pay twenty-five dollars for the apprehension and delivery of said man to me, or Reubin R. Hawkins, or if lodged in any jail, in the State of North Carolina, or Virginia, so that I get him again.<BR>JOHN W. LINDSEY<BR>1 St.
