Transcript: $25 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, on the 26th ult a negro man named ARTHUR; he is of a light complexion, has very large white eyes. a very high forehead, rather inclined to be bald, knock-knee'd, has a down look when spoken to, and speaks very slowly and long; about 38 years old, and about 6 feet high; had on when he left, a suit of cotton fustians, and a wool hat, all considerably worn, which he may have changed, as he has sundry other clothes. I will give $10 for his apprehension, so that I get him, if taken in the State, or $25 if taken out of the State.<BR>CURTIS C. OATES<BR>Duplin County, N. C. July 1, 1835.<BR>44-tf.
