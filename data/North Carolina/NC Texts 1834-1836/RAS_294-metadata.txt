Transcript: $25 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, about the 1st of June last, his negro man named DICK, who is 45 or 50 years of age, about six feet high, straight and well proportioned. He was raised in Jones County, and formerly belonged to Joseph King, of Newbern, where it is probable he is lurking. He was seen last Monday, at the Forest Place, on the north side of Neuse river. All persons are forewarned from harbouring, employing, or carrying him away, under the penal of the law. The above reward will be paid for apprehending and delivering him to the subscriber, or for securing him in jail.<BR>JOSEPH HARTLEY.<BR>24th July, 1835 360 vt.
