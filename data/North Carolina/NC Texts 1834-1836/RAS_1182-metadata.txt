Transcript: Notice.<BR>RANAWAY from Nathaniel L. Mitchell, a Negro man named LEWIS, about twenty one or two years age, a little yellow complected, an ordinary size, and supposed to be lurking about Duplin and Sampson. Said negro he got by marriage. One hundred dollars will be given for said negro, to be lodged in any Jail in the State so that said Mitchell gets him.<BR>NATHANIEL L. MITCHELL,<BR>By EDWARD W MONTFORT.<BR>Onslow County, Sept 25th, 1835.<BR>38-tf.
