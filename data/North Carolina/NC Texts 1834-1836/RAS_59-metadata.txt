Transcript: Ran Away,<BR>FROM the Subscriber on Saturday the 19th & Sunday the 20th March, two negro boys,<BR>ANTHONY & PETER;<BR>ANTHONY is about 19 years old, talks quick and is plausible, quite black and likely; he is the same fellow that attempted to go off in the Schr. Butler, capt. Carter. PETER is about 18 or 19, a bright mulatto, is tall, well grown and likely--Masters of vessels are warned not to harbor said negroes or take them away. The usual reward will be paid for their delivery to me or on their being secured in Jail--or $100 if concealed on board any vessel.<BR>SAMUEL POTTER.<BR>March 25<BR>12-tf.
