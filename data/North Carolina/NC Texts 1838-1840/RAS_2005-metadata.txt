Transcript: $10 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, some time in the month of July last, two negro girls, HALY and AMY TYLER, they are nearly black, and the former is quite slim, speaks very fast, and stammers when questioned particularly. Amy is smaller than Haly. They are supposed to be lurking about George Garnes's, in Craven county, near Newbern. The above reward will be paid for the apprehension of both the girls, provided they be secured so that I get them.<BR>JAMES DAWSON.<BR>Mosely Hall, Bear creek,}<BR>13th Aug. 1838,}<BR>519tf
