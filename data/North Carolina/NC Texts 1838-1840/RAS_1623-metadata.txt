Transcript: RANAWAY<BR>FROM the subscriber on 30th of December last, a Negro man named<BR>HARRY,<BR>Of a black complexion, about 5 feet 4 or 5 inches high, of a good countenance. Has a scar on his right cheek about the size of a 12 1/2 cent piece or larger. His under lip is somewhat long, and he shows his teeth a good deal when he laughs. He had on when he left me a pair of Linsey pantaloons and Linsey roundabout, all new; a hair cap and pair of stitch down shoes. It is believed that said negro is about the town of Salisbury or on the plantations near the Yadkin River.<BR>I will give $20 for the delivery of said negro to me or the Jailor of Rowan county.<BR>WILLIAM COWAN, G.S.<BR>February 23--tf30--s[?]2
