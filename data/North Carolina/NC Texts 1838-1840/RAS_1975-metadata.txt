Transcript: $25 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber some time in the month of March last a negro woman named MARIA, about thirty years of age, of dark complexion, and about five feet six inches high; she is slow of speech when spoken to, was purchased of the estate of Jonathan. P. Daves, decd, by Thomas Lee, and is probably lurking in or about Newbern, among her relatives, or on the plantation formerly owned by Jno. [John] P. Daves, dec., The above reward will be given for the apprehension and delivery of said negro to me, or for securing her in any jail so that I get her.<BR>BENJAMIN HUGGINS.<BR>Oct. [October] 18th, 1839.
