Transcript: $30 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, on the 1st day of April, 1835, a negro girl named EDY, now about 30 years of age, dark complected, ordinary size, good countenance.<BR>Also, on the 26th February, 1839, a negro man named STEPHEN, aged about 26 or 27 years, about 6 feet high, dark complection[sic], weighs about 175 lbs., has no beard or whiskers.<BR>He is a brother of the above named woman, and it is probable that they are now together, and lurking about this neighborhood, or the adjoining part of Richmond county, say about Gilchrist's Mills, where they have relations.<BR>The above reward will be paid for their apprehension and delivery to me, or being secured in any Jail so that I get them again, or $15 for either. Any information addressed to the Subscriber at Solemn Grover P. O., N C. will be attended to.<BR>DANIEL SMITH.<BR>Qu[e]whiffle District, Cumberland C'ty,}<BR>March 14, 1839.}<BR>37 3wpd.
