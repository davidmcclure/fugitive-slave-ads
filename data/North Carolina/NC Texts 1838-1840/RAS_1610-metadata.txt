Transcript: RANAWAY,<BR>ON the 22d of March last, a negro man named DICK; aged about 28, about 5 feet 8 inches high, very black, down look, has had one of his legs and one of his arms broken, and limps. There is no doubt that he is lurking about Fayetteville, where he has worked for four or five years, having hired his own time. He belongs to the estate of ---- Smith, of Robeson County, and is hired to the Subscriber till 1st January next. All persons are forewarned from harboring or employing him, under penalty of the law.<BR>WM.[William] A. NELSON.<BR>Robeson County, May 1.<BR>91-2w-pd
