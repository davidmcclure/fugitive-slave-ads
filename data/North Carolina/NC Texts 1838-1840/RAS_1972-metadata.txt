Transcript: $100 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 20th of August last, a negro man named WASHINGTON,--he is about 28 years of age, 5 feet 6 inches high, yellowish complexion, stout built, has long bushy hair and prominent eyes, is very intelligent, and when spoken to answers promptly. He formerly belogned to Jno. [John] A Averet, Esq., of Richlands, Onslow county, has relatives in Newbern and Raleigh, and is well acquainted in Kinston and Trenton-it is therefore probable that he is lurking in one of those places. The above reward will be given for his delivery to the subscriber or for securing him in jail, if he be apprehended in Newbern, or fifty dollars if taken in any other part of the state. JASON. E. S. DUFFY.<BR>Swansboro'  N.C. 8th Jan. 1839.
