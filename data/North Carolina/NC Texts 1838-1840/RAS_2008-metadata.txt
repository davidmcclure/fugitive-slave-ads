Transcript: $50 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, about twelve months ago, a negro man named ISAAC. He is very black, active and intelligent; about five feet 6 or 7 inches high, and about thirty years of age. He is supposed to be lurking between Newbern and Waynesboro', on the river, as he has been for several years employed by Lovick Fore of Wayne, in boating. Isaac has lost some of his front teeth, whether the upper or lower is not recollected. The above reward will be paid for lodging him in jail, so that I get him.<BR>JAMES C. BRYAN.<BR>Trenton, Jones County,} 10th Oct., 1839.
