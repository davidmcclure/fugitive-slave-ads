Transcript: $30 REWARD<BR>WILL be paid for the apprehension, and delivery to me of my negro man HENRY, who ran away from this place on the 5th instant. Henry is about twenty years of age, of black complexion, five feet teen or eleven inches in height, and slender. He has an impediment in speech, or rather a habit of stammering, and his front teeth are of a dark colour. Henry was purchased from D. Y. Shine, Esq., of Newbern, and may be lurking about that town. The same reward will be paid for lodging him in any jail of the State, so that I get him.<BR>WILLIAM GLOVER.<BR>Elizabeth City, N.C., 14th Aug.--519tf.
