Transcript: $10 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber in the month of March last, a Negro girl named CATHARINE, belonging to the Estate of Jas.[James] Baldwin. She is supposed to be lurking about Fayetteville or in Bladen, where she formerly lived in or about Elizabeth. The above reward will be given for her apprehension and delivery to me in Fayetteville.<BR>H. M. JOHNSON.<BR>June 8, 1839.<BR>49-3w-pd.
