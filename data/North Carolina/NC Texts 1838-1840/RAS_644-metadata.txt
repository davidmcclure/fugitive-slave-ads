Said slave was purchased of Wm.[William] Peal, of Wayne county, by Geo.[George] Wright. The above reward will be paid to any person that will deliver to him or me, or $10 if lodged in any safe jail so that I get him.<BR>SABRY WRIGHT.<BR>Near Laurel Hill, Richmond county, N.C.,}<BR>May 21, 1839.}<BR>47tf
