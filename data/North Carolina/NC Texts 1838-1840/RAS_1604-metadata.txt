Transcript: RANAWAY,<BR>From the Subscriber residing in Person county, N. Carolina, near Cameron's Mills, on the 2nd day of Oct. inst., a negro man by the name of SAM. Sam is of a light black, tolerable large, stout built; he has a double tusk on each side of his mouth, also a large lump or scar on the back of his neck, about 23 or 24 years old, had on when he left dark store Jeans, a scar on one of his feet occasioned by the cut of an axe. I will give 50 dollars if apprehended in the State, and 100 dollars if out of the State so I get him.<BR>E. H. CARRINGTON.<BR>October 3, 1838<BR>42 4w<BR>The Richmond Enquirer is requested to publish the above 4 times and forward account to Red Mountain, Orange, N.C.<BR>E. H. C.
