Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber, his negro man ROBIN, who absconded about the 28th of last month, without any cause whatever.-- Robin is a bright mulatto, nearly or quite six feet high--a stout muscular man, about 42 years old-- has one of his front teeth missing--has a scar on one of his cheek bones, occasioned by a burn, and the mark of a severe cut on one of his thumbs.  He is fond of spirits, and will occasionally get drunk.  Robin has worked in a Tan Yard for 20 years, and is a very good Tanner and Currier.  He is also a good coarse Shoemaker, and carried off with him his Shoemaker's tools.  He had a free woman for his wife, and may probably have procured free papers from some free person of colour.<BR>I will give the above reward for the apprehension and confinement in Jail, of said negro, so that I get him.  Any person apprehending him, will please address me, at Oxford, N.C.<BR>THO.[THOMAS] B. LITTLEJOHN.<BR>Oxford, 26th September, 1838     48 tf
