Transcript: Runaway or conveyed off,<BR>FROM the Subscriber's Residence, near Henderson, on the 16th inst a negro girl called MARTHA, belonging to the Subscriber.  Said girl is of a dark brown complexion, slightly made, and very free spoken, about 21 years of age; she wore a black silk bonnet with feathers; and had in her possession two calico bed quiltings.  I understand she will attempt to pass as a free girl.<BR>A reward of Ten Dollars will be paid either for the apprehension of the person persuading her off, or for securing the girl in any place so that I can get her again.<BR>RIGDON VALENTINE.<BR>Granville County, August 28, 1839.     34--tf
