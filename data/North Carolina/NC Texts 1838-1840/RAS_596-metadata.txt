Transcript: Twenty-five Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber, living in the county of Wake, near the falls of Neuse about the 24th of January, my negro man JIM, fifty years old, though he carries his age very well, about 5 1/2 feet high, quite intelligent, rather light complexion, thick upper lip, has a bold independent air in his walk, and carried with him a plenty of good clothing.<BR>I am inclined to think he will attempt to pass for a free man, and will endeavor to get to a State where he may be free.<BR>I will give the above reward of Twenty-five Dollars to any person who will apprehend said runaway, and bring him to me, or secure him in any Jail, so that I can get him.<BR>JOHN P. COOK<BR>Wake County, Feb. 9th, 1839    16
