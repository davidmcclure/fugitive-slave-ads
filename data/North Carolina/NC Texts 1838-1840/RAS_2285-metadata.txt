Transcript: $20 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber sometime in March last, a Negro Fellow, by the name of STEPPENY. Steppeny is about twenty one or twenty two years old, about five feet high, of light complexion, and of ordinary size. It is likely that the said fellow may be lurking about Wilmington, or the Railroad. The above reward will be given for the apprehension and delivery to me of the said Steppeny, or to the jailer in Wilmington.<BR>NATHANIEL GALLOWAY.<BR>May 22d 1839.<BR>175 3w
