Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, on the 18th inst., a negro man by the name of TEMAR, about 40 years old. He is large and well built, about six feet high, full round face, with a large scar on his neck, and is a Fiddler. I expect he is aiming for Tennessee or Georgia. I will give the above reward to any person that will lodge him in Jail so that I may get him again.<BR>JAMES BENNETT.<BR>Sampson County, March 20, 1840.<BR>90-3w<BR>N.B.--It is supposed he is in company with, or lurking after, a man by the name of Wilson, who is moving to Tennessee, with two Carts, and Women and Children, black and white.
