Transcript: $20 REWARD<BR>FOR the apprehension and delivery to me of my boy JACK LORD, sometimes called JACK HOWE. Said boy is a dark mulatto, about five feet eight inches high, has a sailor like appearance, and a plausible address. I am inclinded to believe he will attempt to make his escape to the North or Charleston, South Carolina, where he has a wife. All masters of vessels are cautioned against harboring or concealing him, or in any way aiding him in his escape, as the law will be strictly enforced.<BR>JAMES T. MILLER<BR>Wilmington. May 24th, 1838.<BR>123 tf
