Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber, on the 7th of April, a mulatto boy by the name of ISAAC, about 16 or 17 years of age, 5 feet 8 or 10 inches high, spare made, a scar over one of his eyes, shows his front teeth very plain, and had his hair very long and well combed, and he probably may attempt to pass for a free man. I will give the above reward for him, delivered to me or confined in any Jail, so that I get him, or five dollars if taken in this county and delivered to me.<BR>WILLIAM BOSTICK.<BR>Richmond county, N.C., April 16, 1840. 94-tf
