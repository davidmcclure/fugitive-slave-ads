Transcript: $50 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 11th of January last, two negroes--ELISHA and JOHN ROBBIN. Elisha is a black, stout, well built fellow, about 30 years of age; 5 feet 8 inches high; has a pleasant countenance when spoken to; no particular marks recollected; said negro has a set of good teeth.<BR>John Robbin is about 26 years of age; 5 feet 4 inches high; yellow complexion; round face; he is an intelligent, pleasant negro when spoken to. No farther description of said negroes is deemed necessary, as they are well known in Pitt and Beaufort counties.<BR>A reward of 50 dollars will be given for both negroes if confined any where within the limits of the State so that I get them; or, 25 dollars for each.<BR>All persons are forewarned not to harbour, employ or carry said negroes away under the penalty of the law.<BR>WM. [WILLIAM] BRINKLEY<BR>Feb. 19. 1839--32
