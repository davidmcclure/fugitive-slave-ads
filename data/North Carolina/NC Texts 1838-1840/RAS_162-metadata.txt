Transcript: $100 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY on the 1st of October, 1839 two negro men, one belonging to each of the undersigned, named<BR>SANDY and ALFRED.<BR>SANDY is a black fellow about 6 feet high, 22 years of age, the right knee considerably knock-kneed, has a fore tooth out, down look, rather spare for one of his height. Among his clothes were a straw hat and brown homespun coat.<BR>ALFRED is a bright mulatto, 18 years of age, 6 feet high, fine looking fellow, very straight, smiles when spoken to, and is apt to put his hand to his right ear when spoken to.<BR>They were raised by the subscribers, and it is feared that they have been kidnapped or assisted away.<BR>The above reward, or $50 for either, will be paid for their delivery, or for their confinement in any Jail so that we may get them again, or a reasonable reward for any information that may lead to their recovery. A reward of $100 will be paid for proof sufficient to convict of their being harbored or assisted away.<BR>ARCH'D[Archibald] McEACHERN,<BR>JOHN GILCHRIST.<BR>Randalsville P. O., Robeson county, N. C.,}<BR>November 27, 1839.}<BR>74tf
