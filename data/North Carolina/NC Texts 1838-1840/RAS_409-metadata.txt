Transcript: $50 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, on the night of the 27th of December, a negro man named CARLISLE, dark complected and rather slow spoken, six feet in height, and very stout built. It is supposed that he is lurking in the neighborhood of D. McEachern's Bridge, in this County, or in the vicinity of Marion Court House, S.C., or near Ford's, in the edge of Marion District, at all of which places he has relations. For his apprehension and safe delivery to me, I will give the above reward, or $25 if lodged in Jail so that I get him.<BR>43tf<BR>JENNET McNAIR.<BR>Cowper Hill, Robeson County, April 28.
