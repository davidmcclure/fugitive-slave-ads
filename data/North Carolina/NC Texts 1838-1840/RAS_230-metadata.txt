Transcript: $20 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, living in Mecklenburg county, 7 miles East of Charlotte, a Negro man named NATHAN, about 24 years of age, stout built, about 5 feet 10 or 11 Inches high, rather of a Copper cast. I suspect he is lurking about the Rev. Isaac Grier's in Providence, or Mr. Hugh Harris' in York District, S. C., or Mr. Robt.[Robert] Grier's, at Mr. Small's Store, or the Rev. John G. Witherspoon's in Iredell county. The above reward will be given for the apprehension and delivery of said boy. When he left he had his blanket and all his clothing with him.--I purchased said boy from the Rev. John G. Witherspoon, Iredell county.<BR>WILLIAMSON WALLACE.<BR>Feb. 24, 1840.<BR>22tf
