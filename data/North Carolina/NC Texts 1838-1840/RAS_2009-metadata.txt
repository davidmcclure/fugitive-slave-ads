Transcript: RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, on the 7th inst. a negro woman named HAGAR.<BR>She is from thirty five to forty years of age, of a slim figure, in delicate health, and has had on one or both of her thumbs what is called a bone film. She has lived for a number of years in the family of Charles Stewart, and having a free coloured man, a sailor, for a husband, it is supposed that she  has gone off with a view of getting to New York. All masters of vessels are hereby cautioned not to carry her away, under the penalty of the law.<BR>The above reward will be paid to any person who will deliver the above named woman to me or secure her in jail so that I get her.<BR>JOHN L. DURAND.<BR>June 13th, 1840.
