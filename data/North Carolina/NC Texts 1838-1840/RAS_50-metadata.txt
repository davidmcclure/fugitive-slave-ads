Transcript: $10 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber a boy between twenty-six and twenty-seven years of age, named JIM, of a yellowish complexion; he is about six feet or the rise in height, has large feet, and a large scar above the ancle[sic] joint, occasioned by the cut of an axe; he has a down look, big white eyes, and a Roman nose, and weights about two hundred pounds. I will give the above reward of ten dollars to any person who will deliver said boy to me, or secure him in jail.<BR>JOHN GERINGER.<BR>Nov. 19th, 1838.
