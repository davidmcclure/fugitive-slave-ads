Transcript: Runaway<BR>Negro JOHN belonging to Gov.[Governor] Dudley is missing--is a likely, full face, black and smooth skin fellow, about 30 years of age. He formerly belonged to Mr. Fillyaw--may pass by that name or by the name of John Brown.--He deserted from some post on the Mail Road early in August--has a mother and sister in or about South Washington, where he may be lurking.<BR>A reward of Ten Dollars will be given for his delivery in Jail at Wilmington, to W. Montgomery the Govr's[Governor's] overseer, at Summer Hill, or to the Subscribers.<BR>R. W. BROWN & SON.<BR>October 5, 1838.<BR>143-9w[?].
