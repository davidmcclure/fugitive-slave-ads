Transcript: Twenty-five Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, two negro fellows-Joel and Moses. Joel is 24 or 25 years of age; complexion very black; height nearly 6 feet.<BR>Moses is about the same age as Joel; yellow complexion; somewhat corpulent; height about 5 feet 5 inches. He has a wife at Col. Tayloe's, near Washington. Joel and Moses are well known in and near that town.<BR>Masters of vessels, and other persons, are hereby forewarned not to harbour, employ, or carry said negroes away, under the severest penalty of the law.<BR>JOHN S. KENNEDY.<BR>Jan. 29, 1839-29
