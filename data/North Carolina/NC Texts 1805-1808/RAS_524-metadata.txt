Transcript: 20 DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber on Friday Evening last, a Negro Man named<BR>FRANK,<BR>About 30 years of age, about 6 feet high and well made, has a scar under the left eye, occasioned by the tooth ache, and two of his under foreteeth are out; had on when he went away a dark coloured drab coat and homespun overalls; and took with him a variety of other clothes. He professes methodism and sometimes exhorts.<BR>I suspect the aforesaid Negro is lurking about Wilmington and will attempt to make his escape in some vesse; all masters of vessels and others are therefore cautioned against harbouring, employing or carrying him away. <BR>The above reward will be given for apprehending and securing said Negro in jail or bringing him to me in Brunswick county.<BR>JAMES FLOWERS.<BR>March 24.
