Transcript: RUN AWAY<BR>FROM the Subscriber, on the 14th day of May, a NEGRO MAN named Abraham, he is about 5 Feet eight or nine Inches high, twenty seven years of Age, well made, he has a Scar under one of his eyes his hair is high on his forehead and may attempt to pass for a free man, as he can tell a good tale.  He was formerly the property of Mr. Charles Harrington, of Anson county.  I will give Twenty Five Dollars Reward and all reasonable expences paid for the above Negro, if delivered to me in Mecklinburg county, N.C.<BR>NATHAN ORR.<BR>August 18th, 1807.
