Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward. <BR>RAN away from the subscriber about the first of April Last, a negro man named BALAAM, about forty years of age, nearly six feet high, very black, remarkably flat footed, and the whites of his eyes tinged with yellow.--The above reward shall be paid to whoever will deliver him to Major James Sheppard, at Stag Park, or Mr. Jonathan Williams on the North East, or lodge him in jail, so that I get him again. He has a wife at Mr. Wm. Davis's it is supposed he will be harboured about that neighborhood. <BR>Wm. F. Strudwick. <BR>June 8th, 1806.
