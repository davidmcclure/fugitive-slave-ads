Transcript: RAN-AWAY<BR>From the Subscriber, living in Franklin county, about the first of May,<BR>A negro man named Tony,<BR>About 25  years old, about 5 feet 9 inches high, dark complected. He was raised by Mr. Spivy near Windsor, where he is expected to be now lurking, or in the neighbourhood of Mr. Boyce, in Granville, where he has a wife. A reward of twenty dollars will be given for the fellow, delivered to me or secured in jail that I get him again.<BR>D.W. DAVIS.<BR>July 22.
