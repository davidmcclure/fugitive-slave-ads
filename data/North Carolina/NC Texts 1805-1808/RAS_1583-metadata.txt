Transcript: RANAWAY <BR> FROM the Subscriber, living on Neuse river, Wake County, five miles below the Fish Dam Ford, a negro man named Pompey, about five feet eleven inches high, twenty-two years old, very black, eyes inclining to be red, stout made. He was raised by John Jones, deceased on Crabtree. It is expected that said fellow is in the neighbourhood of Raleigh, or James C. Jones's in Franklin. Ten dollars will be given for said fellow, either delivered to the subscriber or secured in jail so that I get him again.<BR>WILLIE SHAW.<BR>June 17, 1805.
