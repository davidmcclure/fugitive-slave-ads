Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber, about the 30th of August, a negro fellow, about six feet high, yellow complexion, one or two of his fore teeth is out, and a piece of one of his ears has been bit off, named Sawney; he can read a little, and I apprehend passes for a free man.  Whoever will apprehend and deliver the said negro, or confine him so that I get him, shall receive the above reward, and all reasonable charges paid.<BR>James Sherrod.<BR>Franklin county, April 27th, 1805.
