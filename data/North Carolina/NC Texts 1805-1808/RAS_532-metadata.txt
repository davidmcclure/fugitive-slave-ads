Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber about three weeks ago, a negro man named Jack, yellow complected, about five feet ten inches high, has a large scar between his eyes, which appears to have been occasioned by the kick of a horse, and is about twenty seven years of age--He is well known in Onslow County, in some part of which I suppose he is now lurking, having heard of his being in the neighbourhood of Swansborough.  I will give above Reward for taking up and confining in jail the aforesaid Negro, and in addition thereto will pay all reasonable expences for having him delivered to me on Topsail Sound, thirteen miles, from Wilmington.<BR>Masters of vessls and all other persons are hereby forewarned from carrying away, employing, concealng or harboring him under the severest penalities of the Law.<BR>EDWARD ST. GEORGE.<BR>March 25. 1806.--tf
