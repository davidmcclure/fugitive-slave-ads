Slave name(s): Jack
Slaveholder: John Bryan

Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY<BR>From the Subscriber, on the night of the 24th Instant,<BR>A NEGRO MAN named JACK, between twenty-five and thirty Years of Age : of a dark complexion, about five Feet ten or eleven Inches high, has very red Eyes, and his upper right Eye-tooth out.  He took away with him a quantity of Clothing, viz. 2 Cloth Coats, one a fine blue the other a coarse grey, 2 striped Silk Jackets and other Clothing not recollected.<BR>Any Person delivering said Negro to me in Chatham county, Deep River, or secure him in any Jail so that I get him again, shall receive the above Reward.<BR>JOHN BRYAN.<BR>N.B. It is expected the above mentioned Negro will try to get to William Hill's, Hancock County, Georgia, who formerly owned him.<BR>J.B.<BR>Deep River, Chatham<BR>County. March 29, 1806}
