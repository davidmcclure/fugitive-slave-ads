Transcript: RAN AWAY<BR>From the Subscriber in Johnston County, on the 22d inst.<BR>A Negro Man named Adam,<BR>HE is about twenty-two Years old, five Feet 8 or 10 inches high, stout made, and when spoke to, answers uncommonly bold.  It is probable he will look for shelter in the neighborhood of Raleigh, at Mr. Gabriel Homes's in Sampson County.  I will give a generous Reward to any Person who will secure him with Irons in any Jail convenient to where he may be taken, so that I may get him.<BR>Osborn Hunter.<BR>July 28, 1805
