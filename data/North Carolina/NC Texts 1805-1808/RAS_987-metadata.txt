Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN-AWAY from the Subscriber, in the town of Fayetteville, about the 1st of January last, a  likely Negro man by the name of CRISPIN, about 35 years old, six feet high, very black, speaks good English, and has a scar over the left eye.  it is supposed that he may be in the neighbourhood of Gen'l Eaton's, near Roanoke, as he was seen there in February last, and has not since been known to be in or about Fayetteville.  The above reward will be given to any person who will lodge the fellow in the jail of Fayetteville, or forty to any person who will secure him in any other jail in this state.<BR>Andrew Flinn<BR>August 27th, 1806
