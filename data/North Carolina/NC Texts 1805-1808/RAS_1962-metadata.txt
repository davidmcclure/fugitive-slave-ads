Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away on Monday, March 17th, from the subscriber, in Bertie county, in the state of North Carolina, a Negro man named<BR>MONMOUTH,<BR>about 28 or 30 years of age, about 5 feet 9 or 10 inches high, stout made, of a very yellow complexion, has an impediment in his speech, and when spoken to by any white person, cannot look them in the face, nor give them a ready answer--has a large head, and very thick bushy hair--has a large navel--is very fond of liquor--one of his legs has been cut in the instep with an axe, and is considerably larger than the other, and one of his ears has a small piece bitten off.  He had on when he went away, a tolerable good raccoon hat, a white Negro cotton jacket and trowsers, and a homespun shirt.  He has been frequently driving hogs to several parts of Virginia.  I will give the above reward if taken and secured in any gaol, so that I thereby get him again, or ten dollars and all reasonable charges paid, if delivered to me at home.<BR>William Pugh, sen.<BR>Bertie county, North-Carolina,<BR>March 26th, 1806.
