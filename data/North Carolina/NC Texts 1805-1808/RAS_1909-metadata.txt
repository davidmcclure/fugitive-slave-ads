Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber living in Northampton county, a Negro man named JUBA, about six feet high, yellow complected.  He left me in the month of January last.  It is [presumable?] he is lurking about the town of Halifax or Mush Island.  Any person that will deliver me the said Negro, or secure him in any gaol in this state, so that I get him again, shall recevie the above reward, and all necessary expences paid.<BR>ROBERT FOX.<BR>34th April, 1806.
