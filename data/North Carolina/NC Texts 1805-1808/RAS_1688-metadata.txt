Date of departure: 1806-09-22
County of residence: Richmond County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Harry
Slaveholder: George Hicks
Transcript: RUN AWAY,<BR>From the Subscriber, on the 22d instant, from his Plantation on Cotiledge Creek, in the county of Richmond, to wit:<BR>TWO NEGROES--one Fellow by the name of Harry, about 35 or 45 years of age, five feet eight or nine inches high, of a yellow complection; and a Woman very small in stature, about 20 years old--she is a wife of Harry.  There is no Hair on the back part of her head, occasioned by a Burn when she was small.  She carried off with her a small sucking Boy Child, about 18 months old.<BR>If any person will secure the above described Negroes in any Jail in this State so that I get them again, they shall be entitled to a Reward of Fifty Dollars, and be paid all necessary expences.<BR>GEO.[George] HICKS<BR>Sep 25.
