Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber in Person county, on the 5th instant, a Negro Man named TOM, 21 years old, dark complected, about 5 feet 8 inches high. He was seen in Raleigh on Sunday the 6th, and since at Mr. Thos [Thomas] Busbee's, nine miles south-east from Raleigh, where he is probably now lurking. He had on a homespun coat and pantaloons and blue jacket. The above reward will be given any one who will deliver him to the jailor of Wake.<BR> JOHN PHILLIPS.<BR>March 12, 1808.
