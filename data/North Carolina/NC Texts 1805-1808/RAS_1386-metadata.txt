Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber, on South River Bladen County, a Negro man named TOM, a Shoe-maker by trade, very black, rather small, slim made, has a very upright carriage, quick walk, and small short foot--had on when he went off a pair of Shoe-boots. He was seen about three weeks ago in a small boat, going to Wilmingotn, where I suspect he has got on board some vessel. Masters of vessels and others are cautioned against harbouring, employing or carrying him away.<BR>The above Reward will be paid for lodging him in jail so that I get him, and in addition thereto all reasonable expences for bringing him to me. <BR>Wm. H. Beatty.<BR>October 21.--tf.
