Transcript: -Five Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber, on the 28th of April, a negro man by the name of ISHAM, he is about 22 years old, and speak svery broken English, he also understands and speaks the French language pretty well.  I expect he will aim for Norfolk, as he was purchased there about twelve years ago.  The said negro belonged to William Williams of Franklin.  All persons are cautioned from concealing or carrying him off.  The above reward will be paid to any person securing him so that I get him again.<BR>Joshua Cole.<BR>Halifax county, May 29th, 1805
