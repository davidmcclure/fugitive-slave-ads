Date of departure: 1807-04-01
Slave name(s): Ephraim
Slaveholder: W. Keddie
Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 1st. April last, a negro man named<BR>EPHRAIM,<BR>about five feet four inches high, stout made and stutters somewhat in his speech; branded on his breast with the letter K.  Had on when he went away a blue sailors jacket and homespun trowsers[sic]--supposed to be harboured up the N.E. or N.W. Rivers. Whoever will deliver the said Ephraim to the subscriber or in the Wilmington jail, shall receive the above reward and all reasonable expences by<BR>W.KEDDIE.<BR>June 2.
