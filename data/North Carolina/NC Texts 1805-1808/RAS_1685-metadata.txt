Transcript: RUN AWAY,<BR>From the Subscriber in Robeson county, on the 1st of April last,<BR>A NEGRO MAN, named SAM, 23 years old, about 5 feet 8 inches high, of a yellow complection, goes immediately when he walks, in consequence of one leg being shorter than the other, and his foot on that side turns out--was raised in Pasquotank county, but brought away at five years of age.  Whoever will take up said Negro and confine him Jail, on giving me information thereof, shall receive a Reward of Twenty-five Dollars ; or if delivered to me, Fifty Dollars.<BR>Theophilus Griffin.<BR>Nov. 15.
