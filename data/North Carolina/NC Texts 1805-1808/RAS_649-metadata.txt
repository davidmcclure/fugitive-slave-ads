Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN away from the Subscriber, on Monday the 9th instant, a likely Virginia-born NEGRO FELLOW, by the name of WALKER, aged about 21 years, of dark complexion, a long head, and fully as lusty as common Fellows.<BR>The above Reward will be given to any Person who will deliver the said Negro to the Subscriber, living nine miles south of Pittsborough, or secure him in any Jail in this State, so that I get him again, and ten dollars more to prove an oath, that the said Negro is harboured by any person.-- I do expect the said Negro is in the neighbourhood of Raleigh, from where he will endeavour to make his escape to Orange County, in Virginia.<BR>T. FARISH.<BR>Chatham County, Feb. 18.
