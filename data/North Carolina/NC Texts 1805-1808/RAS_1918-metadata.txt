Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber on the 18th ult a negro Fellow named Jim, about 26 years old, of a yellowish complexion, five Feet 6 or 7 Inches high, both of his Ears cropt, and has long bush Hair for a Negro.  he took with him a variety of Clothes too tedious to describe.  The said Fellow is an active, sensible Negro.  Any Person who shall deliver him to the Subscriber, on Deep River, Chatham County, N C shall receive the above Reward or Eight Dollars for lodging him in any Goal, and giving notice to MURDO MACQUEEN.<BR>Chatham County, March 1st.
