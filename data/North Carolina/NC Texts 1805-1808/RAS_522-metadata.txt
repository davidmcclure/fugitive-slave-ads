Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber living in Bladen county, on the 20th April last, a negro fellow called <BR>QUASH,<BR>about twenty years of age, possesses a pleasing countenance, nearly six feet high, large over the shoulders, small legs, he has a scare[sic] on his left side which extends half round his belly, his father is hired to Mr. William Williams, near the green banks, Brunswick county, Col. James Richardson owns his mother--it is possible he may be about Fayetteville, as he is acquainted with some of Maj. James Owens' negroes that are hired at that place--he is Outlawed. All resonable expences will be paid to any person apprehending the said negro in addition to the above reward.<BR>J. BRADLEY.<BR>July 14.
