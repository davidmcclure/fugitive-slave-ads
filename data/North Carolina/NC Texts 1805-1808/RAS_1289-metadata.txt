Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber living in Orange County, near Haw River, a Negro Man named Cooper, he is a tall, lusty Fellow, 22 years old, he formerly belonged to a Mr. Thomas Troman, of Gates County, where I expect he will aim to go.  Any Person apprehending said Negro and will secure him so that I get him or will deliver him to me, shall receive the above Reward, and all reasonable Experiences paid.<BR>JAMES WHITSELL,<BR>Jan 8th, 1806
