Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN Away from the Subscriber on the 28th of last Month, a Negro fellow by the Name of Jonas, who is supposed to have been carried off by a white woman, and will likely pass as her Property.  The Negro is about 25 Years old, very large and black.  He had on when he left home, a blue mixt Homespun Coat and red Plush Waistcoat.  He is a Fiddler, and took a Fiddle with him.  The woman has a Scar on her right Cheek.  The above Reward will be given for both, or the Fellow if delivered to me or confined in any Goal in the State, so that I get them again.<BR>ROBERT DANIEL.<BR>Greene County, N.C. May 14.
