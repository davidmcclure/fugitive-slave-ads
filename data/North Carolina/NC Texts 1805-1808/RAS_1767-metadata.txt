Transcript: RUNAWAY<BR>From the Subscriber, within 4 miles of Fish Dam Ford, in Wake County, on the 12th inst.<BR>A NEGRO FELLOW about 20 Years old, named Brunswick, 5 Feet 8 or 9 Inches high, stoutly made and active--very black : his Teeth wide apart.<BR>He went off thinly cloathed, having nothing on but a shirt, Waistcoat, and an old Pair of Woollen Breeches.  He carried with him a Fiddle, and it is supposed he would aim for Shocco Creek in Warren County.<BR>Whoever shall lodge the said Negro in Gaol, or deliver him to the Subscriber, shall be handsomely rewarded.<BR>JAMES BYRD.<BR>May 23.
