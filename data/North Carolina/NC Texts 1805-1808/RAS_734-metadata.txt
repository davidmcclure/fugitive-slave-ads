County of residence: Chowan County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Joshua
Slaveholder: Stephen Cabarrus
Transcript: 30 Dollars Reward. <BR> Runaway from the subscriber on Monday last, the 25th inst. a negro man, named Joshua,<BR>6 feet 2 or 3 inches high. He is about thirty years of age, black complexion and well made. He has very remarkable large feet, and a scar mark over his left eye. He is a cooper by trade, plays tolerably well on the fiddle, of which he is very fond. He had on when he went away white negro cotton jacket and trowsers[sic] almost new, but suppose he will shift them occasionally. I will give the above reward to any person that will deliver him to me, or secure him so that I get him. All persons and masters of vessels are forwarned from employing, harbouring, or carrying the said fellow at their peril.<BR>Stephen Cabarrus.<BR>Chowan county, Jan. 26, 1808.  3t 10t
