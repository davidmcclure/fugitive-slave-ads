Transcript: RUNAWAY from the subscriber, living in Franklin county, on the 12th of September last, a negro man named LEWIS, about 27 years of age, five ten inches high, rather yellow complexion. He carried off with him a mixed jacket and an old blue cloth coat. Whoever will apprehend the said negro, and deliver him to me, or confine him in jail so that I get him again shall be liberally rewarded.<BR>TIMOTHY THARP<BR>October 1, 1816.  41  3w
