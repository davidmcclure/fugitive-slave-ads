Transcript: Twenty-five dollars reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber on the 3d February last, a negro man by the name of GREEN, about twenty-three years old, five feet nine or ten inches high, stout made, dark complexion, had a down look, speaks softly; had on when he went away homespun clothes, no other marks recollected. Green was raised in Lenoir County by a Mr. Miles Huchins, where he has large connections. I will give the above reward to any person that will deliver said fellow to me or lodge him in any jail in this state so that I get  him, & all reasonable expenses paid.<BR>BEN TISON.<BR>Pitt County, near Greensville.
