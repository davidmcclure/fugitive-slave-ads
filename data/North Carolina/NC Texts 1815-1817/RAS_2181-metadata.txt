Slave name(s): Edmund
Slaveholder: Samuel Crawford
Former slaveholder: Bartholomew Medley
Transcript: 10 DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber on the 25th March last, my Negro man EDMUND, aged about 23 years, about 5 feet 3 or 10 inches high, dark complected, has a scar on one of his arms between the wrist and elbow, occasioned by a burn; had on when he went away, black kinsey pantaloons and hunting shirt, and black thick cloth waistcoat. I purchased him in October 1813, of Bartholomew Medley of Mecklenburg, Virginia, to which place I apprehend he will endeavor to get. Any person apprehending said fellow and confining him in any jail so that I get him, shall be entitled to the above reward, or if delivered to me living in the Hawfields, Orange County, N.C. all reasonable expences paid.<BR>SAMUEL CRAWFORD.<BR>April 12, 1815.
