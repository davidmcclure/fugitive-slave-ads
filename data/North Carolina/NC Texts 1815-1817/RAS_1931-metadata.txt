Presumed or intended destination: Town Creek (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Jack Noble
Slaveholder: Estate of Abraham Golden
Former slaveholder: Abraham Golden
Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, a few days since, a Negro fellow known by the name of JACK NOBLE, belonging to the estate of Abraham Golden, deceased. He is about 45 or 50 years of age, stout and well built, quite black, and speaks very plain--he is a great villain, and has one ear cut off. He has a wife at the plantation of Mr. Richard Green, and is well acquainted at Mrs. Simpson's, on Town Creek, where, no doubt, he is harbored. The above reward will be paid on the delivery of said fellow to the subscriber, or to the Jailor in Wilmington.<BR>SAMUEL POTTER.<BR>June 10           1t
