Transcript: Notice.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 16th inst. two Negroes, a man and woman. The name of the Negro man is JACK, aged about 25 years, 6 feet 3 or 4 inches high, stout made, of a yellow complexion, his upper lip very thick--he wore away a short homespun coat and dark broad cloth pantaloons. The wench is named Delia, 16 years old. 4 feet 10 inches high, of a yellow complexion, large breasts, and left a child eight months old-she wore homespun clothes, when she left me. A liberal reward, and all reasonable charges will be paid for their delivery to me, or for their confinement so that I get them again.<BR>DANIEL CHISHOLM, Sen.<BR>Montgomery county, April 12, 1816.  16-4t.
