Transcript: Twenty-Five Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, on the 2d instant, a Negro man named Paris, about 20 years old, very black complexion, 5 feet 10 or 11 inches high, proportionably built, quite intelligent, stammers when he speaks. Had on a blue round coat and pantaloons, and a new wool hat, though he will no doubt alter his name and clothing. He was brought from the eastern shore of Maryland, and will endeavor to get back to that place--Any person apprehending said negro and lodging him in jail and giving me information at Rockingham C.H.N.C. so that I get him, shall be entitled to the above reward and all reasonable expences paid.<BR>JOHN MARTIN.<BR>Rockingham C.H.N.C. March 5.<BR>12-3w pd
