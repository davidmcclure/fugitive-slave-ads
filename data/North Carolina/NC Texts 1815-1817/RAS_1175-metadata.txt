Transcript: NOTICE.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber on the 19th instant, a Negro Man by the name of AARON, about 20 years of age, 5 feet 10 or 11 inches high, well made, but rather round shouldered, & has a down look ; had on when he went off, overalls of white cotton homespun and shirt of the same, and an old furred hat.  The said negro was purchased not long since of a Dr. Edwards of Northampton, where I expect he will aim to go.  Any person who will apprehend said negro and give me information, shall be rewarded, and their expences paid.<BR>MOSES FULLER<BR>Person county, N.C. Sept. 22.  36 3w
