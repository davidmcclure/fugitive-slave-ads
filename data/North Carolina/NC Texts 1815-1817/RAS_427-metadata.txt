Transcript: 15 DOLLARS REWARD <BR>RAN-AWAY, <BR>FROM the Subscriber's Plantation, four miles above Newbern, on the 11th inst. my negro man, <BR>ANDREW, <BR>about 19 or 20 years of age, light complexion, slender made, 5 feet 5 inches high, his fore teeth very wide apart, had a scar on his right foot occasioned by the cut of an Axe. He had on when he went away a homespun shirt and trowsers, copperas strip'd round jacket--Any person who will apprehend said fellow; shall receive the above reward, and all reasonable expences paid if securing in Jail so that I get him. <BR>All persons are forbid harboring, employing, or carying him away under the penalty of the law. <BR>Edmund Hatch. <BR>Craven County, Nov. 25th, 1816.
