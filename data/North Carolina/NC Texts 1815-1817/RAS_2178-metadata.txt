Time period (decade): 1810-1819
Person placing ad: Thomas Thompson
Date of departure: 1815-02-18
County of residence: Orange County (N.C.)
Presumed or intended destination: Wake County (N.C.);Wilmington (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Britain
Slaveholder: Thomas Thompson
Transcript: FIVE DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANWAY from the subscriber, (living near Thomson's Mills, in Orange) on the 18th Feb. a Negro man named BRITAIN, about 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, and about 40 years of age, had on when he went away tow overalls and waistcoat, and mix coat. I expect he will try to pass for a free man and go to Wake or Wilmington, he ran away once before and was caught in Wake. Whosoever will take him up and bring him to me, or put him in any jail so that I get him again, shall have the above reward and all reasonable expences paid.<BR>THOMAS THOMPSON.<BR>Feb. 25.
