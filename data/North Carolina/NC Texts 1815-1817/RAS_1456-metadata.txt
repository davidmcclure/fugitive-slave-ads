Date of advertisement: 1816-10-29

Transcript: 100 Dollars Reward,<BR>WILL be given for the apprehending and securing in any jail so that I get him, or delivering to me, a Negro Man, the property of Elizabeth Moore, of Perquimans, by the name of<BR>JACOB,<BR>about 31 or 32 years of age, stout, well made, rather inclined to a light complexion. He formerly belonged to the estate of Francis Newby, dec'd[deceased] from him to Joseph Sutton, Esq.[Esquire] of Perquimans, and from him to Mr. Soloman Elliott, of Chowan; he is supposed to be in the neighborhood of some one of his former owners. The above reward will be paid for said fellow if taken within this state and secured or delivered as above; or 150 Dollars, if taken out of this State.<BR>Henry Gilliam.<BR>Gates Court-House, 25 Aug. 1816.  tf 326
