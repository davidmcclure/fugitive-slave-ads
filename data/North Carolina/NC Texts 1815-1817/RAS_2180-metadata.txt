Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS REWARD<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, on the 19th inst. a Negro Man, called JACOB, about 30 years of age, slender and keen made, 5 feet 9 or 10 inches high, his colour is a yellow brown, and is very march marked with the smallpox. When he went away, he wore a coat and pantaloons of mixed homespun, likewise had pantaloons of yellow Nankeen and Checks, and had a red Pocket-Book and some money; he is by trade a boot, shoe, harness & segar makes--likewise a barber. It is said he has a free pass, and it is on proof, to conviction, of his being harbored by any white man, or free man of colour, or twenty dollars if secured in jail, and information given me at Wadesborough, N.C. Masters of vessels are cautioned against said fellow.<BR>INGOE B. CASH.<BR>March 30, 1815.  15 4t
