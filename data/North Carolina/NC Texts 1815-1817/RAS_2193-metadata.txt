Transcript: Notice.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber living near Ramsay's Mills, about the 21st. day of December last his negro man JO, about about 27 years of rather low stature, and tolerably stout made of dark colour, of a pleasent countenace, part of his fore-teeth out, when he stands or walks is quite erect in his body. He is supposed to be lurking either in Chatman or Moore, or perhaps in Franklin County, as his father lives in the latter of County, all of whom formerly belonged to Benjamin Rush, dec. A reward of 25 dollars will be given to any person who will apprehend him and confine said negro JO in any Jail in this State, so that I can get him, and reasonable expences will be paid to anyone who will deliver him to me.<BR>S. STOKES<BR>Chatham Co. N.C. April 1, 1817.  15-5w
