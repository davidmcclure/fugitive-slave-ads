Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber a few days ago a negro fellow by the name of SPARROW, and a girl named DELIA, late the property of JNO.[John] M. Gabie, deceased. They are both well known in this place and are no doublt lurking about the Town or in its neighborhood. The above reward will be given for the apprehension and delivery to me of both the said Runaways, or Twenty-five Dollars for either of them.<BR>James S. Green.<BR>Wilmington, February 15.
