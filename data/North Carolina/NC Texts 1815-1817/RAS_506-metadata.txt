Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on the 5th inst. a negro fellow by the name of <BR>JACK BATTLE,<BR>Four feet, eight inches high; dark complexion; has one of his fore teeth out; and is a little knock-kneed; he took with him a variety of clothing, a description of his dress therefore, cannot accurately be given. The above reward will be given to any person who will deliver him to me, in Craven county, near Newbern, or secure him in any jail, so that I get him again. <BR>All persons are hereby forewarned from harboring, employing, or carrying away said fellow, under the penalty of the law. <BR>WILLIAM P. MOORE.<BR>Nov. 10        tf 30
