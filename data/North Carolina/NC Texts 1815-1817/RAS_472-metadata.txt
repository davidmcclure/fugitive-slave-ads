Slave name(s): Thompson
Slaveholder: Edward C. Outlaw
Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward. <BR> Will be given for apprehending and delivering to me, my Negro Fellow <BR>THOMPSON,<BR> who ran-away on the 25th of December last. He is about 5 feet 8 or 10 inches high, 30 years of age, yellow complexion, large white eyes, has a scar on one of his eye-lids, a small slit in one of his ears, speaks quick when spoken to, and is a very shrewd artful fellow. The above reward  will be paid if taken within Bertie County or 50 Dollars if taken without and delivered to me, and all reasonable expences paid. He is supposed to lurking in the neighborhood of Mr. Henry Copeland, in Gates County or in our Murfreesborough.--All masters of vessels and others are forbid for harboring, employing or carrying him off at their peril. <BR> Edward C. Outlaw. <BR> Bertie County, May 23, 1816.   tf 514
