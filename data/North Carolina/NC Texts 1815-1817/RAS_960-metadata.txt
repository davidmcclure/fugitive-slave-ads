Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN-AWAY from the Subscriber a Negro Man named<BR>ANTHONY, <BR>about  22 or 25 years of age, of a yellowish complexion, belonging to the Estate of Thomas Foy deceased, he is lurking about the neighborhood of Trent Bridge and has a wife at Thomas Hall's on or near Island Creek, where I suppose he is lurking, perhaps harboured. I will give the above reward to any person who will deliver him to me in Onslow County near the Court House.<BR>CHRISTOPHER DUDLEY.<BR>Onslow county, March 18th, 1815.
