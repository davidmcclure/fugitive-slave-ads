Transcript: Twenty-five Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, on the 5th instant, a MULATTO FELLOW, by the name of ALFRED, about 5 feet 10 inches high, stout built. He was raised in Pitt county, where it is probable he will endeavor to get to; or he may take some other course and pass as a freeman--having formed some acquaintance with some free persons of colour in Chatham county, he may have made thither.<BR>The above Reward, with all reasonable charges, will be paid to any person who will secure him in any Jail, or deliver him to the Subscriber, in Wake county, near Raleigh.<BR>April 13.  JOSEPH LANE, jun[junior]
