Transcript: Twenty Dollars reward.<BR>RUN AWAY from the undersigned at Vesuvius Furnace, Lincoln County, some time in June last, a negro fellow named BRITAIN, slim made, a little stoop-shouldered, about 30 years old, purchased about two years past of Lewis Lutterloh of Chatham, supposed he has gone either to that county or Orange, where it is said he formerly lived and belonged to a Mr. Thompson. Any person who will have him secured in Gaol, so that I get him, shall have a reward of Twenty Dollars.]<BR>  JOSEPH GRAHAM.<BR>October 25, 1816.
