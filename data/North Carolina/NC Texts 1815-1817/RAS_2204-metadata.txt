Transcript: Ranaway <BR>FROM the subscriber on the 27th March, 1816, a Negro Woman, about 20 years old, named CHLOE--She is small, has not particular marks about her. She will probably make for Gates county and perhaps cross Roanoke at Pollock's ferry. Any person apprehending said negro and giving me information, shall receive a reasonable reward.<BR>JOHN SILER.<BR>Chatham, April 1, 1816.
