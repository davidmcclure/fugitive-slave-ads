Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN away from the subscriber in the night of the 24th inst. at Salisbury, a Mulatto man named Adam, between 30 and 35 years of age, about five feet ten or eleven inches high, and spare made, he has a small piece out of one his ears, his hair tolerably long before, had on when he went away a pair of drab coloured pantaloons, a round coat and an old wool hat. It is believed he will try to get to Frederick County, Maryland, where he was purchased lately. The above reward will be paid to any person for apprehending and securing the above fellow in any jail so that the subscriber gets him again.<BR>Salisbury, Nov. 25th. 1816.   49-4t.<BR>JOSHUA GAY.
