Transcript: 100 Dollars Reward. <BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber's Plantation about the first of Janurary, Two Negro Men, named JIM and GUY. THe former about six feet high, stout and well made. The latter about five feet six or eight inches high--has a remarkable sullen countenance. Had on when they went away, a blue negro-cloth round Jacket and Trowsers[sic]. Guy had also on a green Great Coat--Both of them are well known on Topsail Sound and on the North-East, and it is believed they are lurking about one or the other of these places.<BR>ALSO, a Negro Woman, named CAROLINE, about 23 years old--black complexion--and has lost a piece from off the tooth next her eyetooth on the right side and upper row--and on her right breast, as well as is recollected, she has a large Scar from a burn, on which the skin is much raised. The reward will be paid in proportion for one or more delivered to me at my Plantation in New-Honover[sic] County, or Wilmington Jail--she formerly belonged to W. Culter.<BR>Edwd.[Edward] Mills. <BR>February 15---tf
