Transcript: Twenty five Dollars Reward<BR>Ranaway from the Subscriber, in the month of July last, the 8th day 1816, two Negroes, a man and woman, the man by the name of Jack, the woman, by the name of Edeth; the negro man is about 45 years of age, about 5 feet 7 or 8 inches high, with a remarkable scar in his face, on one of his Cheeks, his back scared very much of the whip; the woman is about 19 years of age, she is of common size for a woman, Remarkable long feet the great toe somewhat longer than the others; the said negro formerly belonged to Capt. Ransom and was sold by said Ransom to James Bell; -- the woman formerly belonged to Peter Askey in Bertie County; it is probable the negroes may be now lurking about Washington in Beaufort County. The above Reward will be given to any person who will deliver them to me or apprehending and  securing them in Jail so that I get them again.<BR>AHIMELECH HARTSFIELD.<BR>Lenoir County, N.C. July the 29th.  23 4t
