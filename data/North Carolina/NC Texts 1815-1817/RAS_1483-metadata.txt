Transcript: ONE HUNDRED DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY last night, from Mrs. Banks's on the road leading from Raleigh to Fayetteville, two NEGRO BOYS, ISAAC and SAMUEL.  Isaac is about 25 years old, one of his foreteeth out, light and well made, and is very ragged and dirty.  Samuel is 16 or 17 years old, and of a fine open intelligent countenance.<BR>The above reward will be given for their delivery to me at the said Mrs. Banks' or Twenty Dollars each to secure them in any Jail, so that I get them again.  They will aim for Norfolk.<BR>JOHN C. ELLIOTT.<BR>October 26, 1815    40 2w
