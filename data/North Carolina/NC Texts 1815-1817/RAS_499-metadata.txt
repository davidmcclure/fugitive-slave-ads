Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY on Friday the 23d inst. negro <BR>DANIEL,<BR>About twenty-five years old, five feet 10 inches high, stout made, has got a mark on his lower jaw occasioned by the tooth ache; he had on when he went away, a coarse blue cloth jacket and a pair of yarn trowsers with other clothes. He has expressed his intention of going to Washington in hopes of getting on board of a vessel to make his escape but the greater probability is, that he is gone towards Virginia, where he come from. The above reward will be given to any person who apprehends him and brings him to me, with all necessary expences paid; or to secure him in any jail so that I get him again.<BR>WM.[WILLIAM] GRACE.<BR>Mattamuskeet, 27th Feb. 1816.      tf 44
