Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>I will pay the above reward and all reasonable expences for apprehending and delivering to me in Guilford, near Bruce's Cro. Roads, my man KITT, who run away in the night between the 3rd and 4th of this month; Kitt is a bright mulatto, about 25 years old, five feet nine or ten inches high, has a down look, speaks plain, and lately marked with a whip, had on a homespun mixed cloth coat, his other cloathes not recollected; he probably will attempt to pass for a free man. He was formerly sold by col. Jo.[Joseph] Clark of Rowan, to Charles Philips of Rockingham, of whom I purchased him, but what way he has steered his course is uncertain.<BR>HUGH KIRKPATRICK.<BR>September 24, 1816.  40 3tp.
