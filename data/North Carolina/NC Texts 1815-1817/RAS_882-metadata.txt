Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward. <BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber at Smithville, on Sunday night last, Negro woman named ELSEY, purchased from Mr. William Grisack[?] formerly the property of Mr.[illegible], but [illegible] years back with Mr. Daney[?] at Smithville, she is yellowish black, has bushy hair, is of the middling size, speaks well and plausibly. She is probably at Mr. Grisack's[?] place where she has a husband and with whom she went off. The above reward will be given for apprehending the said runaway, on proof of her having been harboured by a white person, or ten dollars on delivery of her to the Subscriber or lodging her in jail. All other reasonable expences will also be paid. <BR>SAMUEL POTTER.<BR>April 10.--tf
