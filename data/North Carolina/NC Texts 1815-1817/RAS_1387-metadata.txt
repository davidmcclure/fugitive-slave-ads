Slave name(s): Mills

Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber, the 11th September last, a Negro Man, by the name of MILLS, about 23 years of age, 5 feet 10 inches high, stout and well made, rather yellow complected, one of his fingers off at the first joint.  He is a good Sawyer, and has sawed in Wake Franklin & Nash.  He will probably attempt to pass for a free man.  Any person who will deliver him to the subscriber shall receive the above reward--or Five Dollars if secured in any Jail so that I get him again.<BR>ALLEN HINES.<BR>27th October, 1815.    4t 3w
