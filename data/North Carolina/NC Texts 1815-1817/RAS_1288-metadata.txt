Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber on the 10th of Sept. last, a negro Wench named Hannah, formerly belonging to Mr. Almond Hall she is so well known in Wilmington that a description of her need not be given. I will give the above reward to any person that will deliver her to me or confine her in Jail so that I may get her again. I  hereby caution all white persons not to harbour said Runaway under the strictest penalty of the law. If said negro returns home in eight days from this date she shall be forgiven, if not I will out law her.<BR>ROBERR SIMPSON. <BR>November 9, 1815--tf
