Transcript: RUNAWAY NEGRO<BR>MY Negro Boy Patrick, 19 years old, but not well grown of his age, runaway about four weeks ago. Has large white eyes.  Had on an old Linsey Coat, Cotton Shirt and overalls.  He had no other Cloths[sic] with him.<BR>His Father lives near the Rocky Mount, S.C. and he may attempt to get there by means of a free pass.  Five Dollars Reward will be paid, if taken and secured in Caswell County; or ten dollars if taken and secured out of the County, and all reasonable expenses paid.<BR>NATH [NATHANIEL] GOOCH<BR>Caswell County, Aug. 30, 1815    1pd
