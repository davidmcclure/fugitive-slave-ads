Transcript: 50 Dollars Reward.<BR>WILL be given for apprehending and delivering to me, or securing in jail so that I get him, Negro SAMPSON, belonging to the estate of Henderson Standin, dec'd.[deceased] He is about 18 or 20 years of age, and formerly the property of Thos.[Thomas] Penrice. He is supposed to be lurking about Mrs. Arkill's, on Yeopim River. The above reward and reasonable expences will be paid for securing him as aforesaid. Masters of vessels and others, are forbid carrying him off at their peril.<BR>John B. Blount.<BR>Edenton, Aug. 12, 1816.   tf 524
