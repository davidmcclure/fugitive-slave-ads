When sharply spoken to, he stammers a little.<BR>Whoever shall apprehend the said Negro and lodge him in any Jail, so that he may be recovered to the Owner, shall receive a Reward of Twenty-five Dollars.<BR>SHAD.[SHADRACH] STALLINGS.<BR>Duplin county, Feb. 16    67 3w
