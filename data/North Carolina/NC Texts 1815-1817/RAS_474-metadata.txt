The above rewad will be given any person apprehending and delivering her to me, or secure her in Jail, so that I get her and all reasonable expences paid. <BR>All persons are forbid harboring, employing or carrying her away, under the penalty of the law. <BR>PAUL WHITE. <BR>Bachelor's Creek, Craven <BR>County, Aug. 6th, 1816.
