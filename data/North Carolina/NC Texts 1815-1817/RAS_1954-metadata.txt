Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the night of the 19th instant, a Negro Man by the name of LEVEN, about 5 feet 8 or 10 inches high, blind in the right eye, a small crop off each ear, about 37 years old; carried away with him a blue cotton caot and an old white one, a striped cotton vest, high crown'd hat.  I suppose he will aim for Norfolk or Petersburg, Virginia.  Any person that delivers, or secures said fellow so that I get him again, shall receive the above reward, &c[etc].<BR>JOHN ADAMS,<BR>Guilford county, N.C.<BR>2 miles west of Martinville[Martinsville].<BR>September 23.    90
