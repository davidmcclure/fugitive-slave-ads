Transcript: $50 REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, about the 10th of April last, a Negro man named THEOPHILUS, about 28 years of age, 5 feet 10 inches high, tolerably spare made, dark complexion, with a few gray hairs on his head. He was purchased by A. F. Moses, from A. Wiley, in Wayne County. The above reward will be given to any person who will apprehend said Negro, and deliver him to the subscriber in Newbern, to A.F. Moses, in Wayne County, or secure him in any jail so that I get him again; and all reasonable expences will be paid.<BR>JOHN GILDERSLEEVE.<BR>2d June, 1831.    '48tf<BR>The Editors of the Raleigh Star will please insert the above three months, and forward their account to this office for payment.
