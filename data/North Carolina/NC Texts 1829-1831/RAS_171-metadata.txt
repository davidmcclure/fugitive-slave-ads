Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, living in Robeson county, on the 14th ultimo, a negro man by the name<BR>BEN.<BR>Said negro is about 6 feet high, stoutly made, of a very dark complexion, rather inclined to be bald; and has an impediment in his speech. As he previously lived in Fayetteville, he may probably be lurking about, or in the neighborhood of said place. I will give the above reward to any one who will lodge him in any jail so that I get him again.<BR>DUNCAN McBRIDE.<BR>Robeson County. April 6, 1831.    6 3tp.
