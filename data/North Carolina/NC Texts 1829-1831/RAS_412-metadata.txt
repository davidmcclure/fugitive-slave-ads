Transcript: $50 REWARD.<BR>A REWARD of fifty dollars will be given for the apprehension and delivery of a negro fellow named<BR>SOLOMON,<BR>who ran away from the Major Charles Gee whilst living at Mush Island, about 12 months ago. Solomon is a very stout, likely fellow, about 25 or 26 years of age, quite black--or $35 for him if he is secured in any jail so that I get him again.<BR>I will also give Twenty Dollars for the apprehension and delivery of my negro man<BR>NELSON,<BR>who ran away on Tuesday last. Nelson is a yellow fellow, about 27 or 28 years of age, has rather a down look when spoken to; he has been for some time my carriage driver, and was formerly the servant of the late Wm.[William] T. Williams.<BR>STERLING H. [illegible]<BR>.22d March, 1832.
