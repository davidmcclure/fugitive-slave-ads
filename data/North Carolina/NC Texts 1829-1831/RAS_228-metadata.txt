Transcript: $20 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on the 1st inst. a Negro Man named<BR>SIMON;<BR>thirty-three or four years of age, about six feet three inches high, very large round the breast and shoulders, large arms, thick lips, large eye-brows, and very knock kneed, remarkably artful. He may endeavor to hide his being knock-kneed by wearing large pantaloons. He formerly belonged to Thomas Armstrong, deceased, and afterwards to Mr. Jarrott, from whom I purchased him. He is a good Cooper, and can use all kinds of edge tools well. I will give the above reward to any person who will secure him in any jail so that I get him again, and all reasonable charges paid.<BR>JOHN MURCHISON.<BR>Fayetteville, Cumberland County<BR>North Carolina, Feb 8, 1831.       14tf.
