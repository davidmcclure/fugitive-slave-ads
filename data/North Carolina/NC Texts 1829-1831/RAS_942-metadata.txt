Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, on Tuesday the 14th inst. his negro fellow BILLY.  He is about 5 feet 7 inches high, 25 years old, of yellow complection and wears a pair of whiskers.  He had on when he went away, a brown homespun roundabout, cloth pantaloons nearly black, an old bell crown hat and shoes about half worn.  He was purchased of Mr. JOHN GILDERSLEEVE of Newbern and it is expected that he will endeavor to make his way back to that town, via Hillsborough and Raleigh.<BR>The above reward of Twenty Dollars will be paid for his apprehension and confinement in any Jail in the State or for his delivery to the Subscriber, at his plantation in Rockingham county, 14 miles east of the Court-house.<BR>WILLIAM BETHELL.<BR>36 5w.
