Transcript: $5 REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, about the 1st of March last, a negro woman named MERINDA, about 30 years of age, 5 feet 6 inches high, light complexion, and slender made.  She formerly belonged to Mrs. Daves, and is probably lurking in the neighbourhood of Newbern. The above Reward and all reasonable expenses, will be paid for her apprehension and delivery to the subscriber, or for securing her in any jail so that she get her again.<BR>K. SAWYER.<BR>18th June, 1831.   '49 vt.
