Transcript: Thirty Dollars Reward.<BR>Ran away from the subscriber, in November last, from his plantation in Moore county, his negro man JO. He is between 35 and 40 years of age, of common size, quite black and a very keen shrewd fellow. He was formerly the property of a Mr. Harris, of Chatham; but was purchased by the subscriber of Geo.[George] W. Alston, Esq. of Moore. He is frequently seen lurking about in these two counties. A reward of thirty dollars will be given for his delivery to the jailor of Wake county, or to the subscriber in Wake Forest; or twenty dollars for his apprehension and confinement in the jail of Chatham or Moore.<BR>THOMAS M. D. REID.<BR>Wake Forest, Aug. 30, 1831<BR>36 2m
