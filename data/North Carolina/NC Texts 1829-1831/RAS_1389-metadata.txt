Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY about the 1st of Dec. BILL, usually known by the name of Bill Cain. He is about 5 feet 8 inches high, and slender. Bill is unusually intelligent, though he hesitates a good deal when he attempts to speak. He is well known in Wilmington and the vicinity; and has a wife in Fayetteville, in the neighborhood of which place he may possibly be lurking. The above reward will be paid on his delivery at the jail in Wilmington; or to capt. Samuel Potter at Smithville.<BR>Jan. 13-37-tf)    WM.[WILLIAM] DEBERNIERE.
