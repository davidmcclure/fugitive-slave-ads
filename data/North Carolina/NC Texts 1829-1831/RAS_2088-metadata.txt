Transcript: $15 Reward<BR>WILL be paid for the apprehension and delivery to me, or confining in jail so that I get him, negro man HENRY; belonging to the estate of the Rev. Henry Holmes dec'd [deceased]; and fellow is so well known in this place and its neighbourhood, as an excellent Sawyer, a description is deemed unncessary. It is believed that he is lurking in the neighbourhood of Gen. Richard T. Brownrigg, where he has a sister living; or in the neighbourhood of Bear Swamp. All persons are forwarned harbouring or employing him. <BR>WM.[William] B. ROBERTS, Executor.<BR>Edenton Dec. 19 tf 51.
