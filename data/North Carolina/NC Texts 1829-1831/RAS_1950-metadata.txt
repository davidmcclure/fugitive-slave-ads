Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>Ran away from the subscriber, on the 12th ultimo, a bright mulatto man, by the name of ANTHONY, about 5 feet 9 or 10 inches in height, about 24 years of age, with bushy, long hair, and is slender made. He had on when he went away, white homespun pantaloons, a little mixed with streaks of dark wool, a mixed homespun coat, an old black fur hat, with a likeness of Lafayette, surrounded by the words ""Lafayette, a Nation's Guest."" He is in the habit of smiling when spoken to, and speaks very quickly. The above reward will be given for his delivery to me, residing six miles west of Raleigh, on the Chapel Hill road, or for his confinement in any jail, so that I get him again.<BR>WILLIS SCOTT.<BR>Nov. 2, 1830<BR>45 tf
