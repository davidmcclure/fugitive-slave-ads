Transcript: $10 REWARD<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, living in Hillsboro', a<BR>MULATTO BOY about 20 years old, 5 feet 6 or 7 inches high, inclined to be knock-kneed; walks with his feet turned out, and has rather an impertinent look. No idea is entertained where he will aim for, except that he has been seen in Milton, and stated that he was going to Col. Cogourn's, in Halifax county, Virginia. He is a Carpenter by trade.<BR>THO:JEFF:FADDIS<BR>July 25th. 1830--7-tf.<BR>The Lynchburg Virginian will please insert the above two months, and forward the account to this office for payment.
