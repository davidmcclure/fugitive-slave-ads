Transcript: $30 REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber's Agent, while passing through Rockingham County, N.C. about the 12th ult. three Negro men-viz:<BR>TOM WHITFIELD, SIMON, AND AARON.<BR>TOM WHITFIELD is about 40 years of age, five feet seven inches high, well made, quite dark complexion, and his eyes are generally a little red. SIMON is about 28 years of age, about five feet five inches high, stout made, and light complexion. He was purchased near Raleigh, and will probably be lurking in that neighborhood. AARON formerly belonged to Henry W. Jones--he is about 45 years of age, about 5 feet six inches high, dark complexion, and has lost some of his front teeth. He formerly lived in Edenton, where he has a wife, and it is supposed that he will be lurking in that neighborhood.<BR>The above reward will be paid for the apprehension and delivery to the subscriber, of said negroes, or for securing them in any jail so that he gets them again, or ten dollars for either of them.<BR>HENRY B. MITCHELL.<BR>Newbern, 7th, May, 1831.   '43kt
