Transcript: $40 REWARD.<BR> RUNAWAY in May last, a negro man named Jacob, about 33 years old, five feet ten inches high, very black, quick spoken and fast walk, has a scar on his left leg. Any person delivering him to me near Salisbury, shall be paid $40, or $25 if confined in any jail so that I get him again. <BR> ARCH'D.[ARCHIBALD] G. CARTER.<BR>January 1st, 1831.  32tf<BR> N.B. The Newbern Sentinel will please insert the above four times and forward their account to this Office.
