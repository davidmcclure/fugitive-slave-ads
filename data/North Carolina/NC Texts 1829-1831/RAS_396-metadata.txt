Date of departure: 1831-06-05

Slave name(s): Joe
Slaveholder: Wm. [William] P. Biddle
Transcript: $50 Reward.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber on the 5th of June last, his negro man JOE, he is about six feet three inches high, and quite black, has a long neck, and looks, as he is, big and independent. He has been much of his time in Pitt County, and will probably attempt to pass to another State. All persons are hereby forewarned against harbouring or carrying him away. The reward of Fifty Dollars will be paid to any one who will deliver him to me.<BR>WM. [WILLIAM] P. BIDDLE.<BR>Craven County, Nov. 1st 1831. '69 tf.
