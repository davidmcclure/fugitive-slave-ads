Transcript: $25 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber's Cool Spring Plantation, on Tuesday evening, the 19th inst. negro man<BR>WLLL,[Will]<BR>Without very little or any provocation. The above reward will be given to any person who will deliver the said negro to my overseer on the above plantation, or Thirty Dollars, if delivered into the jail of Tarborough. The above fellow is strong and muscular, weighing at least 180 or 190 lbs. and has an impediment in his speech, or stutters in his conversation and considerably when confused. All orderly citizens are solicited to have a look out and arrest him if an opportunity should offer. It is expected he is lurking about in the neighborhood of the above plantation, or has gone to Halifax county.<BR>JAMES S. BATTLE.<BR>Tarboro', April 22, 1831. 36
