Headline: $20 reward
Publishing newspaper: Edenton, N.C. : The Edenton Gazette and North Carolina General Advertiser
Date of advertisement: 1830-06-10
Time period (decade): 1830-1839
Person placing ad: Chas. W. Mixson
Slave name(s): Abner;Primer
Transcript: $20 Reward<BR>WILL be given for apprehending and delivering to me, or confining in jail so that I get them again, my negro men ABNER and PRIMER, who ran away from me a short time since. Abner is about 22 years of age, dark complexion, about 5 feet 8 inches high, and is somewhat lame in his right leg. Primer is a brother of his, is about 20 years of age, black, about the same height, and is a very likely fellow. They are believed to be lurking in the neighborhood of the town of Edenton. I will give the above reward to any person, for the apprehension of both, or $10 for each, and pay all reasonable expenses.<BR>CHAS.[Charles] W. MIXSON.<BR>Chowan Co. June 10.<BR>tf 24
