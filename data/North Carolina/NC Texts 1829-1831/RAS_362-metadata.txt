Transcript: $5 REWARD. <BR>I will give the above reward for the apprehension and delivery to me of my slave PETER, or for such information as will lead to his apprehensioin. Peter is good six feet high, stout, black, and about 26 years old,--has a plain scar through his left eye brow. I expect he is lurking about Newbern, or between Trent River and Bachelor's Creek. <BR>ALFRED STANLY.<BR>Jones County, 9th April, 1830.
