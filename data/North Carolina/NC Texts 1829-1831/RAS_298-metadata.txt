Transcript: $25 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, last June, Negro Man BOSTON, formerly the property of Amos Wade, of this place.--BOSTON is about 27 or 28 years of age, about 5 feet 6 or 8 inches high, stout built, with a prominent forehead. I understand he is loitering about Newbern, and down the River some 10 or 15 miles. Any person apprehending said Negro, and delivering him to John Gildersleave, shall receive the above Reward.<BR>JOHN WILLIAMS.<BR>March 31st, 1831.   '38kt
