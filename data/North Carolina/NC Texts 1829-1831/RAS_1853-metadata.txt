Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, in February last, a Negro Man named GEORGE, belonging to the estate of the late D. Shackelford. He is of yellow complexion, very stout, and six feet two or three inches high. He has a wife near the plantation of John B Dawson, on Swift Creek, about which place and Newbern, he is probably lurking. The above reward will be paid for his apprehension and safe confinement in Jail. <BR>L.S. HUMPHREY. <BR>Richlands, Onslow County, 10th March, 1830.
