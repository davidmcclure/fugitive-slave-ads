Transcript: Ten Dollars.<BR>WILL be given for the apprehension and delivery to me of my man Isaac Wingate, who ranaway from my plantation in April last. Isaac is 5 feet 8 or 10 inches, high 25 to 30 years of age, in color a mustee and has a remarkable bushy head of hair, with large heavy eye brows. I have reason to believe he is now lurking in Duplin where he has a mother.<BR>R. H. COWAN.
