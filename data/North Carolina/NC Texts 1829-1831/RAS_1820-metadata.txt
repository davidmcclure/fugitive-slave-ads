Transcript: Stop the Runaways.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber on the night of the 4th inst. three negro men named<BR>CAESAR, BEN & AMOS.<BR>Caesar is about 25 years of age, five feet 5 or 6 inches high, very black, round face, and had on double-wove home made cloth. Ben is about 22 years old, 5 feet 6 inches high, a bright mulatto, has been stabbed in his left side on the breast, and had on double-wove homespun clothes, which were dyed a dark color; he is quite a sensible, clear spoken fellow. Amos is about 23 years of age, 5 feet 3 or 4 inches high, inclined to be yellow, with a scar (or rather mark, resembling molasses) on his left cheek, had on the same kind of clothing but not dyed.<BR>EDMUNDS MYRICK.<BR>Halifax county, April 6, 1831.<BR>6--tf
