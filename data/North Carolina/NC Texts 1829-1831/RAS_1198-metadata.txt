Transcript: NOTICE<BR>RAN away from the subscriber on Sunday evening last, a negro boy by the name of PATRICK, about 18 years of age, has a mark on his breast about the size of a buck shot, to appearance a mole.  He was seen a little below this place, on the Petersburg road, and is supposed to have gone in that direction.  A reasonable reward will be given for his apprehension and delivery to me, or if he is confined in any jail and information given so that I get him again.<BR>James Allison.<BR>Oct. 6.  1--3W
