Transcript: TEN DOLLARSR[sic] REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 22 ult. a Negro woman named RACHEL; about 50 years of age. She formerly belonged to Mr. John Street--has a sore mouth, and is generally known in this neighborhood. --It is probable that she is lurking somewhere in Newbern or its vicinity. All persons are cautioned against harboring, employing, or carrying her away, under the penalty of the law. The above reward will be paid for her delivery to the subscriber.<BR>BRYAN EDMONDSON.<BR>21st Oct. 1831.  67vt
