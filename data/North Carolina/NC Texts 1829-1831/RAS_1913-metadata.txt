Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber on the 11th instant a negro man by the name of BOB, of very dark complexion and about forty years old.  It is probable said fellow may be lurking about Charles M'Cauley's, as he has a sister there.  I will give a reward of Ten Dollars to any one who will apprehend said fellow and deliver him to me, four miles east of Chapel Hill, or secure him so that I can get him again.  I will also pay all reasonable expenses.<BR>Christopher Barbee, Sen.<BR>July 15th  07--1w
