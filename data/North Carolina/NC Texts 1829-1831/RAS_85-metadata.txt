It is probable is he lurking about Citizen S. Woods, in the Forks of the Yadkin. Any person apprehending him or delivering him to the subscriber, living eight miles east of Concord, Cabarrus county, N.C. shall be reasonably rewarded; if caught within the State, Ten Dollars.<BR>M.
