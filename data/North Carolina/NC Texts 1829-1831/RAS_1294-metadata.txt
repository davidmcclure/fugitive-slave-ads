Slave name(s): Brantly

Former slaveholder: Sam.[Samuel] Smotherman
Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber, on Saturday, the 10th instant, my Negro Boy BRANTLY, who is about 6 feet high, has a down look, is yellow complected and very active.  Had on when he left, an old smooth fur hat and a Northern and a Country homespun shirt, blue cloth vest, and old sattinett pantaloons.  I purchased Brantly from Mr. Sam.[Samuel] Smotherman, of Randolph county, about the 1st of April.  The above reward will be given to any person who will lodge him in any Jail so that I get him, or who will deliver him to me.<BR>JOHN McKINNON.<BR>Mill Creek, Moore County, April 17.    72tf.
