Transcript: RANAWAY<BR>FROM the Subscriber living in Cumberland co'ty[county] on the 14th instant, a Negro girl, by the name of<BR>JEMIMAH<BR>About fourteen years old, 4 1/2 feet high, stoutly made, and rather inclined to be of a yellow complexion. As she was brought from Pitt county by a Mr. Thomas. (from whom I purchased her,) she may attempt to return to, or in the neighborhood of, said county. To any one who will apprehend said negro, in this County, and return her to me, I will give a reward of Three Dollars--or in case she is taken out of the county, and secured in any jail, so that I get her again, a reward of Five Dollars will be given.<BR>JAMES HITCHERSON.<BR>Nov. 24--'3i.
