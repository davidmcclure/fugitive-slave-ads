Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber about the 18th inst. a negro man named STEPHEN, about 45 years of age, a stout made fellow, about five feet seven or eight inches high, has a remarkable broad foot, and a very long heel ;  a cooper by trade.<BR>I forwarn[sic] all masters of vessels from carrying him out of the country, or any person from employing him.  He is an artful fellow, and will try to pass for a free man.<BR>The above reward will be paid to any person, taking and securing him in any gaol, so as I may get him, or delivering him to Mr. Tho. Amia at Halifax.  He formerly belonged to Stewart and Muir, and is well known about Halifax.<BR>CHRISTOPHER CLARK.<BR>Bertie county, Sept. 21  73
