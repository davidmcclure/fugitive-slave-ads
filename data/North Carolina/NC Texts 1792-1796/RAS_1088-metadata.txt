Transcript: FIFTEEN DOLLARS REWARD<BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber's plantation in Moore county, on the night of the 24th of January last, a Negro man named ADAM, nearly six feet high, and about twenty-five years of age, well made and likely, country born: sensible and active and of a colour not very black--had on when he went away a dark drab surtout coat, and a brown closabodyed[sic] do, both some what worn, also a good pair lasting breeches.--It is expected he will pass himself for a free man, I therefore offer the above reward and all necessary expenses, for apprehending and securing the said negro in any gaol, or on delivering him again to  NICHOLAS NALL<BR>Moore county, February 6.    88 5
