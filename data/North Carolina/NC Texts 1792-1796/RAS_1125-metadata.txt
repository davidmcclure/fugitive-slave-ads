Headline: Five pounds reward
Publishing newspaper: New Bern, N.C. : North Carolina Gazette, or Impartial Intelligencer, and Weekly General Advertiser
Date of advertisement: 1794-06-14
Page: 4
Time period (decade): 1790-1799
Person placing ad: William Borden
County of residence: Carteret County (N.C.)
Presumed or intended destination: Newbern
Slave name(s): Mundean
Slaveholder: William Borden
Transcript: FIVE POUNDS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, the present instant, a negro man named MUDEAN, about twenty five years of age, speaks good English, well made, and largely featured in his face, he has lost some of his small toes off of one or both of his feet, it is supposed he will make for Newbern, where he was taken up at lately, but was secreted for some time before, and has said since that he had like to have passed away from thence in some vessel. Any person taking up the said negro and conveying him to me or to the public jail so that I get him again, I will give them five pounds and all necessary expences.<BR>WILLIAM BORDEN.<BR>Carteret County, June 14.<BR>N.B. All persons are forwarned of either employing said negro or carrying him off.
