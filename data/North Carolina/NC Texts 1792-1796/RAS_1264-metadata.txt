Headline: Five pounds reward
Publishing newspaper: New Bern, N.C. : North Carolina Gazette, or Impartial Intelligencer, and Weekly General Advertiser
Date of advertisement: 1793-08-31
Page: 4
Time period (decade): 1790-1799
Person placing ad: Levi Fulsher
Slave name(s): Josh
Slaveholder: Levi Fulsher
Former slaveholder: Francis Lowthorp, Esquire
Transcript: FIVE POUNDS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber about a week ago, a negro fellow, named JOSH; about five feet eight or nine inches high, rather of a yellowish complexion, well set and strong built. He was formerly the property of Francis Lowthorp, Esq. and has lately been outlawed.<BR>The above reward will be paid to any person who will secure him so that I get him again, or to any person that may kill him in case he suffers not himself to be apprehended.<BR> Levi Fulsher.<BR>N.B. ALL Persons are forwarned from harbouring, or employing him and Masters of vessel from carrying him away.
