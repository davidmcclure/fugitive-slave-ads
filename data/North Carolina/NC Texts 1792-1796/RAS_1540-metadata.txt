Transcript: RAN away from the subscriber, on the 3d ult. a negro wench named POLL, sometimes called MOLL, about seventeen years of age. She is well set, of a small stature, of a black complexion, and has very plump eyes.  She has with her a male child, about 14 months old, called HARDY, of a yellowish complexion, one of whose eyes on close examination will appear somewhat smaller than the other.<BR>I suppose she is concealed by a certain Benjamin Gray, of Jones County, who once shipped her away to the West-Indies.<BR>All persons are forwarned harboring or concealing her -- or from buying her from the said Benjamin Gray or any other person; and masters of vessel from carrying her out of the state; as, in such cases, they will be prosecuted with the utmost rigor of the law.<BR>I will give a reward of five pounds to any person who will secure her so that I may get her again.<BR>JAMES WELLS.<BR>Jones County, October 26.
