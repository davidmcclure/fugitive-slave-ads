Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN-away from the subscriber on the 20th August last, a negro man named<BR>NAT,<BR>Country born and sensible, about 27 years of age, very black and strait built, about five feet nine or ten inches high, he is supposed to harbour about William Easters in Jones county. Whoever will take up the said negro, and secure him in any gaol in this state, so that I get him or deliver him to me shall be entitled to the above reward.<BR>RICHARD CROOM.<BR>Wayne county, March 22.
