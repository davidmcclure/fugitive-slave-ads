Headline: Ten dollars reward

Date of departure: 1794-01-26
Slave name(s): Charles
Slaveholder: John Pons

Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN-AWAY from the subscriber on Sunday the 26th of January last, a Negro Man by the name of CHARLES, about 40 years old, stout made, of black complexion, about five feet four inches high has been shot in his left foot, which occassions a very great fear on it.  He was formerly the property of Mr. James Lyne of Granville, afterwards of the late William Branch.  The above reward will be given to any person security of delivering him to me.<BR>Feb. 26.  86 3  JOHN PONS.
