Transcript: RAN-AWAY about the 10th instant, a Mulatto Woman named TAB, betwixt 40 and 50 years of age, pretty stout made, and tolerably sensible, is a little marked with the small-pox.  She had on when she went away, a straw hat, but what clothes in uncertain.  She has a little dog, nearly white, that follows her and answers to the name Fan.  Any Person who will deliver her to me at Warrenton, shall receive FIVE DOLLARS Reward, and be allowed all reasonable charges.
