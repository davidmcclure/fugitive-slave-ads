Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber on the 24th. of July last, a Negro named TOBY--He is a stout likely fellow, about five feet nine inches high, had on when he went away a blue (Sailor's) jacket and Oznaburgs trowsers--It is likely he will endeavour to pass as free, having remained as such with the British since they left this town until within these three years past. He is a sensible fellow, can read (and I belive write) whoever will deliver him in Wilmington to the subscirber, or lodge him in any Goal shall receive the above reward and all reasonable charges paid. <BR>Henry Toomer.<BR>Wilmington, August 2.
