Transcript: TEN POUNDS REWARD.<BR>BROKE Jail, last week, a negro fellow named ABEL, about five feet two inches high, very likely made, nearly nineteen years of age, speaks very good English.<BR>He is supposed to be gone towards Wilmington, and it is likely he will pass by some other name.  Had on ragged homespun clothes.<BR>Whoever will take up said negro and so secure him that I get him again shall have the above reward and all reasonable charges paid.<BR>WILLIAM BELL.<BR>Hyde County, Bell's Bay, near Currituck, May 24.
