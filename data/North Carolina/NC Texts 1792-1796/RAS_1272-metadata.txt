Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN AWAY from the subscriber, in the upper part of Halifax county, on the 11th inst. (July) a negro fellow named CHARLES, about twenty two years old, stout and well made, and has lost part of the great and second toes of the right foot.  It is expected he is gone to Gates county.  Whoever brings him home shall receive the above reward and reasonable charges.  50 5  JOHN ROPER.
