Transcript: RAN AWAY,<BR>FROM the subscriber, on Tuesday last week, a likely young<BR>NEGRO MAN,<BR>Named JAME--stout-built, of a dark copper-color, about 21 years of age; he has a small piece apparently cut off his left ear. Had on when he went away, a short blue jacket, oznaberg shirt and overalls, and a black wool hat. It is supposed he is gone towards the Moravian Towns, or to his mother's in Salisbury.--He speaks the Dutch and English languages plainly.<BR>Any person who will secure the said negro until sent for, shall receive an ample reward.<BR>W. MACNAUGHTON.<BR>Fayetteville, May 3.<BR>w3
