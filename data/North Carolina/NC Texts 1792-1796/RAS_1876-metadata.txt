Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RUN away from the subscriber, on the 19th of September last, a negro women[sic] named ROSE, about 30 years old, stout built, very black, has a scare[scar] over her right eye bone, a flesh mole on one of her ears, near as large as a pea; it is probable she is lurking about the town of Newbern, or over trent.  Whoever secures said negro in Newbern jail, or bring her to the subscriber, shall receive the above reward, with all expenses paid. <BR>March 29. <BR>BENJAMIN GARROT.
