Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 12th inst. a negro man named PETER, about 50 years of age, 5 feet 5 inches high, yellow complexion, has a lage[sic] scar on his righ[sic] arm, occasioned by a burn--had on when he went away a homespun dress, but it is expected he will change it--he was born in this state, is very modest and has a remarkable fine voice--he is an artful fellow and will attempt to pass for a free man.<BR>Any person who will apprehend the above fellow, and secure him in the jail at Halifax, or deliver him to the subscriber at Beech-Swamp, near Enfield Court-house, shall be entitled to the above reward and all reasonable charges paid by<BR>JEREMIAH DOGGETT<BR>January 23, 1793.
