Transcript: RAN away from the subscriber the night of the second day of this inst, two negro fellows, one named LAMB  a yellow fellow well made about thirty five years of age, and about five feet eight or nine inches high, he is sensible and speaks good English, the others, name is PETER, a black fellow of a smaller size, about the same age, and about five feet seven or eight inches high, speaks good English also, any person apprehending said negroes and will bring them to me, or confine them in any gaol in this state so that the subscriber will get them, shall have a reward of five pound for each negro with all reasonable expenses paid accruing therein,<BR>THOMAS HOOKS<BR>Duplin county, December 4th.
