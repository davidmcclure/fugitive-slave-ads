Transcript: RUN-AWAY about the first of August last, a Negro woman, named KATE, about 35 or 36 years of age, yellow complexion, long bushy hair : She was formerly the property of Captain Powell, of Brunswick, Virginia, to which place it is probable she may have gone ; or to Mr. Wm. Harrden's in Suffex, where she has a husband--it is supposed she will attempt to pass for a free woman.  Whoever delivers her to the subscriber, or confines her in gaol so that she may be had, shall receive a generous reward.<BR>DUDLEY HARGROVE<BR>Halifax county, Sept. 30  64
