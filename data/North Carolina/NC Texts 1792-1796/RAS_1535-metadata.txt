Transcript: RAN away from the subscriber, on Bear-Creek in Beaufort County, on Friday the 11th Instant, PETER a negro man about 30 years of age, and nearly six feet high.--He has lost two of his upper foreteeth; and, under one of his ears, is a remarkable scar of a whip.  He had on when he went away a coarse homespun shirt and trowsers -- also at the same time, DINAH his wife a negro woman of yellowish complexion about 19 years old, of midling stature, with a male child, named SIMON about 8 months old.  I will give a reward of ten dollars, to any person who will apprehend the fellow and bring him to my house aforesaid or five dollars for apprehending and confining him in any goal so that I may get him.  A reward of forty shillings will also be given for apprehending and bringing home the wench, if taken in the county or six dollars, if taken else where.<BR>ROBERT GOULEY.<BR>October 26.
