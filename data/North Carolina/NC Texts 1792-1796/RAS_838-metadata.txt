Transcript: EIGHT POUNDS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber two Negroes, one named BOB, and the other FEREBEE, they are about twenty three years old, small and very black, the fellow's back is very much marked with the whip.--<BR>Any person that will deliver the said negro to me, or to secure them that I get them again, shall be entitled to the above reward.<BR>December 25th, 1795.<BR>S. Jernegan.<BR>N.B. All masters of vessels are forwarned from carrying them away; and others from harbouring or employing them at their peril.
