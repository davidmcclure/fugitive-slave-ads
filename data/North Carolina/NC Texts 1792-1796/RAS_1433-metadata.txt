Headline: Ten dollars reward
Publishing newspaper: Edenton, N.C. : State Gazette of North Carolina
Date of advertisement: 1794-04-25
Page: 3
Time period (decade): 1790-1799
Person placing ad: Job Leary
Presumed or intended destination: Virginia
Slave name(s): Gans
Slaveholder: Job Leary
Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN AWAY from the subscriber a likely dark mulatto negro man, named GANS, about six feet high, a down look, about 25 years of age; he has with him a part of nankeen trowsers, a white cotton homespun coat, one pair of cotton trowsers; he has been marked with the whip.  It is expected that he is gone to Virginia, and will endeavour to pass as a free man.  Whoever takes up the said negro, shall have the above reward, if in Virginia and confined so that I get him again.<BR>JOB LEARY.<BR>April 22, 1794.
