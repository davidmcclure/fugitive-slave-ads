Transcript: RUN-away from the subscriber on the 12th of March last, two slaves, one a mulatto fellow named<BR>JIM<BR>about twenty five years of age, he is stout and well built, has a remarkable large foot, and a small piece off one of his ears, the other a lad named<BR>MARCH<BR>of about 18 years of age, he is also stout and well built, his left hand was burnt when an infant, and has but his first finger and thumb--the fellow Jim was hired last summer to Mr. David Murdock near Newbern, it is probable he may be lurking some place about Newbern, and the other lad with him--whoever will apprehend said slaves and secure them in any jail in this state shall have five dollars reward for each, and if brought to me near Fayetteville, shall receive a reward of twenty dollars, or ten dollars for one of them.<BR>JOHN DICKSON.<BR>Blocker's ferry, April 16.
