Had on when he went away nothing more than an old woolen trowsers and jacket, having left his clothes behind.  Any person who will apprehend and deliver, or confine him, so that he may be had, shall be generously rewarded by<BR>J. WILLIS.<BR>Lumberton, Sept 15, 1792.
