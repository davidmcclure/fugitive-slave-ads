Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, some time in December last, two negro fellows, CAESAR and TOM; Caesar is twenty five years old, about six feet high, and has but one eye, he is very fond of playing on the fiddle.  Tom is a low well made fellow, about thirty years old, has a down look, branded on each cheek, his ears cropped, and his back much marked with the whip.  The above reward will be given, or twenty five dollars for either, delivered to the subscriber.<BR>L. O'BRYAN.<BR>Tarborough, Feb. 6th, 1794.
