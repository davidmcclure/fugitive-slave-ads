Transcript: FORTY SHILLINGs REWARD.<BR>RUN-AWAY from the subscriber's plantation on Friday the 11th instant, a mulatto lad named James Bateman.--He is about 14 or 15 years old, stout made. Has a large face, very dark, much the appearance of an Indian; he has a scar on his breast which appears to be occasioned by a burn. The above reward will be paid to any persons will be paid to any person who delivers the said boy to the subscriber in Newbern or to his overseer at Clermont.<BR>R.D. SPAIGHT.<BR>July 26.
