Transcript: FORTY SHILLINGS Reward. <BR> RUN AWAY from the subscriber,  a negro boy named GRANT, about 14 years old, slender made, branded on the shoulder in the West Indies with the letters  G.M.K. He had on when he absented himself a green jacket and trowsers. I will give twenty five dollars reward to any person informing me where he is harboured, so that the person or persons harbouring him may be brought to punishment, and the above reward will be given for delivering the boy to me at Plymouth. <BR> GEORGE M'KENZIE. <BR> Plymouth, February 17, 1794.
