Transcript: FIFTEEN DOLLARS Reward.<BR>RUN away from the subscriber, about the 20th of July last, a mulatto fellow, about thirty-five years old, named DAVID, five feet eight inches high.  He stands crooked on his right ancle, from a dislocation of that joint, and understands the carpenters and house-jointers business.  Any person apprehending the said negro shall have the above reward, if taken out of the county, and ten dollars if within it.<BR>BENJ.[Benjamin] WILLIAMS.<BR>Cashie-Neck, Oct. 15, 1795
