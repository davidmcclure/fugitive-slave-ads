Transcript: TEN POUNDS REWARD.<BR>RAN-AWAY from my plantation on Muth[?]-Island a likely Negro woman, named SUKEY ; she is somewhat taller than the generality of women, remarkably broad faced, very stout and strong, and about twenty five years of age, she was hired out during my minority and it is probable that as she lived in that time in Connoconnary, in the Occonechie Neck, and on the North Swamp, that she will be round in or about some of those places.  Any person delivering her to me or my overseer, shall receive the above reward.  And I hereby forewarn all persons from harbouring the said slave Sukey, as I will prosecute those who shall<BR>HALCOTT BRIGGS PRIDE
