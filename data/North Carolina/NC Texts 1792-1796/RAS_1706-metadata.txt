Transcript: RUN-AWAY from the subscriber the 19th inst. at night, a Negro Wench, named Betty; which my late uncle James McDongal bought of Mrs. Wall : She is between 40 and 50 years old, and is very handy about a house.  I will amply reward the person who delivers her to me, and I hereby forewarn all persons from harbouring her, being determined to prosecute those who shall.<BR>JOHN SLOANE.<BR>Halifax county, Dec. 27  79
