Transcript: Eight Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN away from the subscriber, on Tuesday morning last, a negro woman, named LUCY, about 25 years of age; she is tall, rather a yellow complexion, and when spoken to, stammers, particularly if interrogated closely. . She lived with Mr. Allen one or two years, and formerly belonged to the estate of James Blount, dec.[deceased] her mother lives with Mrs. Blount, near Mosby's Point.  The above reward will be given for bringing her home, or FIVE DOLLARS for securing her in gaol and giving me information thereof, and all reasonable charges.<BR>HENRY HILL.<BR>Bertie county, July 17, 1793.    4
