Transcript: RUN away from the subscriber some time in the month of March, a negro man, called MARCH, he is about five feet seven or eight inches high, and very stout, speaks plain, and has a large scar on one of his cheeks. He has been lately seen on his way to the northeast of Cape Fear, I expect from what information I have had, he means to stay there.<BR>Any person that will apprehend and secure said negro, in gaol, so that I get him, or deliver him to me, shall receive Ten Pounds Reward.<BR>JOSIAH HOWARD.<BR>Onslow County, May 22.
