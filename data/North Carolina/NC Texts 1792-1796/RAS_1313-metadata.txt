Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD<BR>RUN AWAY from the subscriber, a mulatto fellow, by the name of GEORGE, formerly the property of William Rhar[?], Esq.[Esquire] he is pretty stout made, about twenty-four years old, five feet six or seven inches high, has a scar on his left arm occasioned by the stab of a knife; he is by trade a cooper, and being a pretty bright mulatto may attempt to pass as a free man.  It is probable he is lurking about the plantation of Samuel Johnston, Esq.[Esquire] in Pasquotank county, his mother and relations being there. The above reward and all reasonable expences will be given to any person taking him up and delivering him to me.<BR>H. MURFREE<BR>State of North Carolina, Hertford<BR>County, Murfreesborough, 7th<BR>March, 1795.
