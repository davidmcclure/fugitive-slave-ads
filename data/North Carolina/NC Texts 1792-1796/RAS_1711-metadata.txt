Transcript: RUN-AWAY from the subscriber, on the 1st instant, a Negro Fellow, called MARCUS, of a black complection, about 30 years old, has lost some of his foreteeth; he has been marked with the switch, and he has been seen near Newbern, and is supposed to be lurking about that town.  I will give a Reward of five pounds, to any person who will deliver him to me. <BR>JOSEPH HATCH.<BR>April 26, 1794.
