Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN AWAY at Fayetteville, from the subscriber, about the 10th inst. a Negro-Fellow named NED, formerly the property of Mr. RICHARD BENBURY.  He is a short chunky fellow, about 5 feet 6 or 7 inches high; and appears, by his conversation at the time, to be foolish. The above reward will be given to any person who will secure him in gaol, so that I get him again.<BR>LAUGHLIN McCOY.<BR>Jan. 27, 1794.
