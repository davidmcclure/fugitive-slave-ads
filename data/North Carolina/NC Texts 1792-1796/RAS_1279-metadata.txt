Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN-away from the subscriber in Newbern, on the 10th May, a mulatto man named<BR>JIM,<BR>about twenty five years of age, of a sickly yellow complection; about five feet five or six inches high, straight tied hair, speaks good English. He had on when he went away a blue jacket, oznaburg trousers and a new hat, and in his hands a pair of men's shoes; it is probable he is lurking about Newbern, or gone to Washington. Whoever will apprehend said slave, or confine him so that I get him again, shall be entitled to the above reward.<BR>REUBEN BARNARD.<BR>N.B. All persons are forwarned from harbouring or concealing him, and masters of vessels from carrying him away, at their peril.<BR>R.B.<BR>May 23.
