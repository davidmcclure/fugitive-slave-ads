Slaveholder: Edward Witty
Transcript: THREE POUNDS REWARD.<BR> RAN away from the subscriber, a Negro fellow named NED alias DICK, about thirty years of age, of a yellowish complexion, about five feet or six inches high, country born and very talkative. <BR> He is missed since about a month, and is supposed to be lurking about in the neighbourhood or to have gone with Mr. Pollock's Negroes, toward Edenton.<BR>He had on when he went away, an almost new drab coloured coarse cloth coat, and stripped waistcoat--he some times wears boots.<BR>All masters of vessels are forwarned not to carry him away; and all persons from harbouring him. Whoever will deliver him to the subscriber, or so secure him, that he may be got again, shall be entitled to the above reward.<BR>EDWARD WITTY.<BR>Jones County, March 7.
