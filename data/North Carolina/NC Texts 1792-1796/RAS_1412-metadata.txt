Time period (decade): 1790-1799

Slave name(s): Bartlet
Slaveholder: Jacob Booe
Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, living in Rowan county, in the fork of the Yadkin, a Negro Fellow, about 21 years of age, by the name of Bartlet; he is about 6 feet high, very black, surly look, and not very talkative, when questioned looks down : He carried with him an old green shag jacket, osnabrigs overhalls[sic], tow shirt and a small felt hat. though I expect he will alter his name and dress; he is not a very artful fellow.  I do expect that he will endeavour to make to Halifax county in Virginia.  The above reward will be given for delivering said fellow to me, or securing him in any gaol so that I get him again, together with all reasonable charges.<BR>October 19.  JACOB BOOE
