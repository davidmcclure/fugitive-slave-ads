Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN AWAY from the subscriber on the night of the 11th of this instant, a negro man named JACK, (commonly called Jack Dennis) he is about five feet nine or ten inches high, straight and well made, though slender, he speaks commonly as if he had a bad cold; took with him a grey loose coat much worn, also a short coat made of green fluff, and is about thirty-seven years of age.  Whoever brings the said negro to me at Broad Creek, or confines him so that I get him, shall receive the above reward and all reasonable charges.<BR>READING BLOUNT.<BR>March 12th, 1793.
