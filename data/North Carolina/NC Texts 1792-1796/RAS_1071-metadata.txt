Headline: Five dollars reward
Publishing newspaper: Wilmington, N.C. : The Wilmington Chronicle, and North Carolina Weekly Advertiser
Date of advertisement: 1795-09-24
Page: 3
Time period (decade): 1790-1799
Person placing ad: Peter Maxwell
Slave name(s): Ben
Slaveholder: Peter Maxwell
Transcript: FIVE DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>   RAN-AWAY, about six weeks since, from the subscriber, at the Sound, a Negro, named BEN; well known in and about the neighbourhood of Wilmington. He is about nineteen or twenty years of age; five feet four or five inches high; stout and well made.--The above reward will be paid to any person or persons delivering the above named Negro at my house on the Sound, or secruing him in Wilmington goal. --Suspicions having arisen that he has been harboured by some evil-disposed person, whoever shall be found guilty thereof, will be prosecuted to the full extent of the law. <BR> PETER MAXWELL. Sept. 23, 1795.
