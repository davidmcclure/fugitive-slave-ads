Transcript: THIRTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>WILL be paid to any person who will deliver to the subscriber in Georgetown, a [illegible]<BR> SERVANT WOMAN,<BR>NAMED--Nancy Oxindine, <BR>she is a stout wench, of a light complexion, and appears to be about 30 years of age; it is uncertain what kind of clothes she may ware [sic] as she took a number with her.-- It is supposed she has been entic'd away by her brother and sister, who were here in February last--she probably is with one of them: the latter lives in Fayetteville, the former in a distant part of the state. <BR> The above reward will be paid for her and at the rate of ten dollars for every hundred miles she may be, beyond Fayetteville.<BR>JAMES DUFFEL.<BR>Georgetown South-Carolna, June 4, 1795.
