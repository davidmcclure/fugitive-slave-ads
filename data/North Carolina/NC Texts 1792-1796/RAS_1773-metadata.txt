Transcript: SEVEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN away from the subscriber, a Negro Fellow, named DAVY, about five feet three inches high.  He has a large eye, white spots in his face, and one on the right side of his under lip, and a very long flat nose.  The above reward will be paid to any person apprehending and delivering to me said Negro.<BR>STEPHEN CABARRUS.<BR>September 22, 1794.
