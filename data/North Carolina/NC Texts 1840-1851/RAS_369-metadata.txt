Transcript: $25 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on Wednesday night the 30th of September, from Noah Partee's, 10 miles South of Salisbury, a Negro man named DAVY, a tall black fellow, about six feet high, his front teeth out, and rather modest in his conversation--Davy is a very good fiddler, and took his violin with him.--The above reward will be given for the arrest and delivery of said negro in some safe Jail, so that I get him again.<BR>ROBERT HUIE.<BR>I appoint Charles L. Partee at Concord, my Agent.<BR>R. H.<BR>October 5, 1840.--tf11
