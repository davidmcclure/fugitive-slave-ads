Transcript: WILL be given for the apprehension of a Negro Man by the name of DICK, formerly belonging to Amos Alexander, dec'd. He is lurking about in Philadelphia and Sugar Creek congregations and about the Catawba river near Beaties Ford. This boy runaway about Agust, 1847, and has been lurking about ever since. The above reward will be paid to any one who will secure him in any jail so I can get him. <br></br>MARG'T D. HENDERSON<br></br>Charlotte, January 1, 1849.
