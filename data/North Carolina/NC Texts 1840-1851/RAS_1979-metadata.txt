He is probably lurking about this place, but having acquaintances in the town of Washington and in Hyde County, he may perhaps be lurking in the neighborhood of one of those places.<BR>The above reward will be paid for his delivery to the subscribers, or for securing him in jail in Newbern.<BR>BLACKWELL & HANKS.<BR>August 1st 1840.
