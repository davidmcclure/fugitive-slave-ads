Transcript: $20 REWARD.<BR>I WILL pay the above reward to any person who will apprehend and deliver to me, or confine in any jail so that I get her, a negro woman called JINNY, or Jane. She is tall and likely, rather slender, with narrow face of a bright colour and high cheek bones. Any farther description is deemed unnecessary, as she is generally known hereabouts, and is probably lurking in or about town.<BR>D. M. VAN BOKKELLEN.<BR>24th Oct. 1840.
