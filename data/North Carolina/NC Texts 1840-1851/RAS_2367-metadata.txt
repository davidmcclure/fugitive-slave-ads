Transcript: RANAWAY on the way to the Railroad, in the neighborhood of Mason's Ferry on the 19th of September, two colored boys, George and Gus. The above reward will be paid for the apprehension and delivery of both of them to the subscriber, or to any jailor, so that I can get them again, or $10 for either one of the them. <br></br>DESCRIPTION<br></br>GEORGE is about 5 feet 10 inches high, quite black and very broad faced and thick lipped.<br></br>GUS is of light copper color, thick lips and rather delicate make.<br></br>It is supposed they are lurking in either Steel Creek, N.C. or Bethel, York, S.C. Congregation, as they have relationships in both sections.<br></br>MARGARET GRIER<br></br>10 Miles West of Charlotte.<br></br>October 8, 1851              42tf
