Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 22nd instant, negro man SAM. He is about 5 feet 4 or 5 inches high, tolerably black, has lost some of his fore teeth, and is about 26 years old. He formerly had a wife at Dr. Gallagher's when living on Durham's creek, and is very likely in the town of Washington.<BR>The above reward will be given for the delivery of Sam to me, or for securing him in jail so that I get him. All persons are cautioned against harbouring, employing or carrying said negro away under the penalty of the law.<BR>ROBERT TRIPP.<BR>Beaufort county; June 24, 1840--01
