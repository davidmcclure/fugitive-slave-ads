Slave name(s): Simon;Dinah
Slaveholder: Joseph Green
Former slaveholder: Simmons Isler
Transcript: $50 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, on the 2nd Monday of March last, a negro man named SIMON, and his wife DINAH, both purchased at the sale of negroes belonging to the estate of Simmons Isler, decd. of Jones county. Simon is about 43 years of age, 5 feet 6 inches high, stout made, and black complexion. Dinah is about 33 years of old, 5 feet 3 or 4 inches high, of slender form and very complexion. The above reward will be paid for the delivery of the above-named negro man to me, or for securing him in jail so that I get him. If he should not be apprehended by the 15th of November next, he will then be legally outlawed. All persons are forewarned from harbouring, employing or carrying away either of the above-named negroes, under the penalty of the law.<BR>JOSEPH GREEN.<BR>24th Oct. 1840.
