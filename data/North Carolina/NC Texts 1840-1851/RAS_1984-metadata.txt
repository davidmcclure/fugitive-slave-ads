Transcript: $20 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, a few days since, two negro women one named LETTICE,<BR>aged about 26 years, about 5 feet, 4 or 5 inches high, stout made, and yellow complexion, formerly the property of William Brower dec'd., of this place, the other named<BR>PHILLIS, about 22 years of age 5 feet 5 or 6 inches high, yellow complexion, and formerly the property of Washington Venters, of Pitt county. Lettice is probably lurking about Newbern, and Phillis in the neighbourhood of Swift Creek. The above reward will be paid for securing them in jail, or Ten Dollars for either of them.<BR>GEORGE W. BARNES<BR>By WILLIAM P. MARSHALL.
