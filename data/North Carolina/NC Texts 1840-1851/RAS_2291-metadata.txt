Slave name(s): Kent
Slaveholder: Richard C. Richardson
Transcript: RANAWAY from the subscriber about the 1st of November inst., a negro boy named KENT. He is a likely black boy, light made, about 5 feet 6 or 7 inches high, has good teeth, but rather sharp and pointed, is a smart spoken and good tempered boy, and has a remarkably high instep for a real negro as he is. I have reason to believe he will try to make his way to some of the Free States. The above reward will be paid upon his being lodged in Jail so that I get him.<br></br>RICHARD C. RICHARDSON.<br></br>Fulton, Sumter Dist. S.C. Nov., 29, 1847.
