Transcript: $10 REWARD.--Runaway from the subscriber about the 17th Oct. 1839, my negro woman China. She is between 45 and 50 years of age; has two cancer scars on her face: low, and corpulent in person. She is supposed to be lurking about Chatham county. The above reward will be given to any person that will deliver said woman to Jacob Mayned or Ashwell McGee, or any of Jacob Mayned's sons.<BR>JACOB MAYNED.<BR>Wake County, Nov. 18th, 1840     316-3tp
