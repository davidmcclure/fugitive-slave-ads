Transcript: NOTICE. <BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 6th of the present month, a Negro Woman named HANNAH. She is about five feet high and very black. She has lost one of her upper fore teeth, and has a remarkably round face. I will pay ten dollars for Hannah if taken in this State and delivered to me or confined in any jail in the State, and twenty dollars if taken out of the State. <BR>  JAMES BUNN, <BR>  Near Wakefield. <BR> Sept. 30, 1840.--80tf.<BR>Standard till forbid.
