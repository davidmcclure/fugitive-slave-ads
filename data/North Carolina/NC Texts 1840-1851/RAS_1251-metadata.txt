Transcript: Five Dollars Reward.<BR>Ranaway from the subscriber on the 16th July (inst.) a negro man named AARON, the property of Caleb Nelson, of Pitt county. Said negro is between 25 and 30 years of age, about 5 feet high, black complexion; thin face, had on when he ranaway a black fur hat, high crown and narrow brim, a close-bodied black yarn coat and tow trowsers.<BR>All persons are hereby cautioned against harbouring, employing or carrying said negro away under the penalty of the law.<BR>The above reward will be given to any person who will deliver said negro to me or secure him in jail so that I get him.<BR>LEWIS GALLAWAY.<BR>Pitt county, July 20, 1840--04 05pd
