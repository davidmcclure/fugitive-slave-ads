Transcript: Thirty Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, living in Iredell county, on the night of the 11th inst. a Negro fellow named JACK, 34 years of age, very large and strong, no particular marks are recollected, except that he has lost some of his fore-teeth-Had on when he went away a pair of leather breeches and coat, with him some mixt homespun cloth, which he will probably exchange. The above reward will be given to any person who will deliver him to me, or secure him in some jail that I may get him again.<BR>DAVID ALEXANDER, B. Smith.<BR>May 13, 1813.
