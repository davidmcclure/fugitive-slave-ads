Transcript: Stop the Runaway.<BR>ELOPED from the subscriber, living in Chatham county, on the 9th inst. a Negro Fellow by the name of CHARLES, about twenty years old, 5 feet 6 inches high, not very dark complected, handsome made, considerable white in his eyes--He carried away a white coat &  a white pair of pantaloons, a mixt coat and a cotton shirt, a strip westcoat, and a furred hat. I think he will aim for Norfolk, Vir. by Raleigh, Roanoak at Roulhack's --Any person taking up said fellow and securing him in jail, so that I get him again, shall receive a reasonable reward.<BR>JOHN SILER, June 10th, 1813.
