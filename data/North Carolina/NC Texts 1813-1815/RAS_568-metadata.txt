Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward. <BR> Ranaway from the Subscriber on the 1st of January last, a Negro Woman named <BR> SARAH, formerly the property of John Pendleton, of Pasquotank County. She is about 30 years of age, remarkably stout for a woman, and is supposed to be lurking about Job Cartwright's of Pendelton's, the former of whom I have no doubt, harbors her. Her mother lives at Mrs. Warner's, where she in all probability, sometimes resorts. I will given the above reward and all reasonable expences to any person who deliver her to me, or confine her in jail so that I get her again. <BR> Edward C. Outlaw, <BR> Bertie County, April 3d, 1813. tf 371
