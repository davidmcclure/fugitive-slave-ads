Time period (decade): 1810-1819

Date of departure: 1813-03-04
County of residence: Chatham County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Allen
Slaveholder: George Barbee
Transcript: Twenty-five Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Susbscriber, on the 4th of March last, a Negro man named ALLEN, near thirty years old, 5 feet 9 or 10 high, black complexion, and when in the act of laughing, squints much; has a scar on one of his arms, occasioned by the white-swelling; he is trim made, active, pretty sensible and artful; took with him a quantity of clothing; had on a pair of bright top boots; and took with him also a new Dutch blanket. The above reward will be given for the delivery of the said Negro to William O'Kelly, Esq. of Chatham county, whom I have left as my agent, having myself recently lodged in jail, so that I get him again.<BR>GEORGE BARBEE.<BR>April 3.
