Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward. <BR> RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 24th of March, a bright yellow Man, about 28 years old, six feet high, well proportioned and active, speaks slow, has lost one of his foreteeth, and a piece of the lower part of his left ear cut off.-This fellow no doubt will attempt to pass for a free man, or get in with some person to take him off. I will give the above reward to any person who will bring the said fellow to me, or secure him in any jail so that I get him again. <BR> THOMAS ALSTON. <BR> Wake county, N.C. 7 miles east of the Falls of Neuse river, March 22, 1814 }
