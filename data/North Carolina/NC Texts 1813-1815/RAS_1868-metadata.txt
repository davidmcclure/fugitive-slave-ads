F. Smith Esq. and it is supposed she is lurking in the neighborhood of the Town or about Wilkinson's Point. The above reward with reasonable charges will be paid on her being delivered to me or in Jail, so that I get her. <BR>JOHN MERRIT. <BR>Newbern, June 11th, 1814.--285--tf
