Transcript: RANAWAY from Jacob Hadley on the 20th of November last, a Mulatto named JIM 21 years old, five feet, seven or eight inches high had on when he went off a black fur hat, and a blue mixed coat, and black velvet pantaloons, also a horse with lock and chain on his legs.  The said Jim belongs to the heirs of Wm.[William] Ransay, dec.[deceased] ten dollars will be given to any person who will give information where the said Jim is, so as he may be come at[?].<BR>JESSE MEACHAM.<BR>Chatham county, Jan. 24.
