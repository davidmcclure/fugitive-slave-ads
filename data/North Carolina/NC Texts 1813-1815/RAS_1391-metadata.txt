Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from my plantation on Monday the 25th April, a negro man named Allen, about 30 years old, stout made, dark complexion, thick lips, stoops when he walks --He has a wife at Capt. D. Blake's on Crabtree--He was raised near Halifax. I will give the above reward for said negro delivered to me or lodged in any jail.<BR>W. BOYLAN.
