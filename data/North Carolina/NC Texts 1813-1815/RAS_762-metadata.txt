Transcript: 50 Dollars Reward <BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber on Sunday 30th inst. two negro men, named STEPHEN and JIM.--The former aged about 20 years, low and stout built, the latter nearly the same age, though he appears not more than sixteen.--I will give the above reward for satisfactory proof of both being killed, or twenty-five dollars for the same proof of either. Otherwise I will give Ten Dollars for each of them alive delivered to me, or secured in any Jail in the adjoining Counties, so that I get them. <BR>All persons are forwarned from harbouring, employing or carrying them off, under penalty of the law. <BR>Said Negroes are legally outlawed. <BR>LEWIS BRYAN. <BR>Lenoir County, May 31st 1813.--tf.
