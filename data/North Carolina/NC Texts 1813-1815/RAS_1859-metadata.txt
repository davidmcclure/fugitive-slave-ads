Headline: Ten dollars reward

Time period (decade): 1810-1819

Date of departure: 1814-11-21
Slave name(s): Nelly
Slaveholder: William P. Moore
Former slaveholder: John M.P. Crispin
Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RAN-AWAY from the Subscriber on the 21st. inst. a negro Woman named NELLY, formerly owned by Mr. John M. P. Crispin of this place, she is about 45 years of age, dark complexion. All persons are forwarned from harbouring, employing or carrying her out of the State, under the penalty of the law. <BR>I will give the above reward for her to be delivered to me or secured in jail so that I get her, and all reasonable expences shall be paid. <BR>WM.[William] P. MOORE.
