Transcript: 6. Dollars Reward. <BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 28th. ult. A Negro man by the name of <BR>JACOB. <BR>About 25 years of age, slender built, very black, 5 feet 6 or 7 Inches high, he on when he went away a white Cotton Shirt and Trowsers, he is much accustomed going up the Rivers & Creeks,  in Boats and Flats. The above reward will be given any Person apprehending, and delivering him to me, or secure him in Jail so that I get him, and all reasonable expences paid. <BR>All persons are forbid harboring, employing, or carrying him away, under the penalty of the law. <BR>WILLIAM GOOD. <BR>Newbern, Sept. 3d. 1814.
