Time period (decade): 1810-1819

Date of departure: 1814-06-08
County of residence: Wake County (N.C.)
Presumed or intended destination: Johnston County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Duke
Slaveholder: Simon Turner
Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward. <BR> RANAWAY from the subscriber the 8th inst. a likely Negro Man named DUKE, 20 years of age, about 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, has a pleasing countenance, small eyes, his nose not as flat as common Negroes, has been raised a waiter, is smart and active, had on when he left me homespun clothes, his jacket white, made of cotton and wool, had a surtout coat of cloth much worn and dark coloured with a velvet cape. It is probable he is now lurking in Johnston county. The above reward will be given to any person who will deliver him to the subscriber, in Wake county, ten miles south of Raleigh, or ten dollars for securing him in jail so that I get him again. <BR> SIMON TURNER. <BR> June 9th.   25
