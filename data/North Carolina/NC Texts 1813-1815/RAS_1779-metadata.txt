Transcript: Sixty Dollars Reward. <BR>RAN-AWAY from the Subscriber, at his Plantation, on the 1st of April, three Negro Men, viz. <BR>LONDON,<BR>about five feet ten inches high, yellow complexion; HERCULOUS, a low but well set fellow, very black; ELIAS, a boy just grown, dark complexion, has a lump under his left ear resembling a wen. A description of their dress is deemed unnecessary, as they took several articles of clothing with them, LONDON is a very artful fellow and no doubt will be lurking about Newbern.<BR>I will give the above reward of SIXTY DOLLARS on the delivery of the above named Negroes at my Plantation on Trent, (JONES COUNTY,) or on their being secured in jail, so that I get them; or TWENTY DOLLARS for each. <BR>Masters of Vessels, and all others are forwarned from harbouring, employing or carrying them away, under the penalty of the law. <BR>JOSIAH HOWARD.<BR>Jones County, April 7th, 1814.--tf.
