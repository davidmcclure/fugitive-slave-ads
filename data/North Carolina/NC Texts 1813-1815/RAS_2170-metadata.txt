Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.  <BR>  RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 6th day of August last, a large negro fellow about six feet high by the name of DENCEY, it is supposed he is now lurking about in the lower end of this county. I will give the above reward of ten dollars to any persons that will deliver said Dencey to me (six miles below Hillsboro') and reasonable expences paid.  <BR>   JOHN LATTA.  <BR>   Orange County, 15, 1814.
