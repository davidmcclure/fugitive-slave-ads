Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN AWAY from the Subscriber, living 16 miles west of Hillsborough, on the 17th instant, a NEGRO MAN, named Stephen, about 21 years of age, 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, very dark, full faced, has an impediment in his speech when spoken to.  Had on when he went away, a flax shirt, tow pantaloons and striped waistcoat.<BR>It is probable he may aim for some of the seaport towns.  Any person apprehending the said Negro, so that I get him, shall be entitled to the above Reward.<BR>JOSEPH STEEL.<BR>Orange County, Aug. 19.
