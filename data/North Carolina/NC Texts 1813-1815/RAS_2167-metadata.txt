Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber a few months ago, a Negro man called JOSHUA, about five feet ten inches high, round shoulders, and yellowish complected, between twenty and thirty years of age. He was raised in Essex co. Virginia, and carried from thence to Sneedsborough, by Mr. Lee, dec. and since sold by a Mr. Peter Benford, to me in this place. It is supposed that he apprehending the said fellow, and lodging him in any Jail, & giving me notice so that I get him again, shall be entitled to the above reward, and if brought to me, all reasonable charges paid.<BR>JAMES W. ENGLAND.<BR>Fayetteville, July 12th, 1813.
