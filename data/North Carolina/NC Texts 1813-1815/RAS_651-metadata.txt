Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN AWAY from the Subscriber, on the fifteenth instant, a NEGRO MAN by the name of Watt, about six feet high and very stout made ; dark complexion, open pleasing countenance, and a very bandy fellow on a farm.  Said Negro is expected to aim for West Tennessee, as he has made two attempts to get there before. The above reward will be given to any person who shall secure said fellow in any Jail, so that I get him again.<BR>JOHN HAMILTON,<BR>Guilford County, N. Carolina.<BR>June 26.
