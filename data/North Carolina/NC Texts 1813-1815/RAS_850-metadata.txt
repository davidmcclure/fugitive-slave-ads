Transcript: Fifteen Dollars Reward. <BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber on the 3d of last October, in Jones County, near Trent Bridge, my negro woman <BR>CLARRY. <BR>Formerly the property of Elizabeth Bray; she was hired in Newbern by Donum Mumford last year--I have good information that she is now lurking about there, she is of a dark complexion, tall and stout made. The above reward will be given to any person delivering said Negro to me, or lodge her in any jail so that I get her again, and all reasoanble expences paid. <BR>All persons are forwarned harboring, employing, or carrying said Negro out of the State, under the penalty of the law. <BR>JOHN SANDERSON, <BR>Mill Creek, Jones County, April 30th, 1814. <BR>Mr. Hall, <BR>As the FELLOW who signed the above has no right or title to the above named negro, CLARRY: I forbid any person from molesting said negro, or her CHILDREN; she has a right to maintain herself and children, until I call on her. <BR>NICHOLAS A. BRAY.
