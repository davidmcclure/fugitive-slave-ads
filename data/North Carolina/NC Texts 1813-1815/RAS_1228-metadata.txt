Transcript: Five Dollars Reward.<BR> RANAWAY on Monday the 9th ult. from the subscriber, living on Buffaloes Shoal, 4 miles from the Island ford, Catawba river, a negro man named DERRY, about 25 years of age, and about 6 feet high, walks very straight, has a remarkable scar on the instep of one of his feet--it is supposed he may have obtained a pass. Whoever will apprehend and secure said negro in any jail, shall receive the above reward, by sending information to<BR>JAMES MORRISON, Black Smith.<BR> Buffaloe Shoal, Iredell,}<BR> August 27, 1813.}
