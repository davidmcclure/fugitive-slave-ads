Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY on the 15th November from the subscriber, JERRY, a Negro Man about 30 years old, 5 feet 11 inches high, queues his hair, which is 3 or 4 inches long, and has two platts of the same length hanging down from the forepart of his head, and has lost one of his fore teeth, I believe. He was born in Johnston county, and sold by a Mr. Jonnston to a Mr. Fish, who sold him to Philemon Hodges, of Cumberland county, from whom I purchased him. The above reward will be given for apprehending and delivering him to me at Raleigh, or ten dollars for securing him in any jail so that I get him.<BR>WILLIAM POLK.<BR>December 15, 1813<BR>51 tf
