Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN AWAY from the Subscriber, on the first instant, a BLACK MAN, named Walker, about 27 years of age, 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, well made, rather slender, bony face.  He has, as well as I recollect, a small scar over his left eye, on his forehead, extending into the hair on his head.  I will give the above reward to any person who will deliver said Negro to me in Chatham county, on Deep River, N. Carolina, or lodge him in any Jail in this State, so that I get him again.<BR>THO[Thomas] PARISH.<BR>March 20, 1813.    3m
