Transcript: 10 Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber about the 1st August last a negro man by the name of AFFELLOW, about 18 or 20 years old; he had on when he went off a brown broad cloth coat, white homespun trowsers[sic] and an old black chip hat; he was seen lately in Wilmington, and is not doubt lurking about there yet. Whoever will apprehend said negro and bring him to me or lodge him in any jail where he can be found, shall have the above reward and all reasonable expences paid.<BR>All masters of vessels and others are cautioned not to harbor or carry away the said runaway, as the penalties of the law shall be enforced with the utmost rigor against whoever shall be detected in an act so unjustifiable, illegal and base.<BR> JOHN KELLY.<BR>September 25--3t
