Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RANAWAY <BR>FROM the Plantation of the Subscriber in Wake, a negro girl by the name of PAT<BR>She is of a very dark complexion & is about the prime of life.--She will probably lurk about the Plantations, of the Mr. Hatch's on Trent, as she was formerly of the estate of Lemuel Hatch, dec'd. the above reward will be given to any person who may apprehend and confine her in Gaol, or otherwise so that I get her again. <BR>All persons are forwarned from harbouring, employing or carrying her away. <BR>WILLIAM H. HILL.<BR>July 23d, 1814.
