Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward<BR>FOR the deliver and securing in any jail the following Negroes, or Ten Dollars for either of them, viz: a Negro Man of the name BEN, about 35 years of age, 5 feet 7 or 8 inches high, with a scar on his right hand occassioned by a cut : and a Negro Woman of the name Alice, about the same age.  The Man formerly belonged to John Critchlow of Southampton County, Va. and the Woman to Lewis P. Hart, of the same county.<BR>The Negroes ranaway on the 23d of June, and she supposed to be gone towards their former home.<BR>ELISHA BROWN.<BR>Caswell county, July 12, 1814.    73
