Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward. <BR> RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 28th inst. a mulatto boy about eighteen years of age named JOHN, has a blemish in one of his eyes, is about five feet four or five inches high, has a mark on one of his shoulders, has some marks of the whip on his back had on when he went away a suit of mixed homespun cloth. The above reward will be given to any person who will bring the said slave to me or secure him in any Jail within this State, so that I get him again. <BR> THOMAS CARSON, Jr. <BR> Pitt County, November 30, 1813. 59-3tp
