Transcript: RANAWAY,<BR>ON the 28th December last, from the Subscriber, living in Greenville county (N. Carolina) a very light colored slave named BILL.  He will no doubt endeavour to pass for a free man, and take another name-- He is 37 years old, about six feet high, brown hair ; beard thin and rather light colored ; old complexion, large knees and ancles, bones small ; legs bowed, has in his right thigh a little above the knee, a brown spot, and a sear on his left leg, cut with a scythe, can read print tolerably well : his voice rather hoarse : can make coarse shoes and is handy at any labor.  it is thought he will endeavor to get to the British, or into one free State.<BR>All persons are hereby forewarded from harboring said Fellow, and any person that delivers him to me shall have half what he sells for when sold,(it is thought he will sell for 400 dollars) or Twenty Dollars for confining him in Jail so that I get him again.<BR>JOHN HOWARD.<BR>January 15.
