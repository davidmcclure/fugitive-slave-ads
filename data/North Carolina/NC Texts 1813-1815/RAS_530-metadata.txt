Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber's plantation on the 27th ult. a negro fellow called FRANK or FRANCIS, belonging to the estate of the late John Poisson, Esq. He is well known about Wilmington and the rice plantation in its vicinity, where it is supposed he will be harboured. The above reward will be paid for the apprehension of said fellow, or on delivering him to the keeper of the jail in Wilmington.<BR>JOHN GARNIER.<BR>April 5.---tf
