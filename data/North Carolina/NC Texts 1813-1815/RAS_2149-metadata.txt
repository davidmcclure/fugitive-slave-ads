Transcript: Stop the Runaways!!! <BR>  RUNAWAY from the subscriber a Negro man by the name of BEN, about 30 years old, 6 feet 8 or 10 inches high, very black, the end of his middle finger bit off, and one of his toes strait and stands down. Also his wife SALLY, a mulatto wench and two Children, both girls, about one and two years old.  They were raised in Johnston county. Any person apprehending the said negroes & confining them in any Jail so that I get them again shall receive satisfaction for their trouble and all reasonable expences paid. <BR>  CADER COLLINS. <BR>  Cumberland county, N.C.
