Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward. <BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber, on the 29th of March, two negro boys, one named JOHN, the other ANDREW. JOHN is black, about four feet eight on[or] ten inches high, between twenty one and twenty two years of age, with a large hand and foot, rather swelled eyes, when he speaks, inclines to stutter, branded on the cheek with E.H.  ANDREW is a very light coloured Mulatto, about four feet high, fomerly belonged to the estate of Mr. GORDON, and probably will lurk about Newbern and its vicinity. Ten dollars reward will be given for either of the above described negroes, or twenty dollars for both, delivered to me four miles above Newbern. All persons are forwarned from harbouring of said Negroes under the penalty of the Law. <BR>EDMUND HATCH. <BR>Craven County, April 1st, 1813.
