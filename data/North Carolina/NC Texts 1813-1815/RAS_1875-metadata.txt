Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RANAWAY<BR>FROM the Subscribers Plantation on the 20th of July, my Negro Man, <BR>JOHN, <BR>About twenty-five years of age, between five and six feet high, of a yellow complexion, stout and well made, with the letters E.H. on his cheek: he is very artful and cunning, and I think he may try to get up the Country. He had on when he went away Cotton Homespun Clothes; any person who will apprehend said fellow, shall receive the above reward, and all reasonable expences paid if secured in any Jail so that I get him. <BR>EDMUND HATCH.<BR>Craven County, August 4th, 1814.
