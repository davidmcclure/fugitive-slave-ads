Transcript: RUNAWAY<BR>From the Subscribers on the 30th of September last, two Negro Fellows named Croatan and Merica.<BR>CROATAN<BR>Is about 30 years of age, six feet high, very black, with a good set of teeth, and a very large flat nose; one of his eyes, (believed to be the left) has been injured which he keeps about half shut.<BR>MERICA<BR>Is a well built fellow, about 30 years old, five feet six inches high, black, with a good set of teeth, sharp nose and mouth; no private mark recollected.<BR>Any person who will apprehend said negroes and confine them in jail so the owners get them again, shall receive FIFTY DOLLARS, or TWENTY-FIVE for either.<BR>Daniel Lindsay, jr.<BR>Thos.[Thomas] Jarvis, jr.<BR>Powel's Point, Currituck County, N.C.<BR>October 7th, 1826.
