Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>Ran away from the subscriber, about the 10th of November last, a negro man, by the name of LEWIS, of a tolerable dark complexion, about 38 years of age, about five feet eight or nine inches high; carried off, and wore away common homespun negro clothing; has a scar on the right side of his nose, which runs down quite through his lip. He is supposed to be lurking in the neighborhood of Haw Landing, in Greene county.<BR>Should any person apprehend and secure said fellow, so that I get him, I will give the above reward and pay Jail expenses.<BR>JAMES ALBRITTON.<BR>Greene county, Jan. 10, 1827.<BR>3-3t
