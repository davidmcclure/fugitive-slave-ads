Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS REWARD<BR>WILL be given for the apprehension of negro WESTON. He has been runaway nearly twelve months; has been repeatedly seen in the neighborhood of Raleigh, and is a dark mulatto, about 24 or 25 years of age, 5 feet 4 or 5 inches high, stout and well made.  He goes constantly armed with a gun.  The above reward will be given on his being delivered to me, or being lodged in the Gaol in Raleigh.<BR>GEO.[GEORGE] W. MORDECAI.<BR>April 18th, 1827.    5
