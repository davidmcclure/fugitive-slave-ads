Slave name(s): Isaac; Lucy
Slaveholder: Edmund Hatch
Former slaveholder: George C. Hatch
Transcript: Sixty Dollars Reward.<BR>THE above reward will be given to any person that will deliver to me, ISAAC, AND LUCY his wife, or secure them in jail so that I get them.  Isaac is a bright mulatto, about five feet 7 or 8 inches high, between twenty-five and thirty years of age, formerly belonged to Mr. George C. Hatch, and has a father living near Newbern, on a place belonging to Durant Hatch, jr. Lucy is a small black woman, with rather large eyes, and has a mother living with William S. Blackledge, Esq.[Esquire] in Craven county.  I will give fifty dollars for Isaac alone, and ten for Lucy; and will not hold any person accountable that may cripple or kill Isaac in taking him.<BR>EDMUND HATCH.<BR>Jones County, Oct.[October] 12, 1826.--'47tf
