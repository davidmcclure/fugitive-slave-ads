Transcript: Thirty Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Plantation of the Subscriber, in Jones county on the 29th inst. a negro man named CATO; about 34 years of age, six feet one inch high, dark complexion; has a downcast look--& remarkably large feet. He is probably lurking in the neighbourhood of Daniel Simmons, on South West or on Cypress Creek, or about Trenton.  In a former attempt to runaway, he made for the plantation of Mr. Ezekiel Lane, on Rocky Point, near Wilmington, where he has a brother.<BR>The above Reward will be given, and all reasonable expenses paid, to any person who will deliver said negro to me or confine him in jail so that I get him again.<BR>BRYAN BECTON<BR>Jones County, August 30, 1826--41tf.
