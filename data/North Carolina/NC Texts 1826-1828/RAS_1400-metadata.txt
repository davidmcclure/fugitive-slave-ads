Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 1st of January last, my Negro Man CAESAR, about 20 years of age, 5 feet high, yellow complexion, and thick set.  It is supposed he is lurking about Newbern.  The above reward and reasonable expenses will be paid to any one who will apprehend him, and secure him in jail, so that I get him.  All persons are forewarned under the penalty of the law, from harboring or employing said negro.<BR>DANIEL TOLSON.<BR>February 24--66tf
