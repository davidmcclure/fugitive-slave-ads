Time period (decade): 1820-1829

Slave name(s): Bob
Slaveholder: Seymore Puyear
Transcript: $20 Reward.<BR>Ranaway from the Subscriber on the 18th inst. a Bright Mulatto fellow, by the Name of Bob, about 28 years of age, 5 feet 8 inches high, stout made very active, and quick spoken, he is very freckle for a Negro, he had on when he went away a White hat, Brown pantaloons, drab coloured home made Coat, and a drab big coat with a large cape, he had also a double eased silver Watch marked Norton, London, N. 334 with D. Scotts Watch bill, in the case.<BR> The above reward will be paid to any person who will take up said fellow and deliver him to the subscriber in Orange county or to the Jailer of Guilford county--or ten dollars if secured in any other jail so that I get immediate information.<BR>SEYMORE PURYEAR.<BR>Dec 27 1826.--36<BR>The Raleigh Register will publish the above three times and forward their account to this office.
