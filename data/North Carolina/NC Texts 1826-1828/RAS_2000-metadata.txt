Transcript: Five Dollars Reward.<BR>Runaway from the subscriber on the night of the 17th of June last, negro girl<BR>HARRIET,<BR>belonging to John Davis.--She is supposed to be lurking in the neighborhood of Nixonton, or Newbegun Creek, where she has a mother living--she is a bright, mulatto, 19 years old, of an audacious impudent temper, slothful and dirty habits--but notwithstanding all these qualities, a great favorite of some people who call themselves gentlemen. The above reward will be given, when secured and delivered to me in Elizabeth-City.<BR>T. E. Krozer.<BR>Elizabeth-City, July 7.<BR>Bt
