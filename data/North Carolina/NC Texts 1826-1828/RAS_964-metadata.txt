Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD! <BR> RAN away from the plantation of Mrs. Trotter, near Salisbury, SAM, a bright mulatto boy, about 30 years of age, and near six feet high. He is stout, and well made; has a down cast look, and a little inclined to stoop in the shoulders. The above reward  will be given for his apprehension and delivery to me, in Salisbury, or to the overseer on the plantation. <BR> THO.[Thomas] G. POLK. <BR> December 1st, 1827.  4t94<BR> The Raleigh Star and North Carolina Journal, will insert the above 4 weeks, and forward account for payment.  T.G.P.
