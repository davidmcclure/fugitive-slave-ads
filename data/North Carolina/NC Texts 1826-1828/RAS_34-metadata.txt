Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>A Servant of mine called LYDIA, ranaway from my plantation on Rocky Point, in New Hanover county, some time ago.  She formerly belonged to Mr. Lallerstedt, who lived on the No.[North] West, below Fayetteville, and is well known on that river.  I will give $10 for putting her in any jail, and giving me notice thereof, or $10 and expenses for delivering her at my plantation.<BR>C. WALKER.<BR>September 3, 1827.    35-3w.
