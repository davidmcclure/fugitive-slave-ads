Time period (decade): 1820-1829

Date of departure: 1827-02-13
County of residence: Anson County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Abram
Slaveholder: Samuel Tyson's son
Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward. <BR> RAN away from the subscriber's son, in Montgomery county, while taking him home from Concord jail, on Tuesday night, the 13th inst. a Negro Man named Abram, about 20 years of age, black skin, short, stout built, has a blemish in one of his eyes, a pretty large scar on his right arm, and is rather bold spoken. Any person who will take up said Subscriber, in Anson county, on the Salisbury road, about 9 miles from Wadesborough, and 4 1/2 from Beard's store, or lodge him in jail and give me information so that I can get him, shall be entitled to the above reward, and all reasonable charges paid. Any letter that may be written on the subject, can be directed to Beard's Store, Anson county. N.C.<BR> SAMEUL TYSON. <BR> Feb. 24. 1827.  2t52
