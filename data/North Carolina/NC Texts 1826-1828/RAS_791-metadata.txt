Transcript: 50 Dollars Reward<BR>For Cut-finger CAD.<BR>WHO ran away on the 15th instant.  He is a yellow fellow, of common size, his hair inclined to be straight, his foreteeth somewhat decayed, the forefinger of one of his hands off, (I believe the left,) quite intelligent, fond of drink, and great at the Odd-trick.<BR>He wore away a blue cassinet short coat, blue broadcloth waistcoat with yellow buttons, grey pantaloons, and linen shirt--all of which he may exchange, as he will no doubt attempt to pass as a free man, and is likely prepared with papers for the purpose.<BR>For the securing of him in any Jail within this State so that I get him again, I will pay twenty dollars, and if taken, secured, &c. out of this State, fifty dollars.<BR>JO.[Joseph] HAWKINS.<BR>Raleigh, N.C. Jan. 17, 1827.    3tf<BR>The Editors of the Fayetteville Observer, Wilmington, Edenton and Newbern papers, will insert the above three times and forward their accounts.
