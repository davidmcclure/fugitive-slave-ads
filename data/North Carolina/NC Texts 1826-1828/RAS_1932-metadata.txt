Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, in June last, a negro fellow, 38 years of age, 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, dark complexion, and has a scar, it is believed, on his breast. He is supposed to be harbored somewhere between Charlotte and my residence, as he has been frequently seen. Whoever will apprehend said negro and return him to me, near Tuckasege Ford, or give me information so I get him, or secure him in any jail, shall be entitled to the above reward.<BR>ROBERT WILSON.<BR>Nov. 27, 1827.--3t61r
