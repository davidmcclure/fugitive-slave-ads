Said negro is very black, has a scar on his left thumb, thick lips, quick spoken, low and well made.<BR>All persons are forbid employing or harboring him; and all masters of vessels are forewarned from carrying him off under the penalty of the law.<BR>Saml.[Samuel] Ferebee.<BR>January 13.
