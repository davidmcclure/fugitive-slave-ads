Time period (decade): 1820-1829

Slave name(s): Jim
Slaveholder: Alexander Nicholson, Sheriff
Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from Richmond County, N.C., a negro man named JIM, about 22 years of age, about 6 feet high, light complexion, slender make, flat nose, very large eyes, shewing[showing] more of the white than ordinary, unusually long hollow feet.  The above reward will be paid for his apprehension and confinement in any jail in the state, so that he is recovered, and all reasonable expenses paid for his delivery to the Subscriber in Fayetteville, or to his master, Alexander Nicholson, Sheriff of Richmond county.<BR>HUGH McLAURIN.<BR>September 20, 1827.    37-tt.
