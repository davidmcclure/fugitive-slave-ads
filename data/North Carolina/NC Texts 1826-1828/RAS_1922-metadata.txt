Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber on the 7th inst. a negro man, named OSTER, between 25 and 30 years of age, five feet 4 or 5 inches high, stout made, and of yellow complexion, bordering on the mulatto.  He has acquaintances in Newbern, on Swift and Broad Creeks, and on Pamlico.  All persons are cautioned from harbouring said runaway, and masters of vessels from carrying him out of the State.  The above reward, and reasonable expenses will be paid for his delivery to me, or for his confinement in Jail so that I get him.<BR>THOMAS GASKINS.<BR>Craven County, Oct.[October] 27.
