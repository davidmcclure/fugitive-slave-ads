Transcript: Five Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, a negro man named AARON--formerly the property of the heirs of the late Wm.[William] Shepard, Esq.[Esquire]--Aaron is of a yellow complexion, 5 feet, 6 or 7 inches high, and about 40 years of age.  It is supposed that he is lurking about Newbern --All persons are forwarned from harboring or employing said negro, and Captains of Vessels from carrying him out of the State, under the penalty of the law.<BR>GEORGE REID.<BR>June 9, 1827.--'81.
