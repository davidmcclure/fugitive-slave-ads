Dick is a tall slim fellow, and it is supposed he is lurking about the plantation of Mr. McDaniels, near Trent Bridge.--I will give a reward of Ten Dollars for either of them, if delivered in Newbern, or secured in any Jail so that I get them, and all reasonable expenses paid.<BR>GEO.[GEORGE] REID.<BR>August 12, 1826--38tf.
