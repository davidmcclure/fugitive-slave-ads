Transcript: Ranaway<BR>FROM the subscriber on the 12th inst. Negro Boy AARON, about 40 years old, dark complexion, lame in his knee or limps in walking, quite an intelligent fellow and talkative, was raised in the neighborhood of Petersburg, Virg. in the Spain family.  It is probable he may have gone thither or to Richmond county, New Rockingham, in this State, where he was hired a few years since, and was much attached to some persons in that vicinity.  He had on when he went off, striped pantaloons and drab over coat.<BR>Whoever will return the said negro or give information where he may be found, if within the State, shall receive a reward of Ten Dollars or if out of its limits, Twenty-five Dollars.<BR>WM.[WILLIAM] R. FOWLER.<BR>Wake county, Nov. 20.    19 tf
