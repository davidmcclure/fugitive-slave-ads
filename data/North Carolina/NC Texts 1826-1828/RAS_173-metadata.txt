Transcript: $10 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, on the 27th day of November last, a bright mulatto boy by the name of<BR>RICKEY MITCHELL,<BR>Between 19 and 20 years of age. I expect he has steered his course toward Halifax. I am told he has a brother by the name of Lewis, in the town of Halifax, learning the blacksmith trade. The above reward will be given if delivered to the Subscriber near Tarborough, or FIVE DOLLARS, if secured in any Jail in this State so that I get him again. All persons are hereby forbid harboring or employing said boy, under the penalty of the law.<BR>S.L. HART.<BR>Dec. 24, 1827.<BR>19
