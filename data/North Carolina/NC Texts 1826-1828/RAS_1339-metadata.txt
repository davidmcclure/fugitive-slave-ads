Transcript: Thirty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Plantation of the Subscriber, in February last, LILLY, a negro woman;--also, on Friday the 8th inst. AMERICA, a negro woman.  These women are lurking about Newbern and its vicinity, where they are sufficiently known without a description.<BR>I will give the above reward for the delivery of said women to me, or for such information as may enable me to get them:--or I will give Fifteen Dollars for either of them, or the necessary information to obtain them again.<BR>WM.[WILLIAM] S. BLACKLEDGE.<BR>Sep.[September] 26, 1826--45 48
