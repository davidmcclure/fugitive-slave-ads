Transcript: Notice.<BR>RAN OFF, some time in the month of December last, negro man JACOB, bought by Elias Carr of Hardie Flowers, Esq. some years since. He is supposed to be lurking either in the neighborhood of Tarborough, or of said Flowers. A liberal reward will be paid to any person who will either deliver said negro to the Subscriber, or lodge him in any jail so that he gets him.<BR>JONAS J. CARR.<BR>Edgecombe County, Jan. 31, 1828. 25
