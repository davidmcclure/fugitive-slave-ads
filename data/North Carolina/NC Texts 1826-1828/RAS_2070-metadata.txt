Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward.<BR>Ranaway from the Subscriber on the 27th June, Negro Jim.<BR>about six feet high, of a very black color, well made; has a wife at Mrs. Gardners in the Town of Edenton, and also one at Thos. [Thomas] Walker's Esq. in the county of Washington. The above reward will be paid for apprehending and securing him in Jail so that I get him or upon delivering him to me. All Masters of Vessels are warned not to carry him off, as he may probably attempt to go the Northern States.<BR>THOMAS JOHNSON.<BR>Washington County, July 15 1826.
