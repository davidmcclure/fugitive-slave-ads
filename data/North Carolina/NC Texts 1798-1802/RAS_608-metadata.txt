Transcript: Twenty-five Dollars<BR>WILL be given to any person who will apprehend and deliver to the Goaler in Wilmington, the Negro fellow ELIJAH, formerly advertised in this paper, having eloped from his employer--he has been harboured at John Telfair's plantation down the river, as well as in Wilmington----he has been up the Northwest river, at Catfish, among the negroes there and at Mr. Daniel Maller's[?] plantation, as also at other plantations in the neighbourhood.--He has lately been guilty of a crime for which he delivers to suffer capital punishment, and therefore the printers of Newbern and Halifax are requested to insert this advertisement one week in their papers. Masters of vessels are forbid carrying him off.<BR>John Burgwin.<BR>Wilmington, Oct. 21.
