Transcript: RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 29th of March, in Mecklenburg county, North-Carolina, a negro man named JACK, about six feet high, 35 or 40 years old, he has a scar on his left [illegible] occasioned by the tooth ache, he is an outlandish fellow and cannot speak good English.  He had on when he went away a blue coat, the sleeves patched with brown cloth, a blue jacket with no buttons, two pair of overalls, one pair of blue cloth, the other pair of course linen, a pair of light grey [illegible] stockings, a course shirt and an old [illegible] wool hat, likewise carried away with him a short linen coat.  Any person that will confine him in some jail, or bring him home, shall be handsomely rewarded by me.<BR>JOHN SIMMONS.<BR>April 6th.
