Transcript: Five Dollars Reward.<BR>ELOPED from my service a Negro Fellow named Manuel, well known in Wilmington, belonging to David Jones, Esq. but mortgaged to me. Whoever apprehends said fellow and delivers him to me or lodges him in the Goal at Wilmington, shall receive the above reward. He has been heretofore harboured by a Negro Woman belonging to Mrs. Heron, who he has for a wife, and was there last Sunday night.<BR>The same reward of Five Dollars will be given to any Negro who will inform who harbours him, to be paid on conviction.<BR>John Burgwin.<BR>January 8.
