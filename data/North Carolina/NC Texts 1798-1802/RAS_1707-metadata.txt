Transcript: RUN-AWAY from the subscriber, a French negro, name<BR>JOE,<BR>Taylor by trade, speaks broke English--branded on the right breast with his former masters name--the letters P. P.--about 25 or 26 years old. Had on when he went away a Blue Jacket and Canvass Trousers. All masters of vessels and other persons are forbid from harbouring him at their peril. Any person who will deliver him to me, or to the Goaler in Wilmington, shall receive a reward of Five Dollars.<BR>PETER WISS.
