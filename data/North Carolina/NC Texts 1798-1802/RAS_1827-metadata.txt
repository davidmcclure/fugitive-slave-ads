Transcript: Ten Dollar Reward. <BR> RAN AWAY from the subscriber, a negro Girl named MERIUM. <BR> She is about 16 or 17 years of age--very black.------She ranaway about 18 months ago, and then passed for a free person, near Trenton. I will give the above  reward for her delivery; or her being so secured that I get her again--All persons are forwarned from harbouring or employing, and Captains of vessles from carrying her away, at their peril.<BR> BENJ. BORDEN. <BR> Carteret County.
