Transcript: ABSCONDED from the subscriber, on the 29th of December last, two NEGRO SAILERS--one named JIM, about 32 years old, about 5 feet 10 inches high, slim made, had on when he went away, a blue jacket, purpple colored Bath coating trousers, a checked shirt, and a tarpoling hat-the other named WILL,a stout well made fllow, abut 5 feet 8 inches high, about 28 years old, of a yellowish complexion, had on when went away a blue jacket and trousers, and a black hat. Whoever will take up said runaway slaves and deliver them to the jailer of Wilmington, shall receive a reward of Ten Dollars. <BR> JOHN WEBB.<BR> Wilmington, January 29, 1801.
