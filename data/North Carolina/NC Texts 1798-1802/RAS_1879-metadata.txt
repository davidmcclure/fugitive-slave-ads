Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward. RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 7th instant, a Negro Fellow named OTHELLO--He is about thrity years of age, thin visage, and about five feet seven inches high--had on when he went away a blue woolen sailor's jacket, and stripped wool & cotton homespun trousers. The above reward will be given to any person apprehending said Negro Fellow and lodging him in Wilmington jail, or delivering him to the subscriber. <BR>JAMES MOORE.<BR>Wilmington, 18th April, 1801.
