Presumed or intended destination: Bath; the estate of Mr. Stephen Cambreleng
Slave name(s): Jack
Slaveholder: James R. Emery
Transcript: 15 Dollars Reward. <BR> RAN away from the subscriber, on the 12th day of March last, a negro fellow by the name of Jack; he is of a dark complexion, likewise short and well sett--by trade a Butcher. Any person that will apprehend and secure the same, so that I may get him again shall receive the above reward. As he has relations down about Bath, and a wife in Washington, belonging to the estate of Mr. Stephen Cambreleng, dec. it is very possible that he may be lurking about these places. <BR>JAMES R. EMERY.<BR> May 11.
