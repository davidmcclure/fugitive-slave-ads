Transcript: TWENTY-FIVE DOLLARS REWARD.<BR> RUN away from the subscriber, on the 10th of May last, a negro man named BRISTO about 20 years of age, 5 feet 8 or 10 inches high, of a dark complexion, and is well made, has a scar on his breast, occasioned by a burn when a child. He is well known in the town of Edenton. I will give the above reward to any person, that will deliver him to the subscriber, or to Mr. H. Flurry in Edenton, and all reasonable charges paid.<BR>DANIEL YOUNG. Windsor, Sept. 6th, 1800.
