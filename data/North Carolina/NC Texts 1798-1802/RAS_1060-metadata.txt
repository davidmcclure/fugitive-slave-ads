Transcript: Fifty Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN AWAY from the subscriber on the 18th of November last, a negro man named ELIAS, about thirty years old, about five feet seven or eight inches high, dark complexion, short hair ; he was raised by Mr. William Henry, near the town of Halifax.  He is a good groom and is very sensible.  He was sold by Mr. William Gilmour, merchant in Halifax.  He had different clothes, one mixt coat of homespun, a round striped homespun jacket, and a blue cloth coat, turned up with white cassmere.  The above reward will be given to any person who will devlier to the owner, or secure in gaol, the said negro.<BR>JOHN CALLAND.<BR>Hogston, March 18.    306 2P
