Transcript: FIFTEEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN AWAY from the subscriber, the 12th day of January last, a negro woman, named May; she is tall and black, & has rather a stoppage in her speech, and is thin in her face--I suppose she is in Hertford county, near her husband, who belongs to Langton.  Any person who will deliver said negro to me, shall receive the above reward.    MOSES MOORE.<BR>Bertie county, April 24.    54
