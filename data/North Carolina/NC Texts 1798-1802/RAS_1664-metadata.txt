Transcript: RUN AWAY from the subscriber on the 6th of July, a negro man named <BR> PETER,<BR>formerly the property of Dr. Guion. He is very black and speaks good English-He is about forty five years of age, and has a free wife in this town, at whole house. I have reason to suppose he is harboured. As he is well known in Newbern I need not describe him more particularly. <BR> I will give a reward of Ten Dollars to any person who will deliver him to Mr. Dudley the goaler or to the subscriber.--All persons are forwarned from harbouring or employing said fellow at their peril. <BR> THOMAS CURTIS.
