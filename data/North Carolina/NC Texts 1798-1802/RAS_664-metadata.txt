Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN-AWAY from the subscriber in Sampson county, the 16th of this instant, a likely Negro Man named JACK, about twenty-three years of age, of a yellow complexion, about five feet five or six inches high, well made to his highth; he had on when he went away, an old felt hat, and dark coloured woollen[sic] clothes, though he may disguise himself with other clothes. The said Negro was purchased a few months ago from Author Pearce in Johnston county.<BR>All masters of vessels and all other persons are forewarned from harbouring, employing or conveying away the said Negro.<BR>Any person who will bring said Negro Fellow to me, or secure him in any jail so that I get him again, shall have the above reward.<BR>March 30.     BENJAMIN THORNTON.<BR>The Printers in the state are requested to insert the above one week in their Newspapers.
