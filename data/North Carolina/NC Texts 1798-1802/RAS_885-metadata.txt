Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward. <BR>RUN away from the Subscriber, on the 28th of November last, a NEGRO MAN, named MOSE, about 20 Years old, 5 feet 6 or 8 Inches high, red Eyes, dark Complexion, and well set. He has been brought up to the Seafaring Business, and it is supposed he will make for some Seaport, probably Norfolk. <BR>Whoever will take up the said Negro, and deliver him to the Subscriber in Wayne County, on Bear Creek, or secure him in any Goal, so that he get him, shall be entitled to the above Reward and reasonable Expences paid. <BR>WILLIAM BRYAN. <BR>Jan.[January] 11, 1802.
