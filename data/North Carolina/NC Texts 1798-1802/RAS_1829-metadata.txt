Transcript: TEN DOLLARS DOLLARS.<BR>RUNAWAY from Col. Phil Hawkins near Warrenton, in February last, a likely [illegible] negro fellow, about five feet ten or eleven inches high, slim made, light complexion, by the name of JOSHUA.  He is an artful fellow, and has been hired for some months near Warrenton, and in the neighbourhood of Mr. Wm. A. Perry.  All persons are hereby forwarned from harbouring or carrying the said negro out of the state at their peril.  Whoever will deliver the said negro to Col. Hawkins, or to the subscriber, shall receive the above reward and all expences paid.<BR>JESSE LEE.<BR>Warrne county, September 6.      77 3
