Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN-AWAY the 23d day of February last, a Mulatto Man a slave, named POMPY, the property of the subscriber; had on when he went away, a brown broad cloth coat, a stolen light coloured great coat; he also stole a Mare, Bridle and Saddle, which mare he has since exchaged[exchanged] for a sorrel mare, on the north side of Cape-Fear rive. He passes by the name of JAMES BUTLER, a freeman; his toes are frost-bitten--said to be formerly brought from Tyrell county, N.C. Any person that will deliver said slave to the owner, or confine him in some gaol that the subscriber gets him, shall receive the above reward of <BR>THOMAS STANBACK.<BR>Richmond County, March 19.
