Transcript: Negro Run Away.<BR>RUNAWAY.<BR>From the Plantation of Thomas Clements, on Neuse River, in Granville County, near the Wake and Orange Lines, on the 14th Instant,<BR>A NEGRO FELLOW, about 30 Years of Age, six Feet high, strong and well-set, Complexion dark; has a remakable Mark on his Neck, which is considerably risen, occasioned by a Burn; has had one of his Ears cut.  Had on striped Overalls and a red Jacket.<BR>Whoever shall apprehend the said Negro, lodge him in any Gaol, and give Notice to the subscriber, will be well rewarded, and all Expences paid.<BR>THOMAS CLEMENTS.<BR>Aug. 15.
