Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY,<BR>On the Fourth Instant,<BR>A Negro Fellow named PRINCE, about five Feet high, Guinea born; he has the Marks of his Country upon his Eye, has a large Lump on his Brow, and a bald Place on the Crown of his Head; was formerly the Property of Mr. Christopher Christopher, of Raleigh.<BR>The above Reward will be paid to any Person who will lodge him in any Gaol in the State.<BR>JONES T. BRYAN.<BR>Jones County, Jan. 10, 1801.<BR>The Printers in this State will be please to give this a Place in their Papers for three Weeks, and send them on, together with their Bill, and they will be paid.<BR>J.T.B.
