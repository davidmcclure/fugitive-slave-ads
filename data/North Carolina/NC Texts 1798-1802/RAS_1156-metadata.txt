Transcript: Forty Dollars Reward.<BR> RAN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 8th of November last, a negro fellow named<BR> SIMBO.<BR>He was formerly the property of Francis Burns, dec. of Francis Baras, dec. of Onslow County, he is a Methodist Preacher, and can read and write.--He is about 6 feet high very black and smooth skin, and speaks very distinct.<BR> He is supposed to be lurking sometimes down Neuse river, and at others going up the same, and so he ranges through Craven, Jones, and Onslow counties. <BR> Any persons apprenhending the said negro, and delivering him to the subscriber, within five miles of Swansborough, shall be entitled to the above reward--Or any person will so secure him that I get him again, shell receive Twenty Dollars.--<BR> The most probable method to catching him, will be at Methodist meetings.  All Masters of Vessels and others are warned from harbouring, emploing or carry away, at their peril. <BR>HENRY LOCKEY.
