Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 5th of October last, a negro woman about 35 years old, by the name of RACHEL, of a common size, black complexion, walks very pert, and answers quick when spoken to.  All persons are hereby forwarned from harbouring or carrying the said negro out of the state at their peril.  Whoever will deliver the said negro to Duncan Lamon, jun. in Nash county, shall receive the above reward and all reasonable expences paid.<BR>DUNCAN LAMON, jun.<BR>November 3.    36 2p
