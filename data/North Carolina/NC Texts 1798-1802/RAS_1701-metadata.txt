Transcript: RUN-AWAY from me in November last, a negro man about forty five years old, of a low frame, yellow complection, and goes by the name of JACOB, his forehead runs from his head, which appears bald higher than is usual, he is a very humble fellow.  I hereby caution and forwarn all owners and masters of vessels from carrying him away, or harbouring said Jacob, and will give a generous reward to any person that will deliver him to me, or secure him in any gaol so that I get him.<BR>JAS. B. BRYANT<BR>Scotland Neck, Jan. 30    99
