Transcript: 50 Dollars Reward. <BR>   RUNAWAY from the Subscriber, on the 1st day of November last, a Negro Man named  ISAAC, about 22 years of age, 6 feet high, yellow complexion, thin visage, spare made, lost part of one or two of his fore teeth, is remarkable talkative, and has a remarkable small foot, the wool of his head inclinable to be short, and a shoe maker by trade. He formerly belonged to Abner Nash, Esquire, of the state of North-Carolina, deceased, and is well known in the town and vicnity of Newbern. It is supposed he has aimed for Virginia, by the way of Edenton or Halifax. Perhaps he has alterned his name to that of Jacob Spelman, and may as a free man. The above reward will be given to any person that will lodge him in any goal of this state, so that I can get him again, or the reward of Sixty Dollars, with all reasonable charges, to deliver him to the Subscriber, in Jones County, North Carolina, near the town of Newbern. <BR>   DURANT HATCH. <BR>   May 5th, 1800.
