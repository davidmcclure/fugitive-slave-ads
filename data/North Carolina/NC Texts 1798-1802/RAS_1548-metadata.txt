Transcript: RAN away withtout the least provocation, from the Hermitage, a few days since, a Negro fellow named TELEMACHUS, commonly called Macus, is an well made fellow, civil and plausible of a black complection, and is about 5 feet 9 inches high, speaks plain English-his wife is the property of Thomas Hill Esq. named Oliver, who I'm informed is gone up to Chatham county wit her mistress, and it is probable the fellow is gone up here after his wife and my harboured about Fayetteville or on Mr. Hill's plantation, or somewhere near Wilmington. <BR> I will give any person a reward of Twenty Dollars who will apprehend the said fellow and bring him to me at the Hermitage. <BR>J. BURGWIN.<BR> August 18.
