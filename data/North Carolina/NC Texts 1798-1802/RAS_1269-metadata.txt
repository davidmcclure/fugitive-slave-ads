Headline: Ten dollars reward

Date of departure: 1799-01-01
Slave name(s): Muse
Slaveholder: Elizabeth Bond
Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN away from the Subscriber, on the first day of January last, a negro man by the name of MUSE, about 22 years old, 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, a well built fellow, and is a little bow legged, has a large scar above one of his knees, occasioned by a burn when small, and has very small teeth for a negro, and stammers when speaking fast, had on when he went away, a light coloured homespun jacket, and a light coloured surtute.  Whoever will secure the above mentioned negro in any gaol so that I can get him again, shall receive the above reward, and all reasonable expences paid.<BR>ELIZABETH BOND.<BR>February 15, 1799.
