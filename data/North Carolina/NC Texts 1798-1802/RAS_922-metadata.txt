Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, a large black Negro Man, named JACK, about six feet one or two inches high--he has a very large mouth, bold look, and is quick spoken--had on when he went away a homespun shirt and overalls, a hunting shirt with stripes round him, and an old great coat. It is expected he will try to pass for a freeman. Any person who will bring the said Negro to me, shall receive the above reward.<BR>HARDY ROYAL.<BR>Sampson county, August 18.
