Transcript: RUN AWAY from the subscriber the 15th of January, a negro fellow by the name of PRIMUS, about 5 feet four inches high, of a yellowish complexion, will small scar on one of his temples, speaks plain English, & is very talkative-he went up about Christmas with one John Campbell, to whom he hired himself. It is likely that the fellow works in Fayetteville and passes himself for a free man. He had on a blue homespun jacket, and white homespun trousers and shirt. Whoever will apprehend the said fellow and lodge him in Wilmington or Fayetteville jail, shall receive five dollars reward.<BR>SUSANNA BORDEAUX.<BR>Black River, Feb. 4.
