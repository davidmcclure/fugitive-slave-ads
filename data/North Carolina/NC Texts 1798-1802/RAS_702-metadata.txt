Date of departure: 1798-12-20
Presumed or intended destination: Cumberland (Va.)
Slave name(s): Adam

Transcript: TWENTY FIVE DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN away from the subscriber, living about eight miles below Halifax, on the 20th December, a negro man by the name of ADAM, carried with him a negro woman by the name of Amey, said to be his wife; also a girl.  He is a low well set negro, has a scar under one eye, one of his eye-teeth is out.  It is likely he will go to Thomas Dean's, in Cumberland, Virginia, where he has a sister.  Whoever secures him so that he may be had again, shall receive the above reward.    39 8    JOSEPH SPEARS.
