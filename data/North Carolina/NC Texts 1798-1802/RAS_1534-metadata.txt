Transcript: RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on Monday the 4th of this inst. a Negro gril [sic] named NAN, formerly the property of Silas W. Arnett. Four dollars reward will be given to any person that will bring said Negro to me, if alive, or ten dollars, if dead. <BR> REBECKAH DELASTATIUS<BR> March 16.<BR>N.B. All Masters of vessels are forwarned from carring said Negro out of this port, under the penalty of the law, or any other person from harbouring the same.
