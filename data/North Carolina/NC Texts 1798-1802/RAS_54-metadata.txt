Transcript: RAN away, on the 28th ult. a negro fellow named TOM, about twenty-eight years of age, five feet, eight or ten inches high, has lost his eye-teeth.<BR> He was bought of Mr. Thomas Satterfield, of Edenton, and has a sister in Windsor: he is well known in both those places.<BR> TWENTY DOLLARS reward will be paid on delivery of him to me, or TEN DOLLARS for securing him, so that I get him again. <BR>SHADRAC COLLINS. <BR>August 11. <BR> N.B> Masters of vessels are forwarned from carrying him away, and all persons from harbouring, or carrying him away, under the penalty of the law.
