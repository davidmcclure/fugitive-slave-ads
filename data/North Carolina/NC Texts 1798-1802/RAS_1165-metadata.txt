Transcript: Forty Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN-AWAY from the subscriber, a negro man named PETER, about five feet ten inches high, well made, twenty years old, had on when he went away, a brown jacket and overalls, much worn and ragged, wears his hair pinned on each temple, and sometimes behind.  Also a negro woman maned FANNY, about nineteen or twenty years old; has on a blue woolen homespun petticoat and wrapper, and other clothes I cannot describe.  Said negroes were purchased in Norfolk, Virginia, and it is supposed will aim for that place.  Any person who will apprehend them, and lodge them in gaol so that I can get them, shall receive the above reward and all necessary expences paid.<BR>THOMAS BATCHELOR.<BR>Tarborough, March 20.    300 3P
