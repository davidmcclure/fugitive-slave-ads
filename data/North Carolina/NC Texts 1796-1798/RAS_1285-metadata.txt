Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN-away from the subscriber, about four months ago, a negro man named<BR>SHADE;<BR>he is an artful fellow, about 40 years of age; had on when he went away a short blue jacket and trowsers, he has lost four fingers off his right hand, by the cut of an axe. I am informed he is harboured or employed in Newbern, and it is very likely he may be found with one Jack Hamilton, with whom he is intimately acquainted.--I will give the above reward; with all other expenses, to any person who will apprehend and deliver said fellow to me, or so secure him that get him again.<BR>CHARLES SMALLWOOD.<BR>Washington, March 11.
