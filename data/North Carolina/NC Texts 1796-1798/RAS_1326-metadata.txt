Time period (decade): 1790-1799
Person placing ad: Edmund Hatch
Date of departure: 1796-09-25
County of residence: Jones  County (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Jack;Charles
Slaveholder: Edmund Hatch
Transcript: TEN POUNDS REWARD.<BR>RUN-away on the 25th inst. two negroes, viz. JACK and CHARLES. Jack is about 18 years of age, country born, yellow complection, thick lip; about five feet two or three inches high, and was clothed in homespun. Charles is about the same height, black complection, well made, and walks bow legged, has a scar on his breast, and one over his eye, speak broken English and good French; was clothed in homespun, except a black negro cotton jacket. Any person that will apprehend the said negroes, and deliver them to the subscriber in Jones county, or lodge them in any jail, so that the subscriber gets them, shall be entitled to the above reward, or five pounds for either of them.<BR>EDMUND HATCH.<BR>September 27.
