Slave name(s): Tony
Slaveholder: William Stubbs
Transcript: RUN away from the Subscriber, a yellow fellow, by the name of TONY; he is almost five feet eigh[t] inches high, nearly fifty years of age; had on when he went away a short blue jacket, a corduroy vest, a pair of white tow trowsers, a long blue coat, a pair of black velvet breeches, and sundry other articles.  Whoever will take up said runaway, and bring him to the owner, shall receive TEN DOLLARS REWARD, if taken out of the county, and FIVE DOLLARS if in the county.  --The said negro is cunning artful fellow, and it is supposed he will attempt to get to the state of Maryland, where he was raised.<BR>WILLIAM STUBBS.<BR>Tyrrell County, May 31, 1798.    4w
