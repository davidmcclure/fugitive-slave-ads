Transcript: Five Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, the 29th of January, a Negro Wench named SALLY, about 30 years of age. She has always attended about the house, has a husband belonging to Mrs. Ann Quince of Wilmington, named Virgil, by whom she will no doubt be harboured in or about town. Whoever will secure the said wench in Wilmington jail, or deliver her to the subscriber at Moorfields, shall receive the above reward, and reasonable expences paid.<BR>RICHARD QUINCE.<BR>February 8.<BR>58[?]
