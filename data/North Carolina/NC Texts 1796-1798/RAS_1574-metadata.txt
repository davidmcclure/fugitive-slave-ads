Transcript: RAN-Away, a likely negro lad, lately the property of Mitchell Downs, Esq; now of Mrs. Mary Buyan. His name is<BR>JOHN.<BR>He is of a middle stature, and has a thin visage. Whoever will secure him, so that he may be got again, will be handsomely rewarded, and all reasonable charges, by<BR>DURAN HATCH.<BR>N.B. All persons are forwarned from harbouring or employing him, and masters of vessels and others, are particularly cautioned, not to carry him away, under the penalty of the law.
