Transcript: RUN-AWAY from the subscriber, a negro man, named DOCTOR, formerly belonging to Mr. William Watson, in Bladen county-he is about twenty-three years of age, middle size, sluttters when he speaks, and is branded on both cheeks with HLL-when he lived with me, he went by the name of JOHN MOONEY. Whosoever will secure the said negro so that I may get him, will receive a reward of ten dollars for their trouble.<BR>HENRY LOUIS LUTTERLOH.<BR>Chatham, May 9th,     61-3
