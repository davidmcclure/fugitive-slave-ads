Transcript: 15 DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>  RUN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 12th of April, 1797, a young negro fellow named FRANK, about 23 years of age, five feet four inches high, well set, thick lips, and stutters in his speech, formerly belonging to Richard  Benbury. Any person that will apprehend said negro, and bring him to me, or confine him in any gaol so that I get him again, shall receive the above reward. <BR>  BENJAMIN MANNING. <BR>  Edgecomb County, Aug. 29, 1797.
