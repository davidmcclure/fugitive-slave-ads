Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR> RUN-AWAY on the 6th of August, from the subscriber living eight miles above Fayetteville, near Armstrong's mills, on Cape Fear, a negro man, about 30 years of age, named ARMISTEAD, stoutly made, about 5 feet 10 inches and a half high, a large broad face, and had on when he went away an osnaburgh shirt and trowsers[sic]-he has been lurking in town until a fortnight ago. Any person who will lodge said negro, in any jail in this state, will receive the above reward.<BR> NATHAN KING.<BR> Aug. 10.
