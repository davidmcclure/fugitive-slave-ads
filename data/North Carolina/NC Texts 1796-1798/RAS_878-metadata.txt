Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward. <BR>RAN away some time ago, a mulatto fellow named ELIJAH, about 5 feet 7 or 8 inches high, well known in and about Wilmington, was some time ago taken at Swansborough, has an impediment in his speech, and the lower part of his left ear taken off; he is a good shoemaker, and will probably attempt to pass as a freeman. The above reward will be given to any person that will deliver him to Doctor Hill in Wilmington, or the subscribers at Rockey Point. <BR>W. DAVIS, <BR>April 6. ts T. MOORE. <BR>N.B. The printer in Petersburg and Richmond will please insert the above.
