Slave name(s): Frank
Transcript: RUN away from the subscriber, a likely negro man named FRANK, of a yellowish complexion, about 30 years of age, and about 6 feet 9 or 10 inches high, one of his fingers crooked and one of his ears cropped; he had on when he went away a regimental coat; he has a free pass, signed David Standly and John Dawson, both of which have been dead this some years past; he went away about the 24th of September, 1798.  Any person that will apprehend the said negro, so that I get him, shall receive Twenty Five Dollars Reward.  I hereby forwarn all masters of vessels and others from carrying the said negro out of the state, as I shall prosecute those who do to the utmost rigour of the law.<BR>HENRY SPILLER.<BR>Cashie Neck, Bertie county, Oct. 2, '98.
