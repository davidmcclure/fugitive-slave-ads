Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from my Plantation on Saturday morning last, a young Negro Fellow named Jem, about 22 years old, five feet 8 inches high, slender made with a smooth likely face speaks plain and plausible, but rather hoarse--he has a Negro wench for a wife, belonging to Mrs. Maurice Jones and it is probable he may be harboured in Wilmington. Whoever takes up the said Fellow and delivers him to me at Hermitage, shall have the above reward paid by<BR>John Burgwin.<BR>Hermitage, 26th Feb. 1798 [illegible]<BR>Masters of vessels are warned not to take him off.<BR>The said Negro is outlawed--and it is said he as been seen near Holley Sheldon.
