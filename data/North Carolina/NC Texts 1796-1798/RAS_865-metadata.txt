Transcript: FIFTEEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN away from the subscriber, on the 17th ult. a negro man named SIMON, about 18 years old; he is of a dark complexion, about four feet ten inches high, stout made; had on when he went away a blue homespun coat, kersey trowsers, and homespun shirt, an old hat patched with a piece not dyed.  I hereby forwarn all master of vessels from carrying him off.  Whoever will deliver the said negro to me, or secure him in any gaol so that I get him, shall receive the above reward and all reasonable charges paid by<BR>JOSHUA HAYES.<BR>Bertie county, April 2d, 1797.
