Date of departure: 1797-06-30
Presumed or intended destination: Gates County
Slave name(s): Sam;Sim
Slaveholder: David Day
Transcript: TWENTY-FIVE DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUNAWAY on Friday the 30th of June last, a mulatto man, named SAM, about 20 years of age, of a middling height, but stout made, has a scar on one side of his nose, and has a thick broad foot.  He is said to have a white father in Gates county, whither he may try to get, and endeavour to pass for a free man.  The above reward will be given to any one who will deliver him to me, or secure him in any jail so that he may be got, with all reasonable expences.<BR>July 1.    59    DAVID DAY.
