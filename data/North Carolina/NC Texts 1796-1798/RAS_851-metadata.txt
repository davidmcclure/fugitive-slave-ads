Transcript: FIFTEEN DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>Ranaway from the subscribers, about the 4th of February last, a likely negro fellow, named<BR>TONEY;<BR>about thirty-seven years of age, five feet seven or eight inches high, slender made, very black, has a sly look, and speaks very polite; he has many clothes with him, he plays on the violia, it is supposed that he has a forged pass.<BR>Whoever will take up said negro, and return him to us, or secure him in any gaol so that we get him again, shall have the above reward.<BR>ISRAEL & THO's[Thomas] WENDLEYS.<BR>N.B. All masters of vessels and others are forwarned from harbouring or carrying him off at their peril.<BR>Beaufort county, March 15.
