Transcript: TEN DOLLARD REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from my plantation in Rowan county, near Lexington, a Negro Lad, 17 or 18 years of age, (marked as follows) very black, low but well made, had a large scar on his left wrist, and also his left knee, occasioned by fire when small; a large scar on his breast, by being burnt with powder, lately the grains plain to be seen, a small white scar on his upper lip.  Any person securing him in jail, so that I may get him, shall be entitled to the above reward.<BR>JAMES CAMPBELL.<BR> Rowan County, Sept. 30.      34 3m
