Slave alias: Phil
Slaveholder: David Witherspoon
Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, living in Wilkes county, forty five miles above the Moravian Towns on the Yadkin river, a Negro man named MOSES, but calls himself PHIL, five feet nine or ten inches high, between thirty six and forty years old, long visage, or a dark complexion, is a very artful and cunning fellow. I bought him in December last, in Perquimans county, where he will aim for he left my house the tenth of last March.  Any person that will apprehend said Negro, and deliver him at my house, or secure him in some gaol, and acquaint me by post directed to Salem, where I can get him, shall have the above reward and all reasonable charges.  DAVID WITHERSPOON.<BR>June 20.    7 2
