Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN-AWAY on the 1st instant, from the subscriber, a Negro boy named DICK, about 15 years old, formerly the property of Mr. THOMAS BRICKELL of Franklin county, he is of a dark complexion; from what I can hear, it appears that he was stolen by some white person or persons, and that he is now carried off by them to Cumberland.  Whoever will deliver the said Negro to me, or secure him in any jail so that I get him again, shall receive the above reward, and reasonable expenses paid by PATRICK WALKER.<BR>N.B. He had on when he went away a negro cotton jacket and brown holland shirt and overalls.<BR>Warren county, November 2.<BR>25 2
