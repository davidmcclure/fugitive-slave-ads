Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN or stolen from the subscriber on the 4th inst. a negro fellow by the name of STRUTHAM, about 30 years of age, five feet nine inches high, yellow complexion, had a small scar on his forehead, and a remarkable long narrow foot; had on when he went away a white yarn homepsun shirt and breeches, a mixed blue coat, a black velvet jacket and a new pair shoes.<BR>By some information I had since he went away, I suspsect him to be harboured or concealed by a certain Wm.[William] Foshee, in one of the Southern counties of this state: Whoever will apprehend said negro and lodge him in gaol, shall receive the above reward of ten dollars and all reasonable expences.<BR>WM.[WILLIAM] RAMSEY.<BR>Red Field Ford, Chatham<BR>County, June 11.      7 2
