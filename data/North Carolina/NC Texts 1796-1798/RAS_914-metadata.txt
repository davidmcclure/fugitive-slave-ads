Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber on the 28th inst. a negro fellow named TOM about five feet 8 or 10 inches high.  He has lost one or two of his eyeteeth.  I bought him last spring of Mr. Thomas Satterfield, in Edenton; where I suppose he is lurking.  Whoever will deliver said fellow to me at Poneck's Ferry, or secure him in any gaol so that I get him, shall receive the above reward.<BR>July 29.    SHADRACH COLLINS.
