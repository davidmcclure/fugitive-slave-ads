Slave name(s): Andrew
Slaveholder: Samuel Wornum
Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber, the 19th of May ult. a Negro man named ANDREW.  He carried away several sorts of clothing, mostly homespun.  He is about six feet high or more, coarse voice, knock-kneed, has red eyes, with two spots over them; very large bones and sinews.--His range is very large, as it is supposed he may have gone to Warren, Franklin or Chatham counties, having a brother at Mr. James Ransom's in one of those counties; or to Scotland Neck, where he has acquaintances, having sawed there several months.--The above reward will be given to any person who secures him in any gaol so that I get him again.<BR>SAMUEL WORNUM.<BR>N.B. He has had the small-pox, but very slightly.<BR>Northampton county, June 1.
