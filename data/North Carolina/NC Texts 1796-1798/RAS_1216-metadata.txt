Transcript: NOTICE.<BR>RAN-away from the subscriber on Saturday the 11th instant, a negro fellow, named JACK, his wife TAMER, a yellow wench, and their four children: they are supposed to be lurking about the town of Newbern. All persons are hereby forewarned not to harbour them, and all Captains of vessels from carrying them out of the state at their peril, as I am determined to prosecure with the utmost rigour of the law, every person or persons who shall offend herein.<BR>Jack and Tamer are both well known in Newbern, and its vicinity. A reward of FIVE POUNDS will be paid to any person who will deliver the said negroes to the subscriber at Milton plantation, near Newbern.<BR>WILSON BLOUNT.<BR>February 24.
