Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber in Fayetteville, on the 4th instant, a negro man by the name of SIMON; he is of a black complection, about 5 feet 10 inches high, and about 34 years of age, carries his head low when he walks, he is marked in both ears, with a half crop and half moon in the left, and a crop and slit in the right, and had a sore on one of his legs when he went away.  He was formerly the property of Mr. Rhodes, on Roanoke river, near Halifax.  He also carried away with him a mulatto wench by the name of BET, about 26 years of age, 5 feet 9 inches high, and big with child.<BR>Whoever delivers said fellow and wench to me in Fayetteville, or secures them so that I get them again, shall be entitled to the above reward.<BR>THOMAS WHITE.<BR>October 10.
