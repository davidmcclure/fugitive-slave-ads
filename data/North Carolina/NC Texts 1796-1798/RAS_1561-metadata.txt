Transcript: RAN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 2nd inst. a tall black negro man by the name of HENRY; he has large white eyes, and is about 40 or 45 years of age, and is a ditcher by trade, he wore when he went away, an old brown broad cloth coat, and a pair of velvet pantaloons, and carried with him other clothes, he was raised in the state of Maryland, and has passed in the county as a freeman; he speaks tolerable good english and can write and read indifferently; he is a left handed, though a handy fellow at [illegible] business, such as farming, &c. I forewarn all persons harbouring this fellow at their peril.<BR>I will give five dollars if in this state, if in any other ten dollars provided I get the negro.<BR>JAMES WRIGHT<BR>Duplin county, October 7
