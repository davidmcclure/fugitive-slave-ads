Place of residence: Bald Mountain (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Will
Slaveholder: Anthony Peele
Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR> RUN away from the subscriber, living on the Yadkin river, near the Bald Mountain, in Rowan county, on the 21st of May last, a likely Negro fellow, of a yellow complexion, supposed by some to be a mulatto, named WILL, about 24 or 25 years of age, and about five feet seven or eight inches high, well set, straight limbs, featured much like a white man, has small hands and feet, plays on the banjo, and makes a tolerable good shoe--carried off clothes of different kinds--Whoever will take up said negro and deliver him to me, shall have the above reward, or ten dollars for securing him in any jail, so that he may be held again, and reasonable charges.<BR>ANTHONY PEELE.
