Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber, on the 11th inst. a Negro Fellow named JACOB, about 22 years of age, and about five feet 10 inches high.  It is supposed he will make for Petersburg.  He took with him a great quantity of fine clothes, and a large sum of money.  All persons are forewarned from harbouring or carrying him off, under the penalty prescribed by law.  Whoever secures him so that he may be had, shall be entitled to the above reward.<BR>58 4    DAVID M'ADOW.<BR>Guilford county, June 14.
