Transcript: RAN away from the subscriber, on Sunday night, the 30th ultimo, a Negro fellow named CATO--he is very talkative and deceitful, has long hair, but not tied when he left me, a high nose for one of his colour, a remarkable good foot, ahd[sic] plays on the violin. He is well known in Wilmington, by Dr. Hill and Mr. David Jones, and I believe he has made for that place.<BR>Ten Dollars reward will be paid to any person who will deliver him to me at Moseley Hall, on Rocky Point, or secure him in the Goal of Wilmington, so that I get him again.<BR>I do hereby forewarn all masters of vessels, and others, from harbouring, employing, or carrying him away at their peril.<BR>Tobias Cobb.<BR>October 1. 93
