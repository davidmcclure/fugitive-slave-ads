Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, living at Williamsborough, Granville county, N. Carolina, on the 20th of last month, a Negro Man, named OLIVER, about twenty-seven or eight years of age, by trade a blacksmith, saws very well with the whip saw, and is very handy at almost any kind of business: spare made, rather under the common size, left handed, has a large mole on one of his cheeks, and a scar on his face, occasioned by a burn when small; very sensible, pert, and active: reads tolerably well, and attempts to write: will I expect attempt to pass for a freeman: he was formerly the property of Capt. Blackman Moseley, of Chesterfield county, Virginia.  I will give the above reward to any person delivering him to me, or securing him so that I get him again.<BR>May 30    STEPHEN SNEED.
