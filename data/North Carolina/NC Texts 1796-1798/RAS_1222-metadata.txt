Transcript: FIVE DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from me, some weeks since, a negro wench named SABINA, well known in and about this town, and remarkable for a scar on one of her eye-lids. I will give the above reward to any one who will deliver her to me or the jaiior[sic] of the county; and I hereby forbid all persons at their peril to employ, harbour, or conceal her. She will endeavour to pass as in the service of General Smith.<BR>William Claypoole.<BR>Wilmington, April 20.
