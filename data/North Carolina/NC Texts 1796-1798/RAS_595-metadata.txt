Transcript: TWENTY-FIVE DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 26th of July last, a Negro lad named ABRAHAM, about 19 or 20 years old, about five feet five or six inches high, long limbd[sic], rather yellow, has a small scar at one side of his nostrils, he says was occasioned by the kick of a horse when small ; he formerly belonged to a Mr. Edmond Goodwin at Isle of Wight county, in Virginia : It is probable he may endeavour to get back to his old neighborhood again  I will give the above reward to any person that will bring him to me or secure him in any gaol, and send me word so that I get him again, and pay all reasonable expences.<BR>FIGURES LEWIS<BR>Edgcombe[sic] county, Feb. 21  41 3t<BR>N.B. I forwarn all persons from harbouring said negro.
