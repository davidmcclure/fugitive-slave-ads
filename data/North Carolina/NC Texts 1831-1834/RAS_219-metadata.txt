Transcript: $20 REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, on the 14th May last, a negro man named EZEKIEL, about 24 years of age, 5 feet 5 or 6 inches high, of dark complexion, and by trade a House-Carpenter. He is probably lurking about the plantation of Michael N. Fisher, on Handcock's Creek, about 25 miles from Newbern, where he has a wife. He can read and write tolerably well, and may attempt to pass for a free man. He is well known at the plantations fo Lemuel D. Hatch and James Hatch, in Duplin and Jones Counties, where he has worked.<BR>The above reward will be given for the delivery of said negro to me in Newbern, or for his confinment in any jail so that I get him; and all reasonable expences will be paid.<BR>WILLIAM L. SEARS.<BR>Newbern, Feb. 17th, 1832.    '83tf.
