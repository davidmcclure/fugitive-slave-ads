The above reward will be given to any person who will safely deliver him to me, or thirty dollars will be given for apprehending and securing him in any jail, so that I get him again.<BR>RICH'D[Richard] WARD.<BR>Franklin county, N. C.}<BR>Nov. 27, 1831}<br /><br/>43 Twp<BR>N. B. Tom may be passing for a free man.
