Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, on the night of the 20th inst., a Negro Man named DEMPSEY or yellow complexion, about 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, aged 21 years and tolerably likely. Said Slave is the property of Samuel Shaw of Alabama, formerly of this County.  When Mr. Shaw removed to the West in November last, Dempsey was in the woods but was soon thereafter arrested and confined in Fayetteville Jail, where he remained about seven months.  The Subscriber then took him out, and has had him in possession since June last, up to the date of his elopement.<BR>The above reward will be paid for his apprehension and confinement in any Jail in the State.<BR>MATTHEW LYNN,<BR>Wake county, 14 miles N.W. of Raleigh,<BR>October 25.    31<BR>The Fayetteville Observer will insert the above two weeks, and charge this Office.
