The said Negro is about 28 years of age, light complexion, stout built, and free spoken, supposed to be lurking about Raleigh, where he is well known. The above reward will be paid to any person confining him in jail, so that I get him.<BR>H. M'ADEN.<BR>Caswell Court House,}<BR>N. C. Nov. 9, 1833}<BR>50 4w
