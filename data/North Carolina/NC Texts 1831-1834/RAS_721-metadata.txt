Transcript: 25 or 50 Dollars Reward<BR>WILL be given for Negro DICK and his wife SALLY, who left me on the night of the 14th instant.  Dick is about 35 years old, about 5 feet 8 inches high, bowlegged, down look and very black.  His wife is very fleshy, rather low and not very black.  Dick was dressed in linsey, and has with him some good clothes to wear, and some bed clothes, &c. If these Negroes are taken, and confined in any Jail within the State, $25 will be given; if out of the State, $50.<BR>SAMUEL COBLE,<BR>Orange county, N.C. (Rock Creek)<BR>January 15, 1834    11 3t
