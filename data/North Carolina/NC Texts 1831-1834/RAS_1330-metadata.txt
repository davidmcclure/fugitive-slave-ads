Transcript: Thirty Dollars Reward,<BR>FOR apprehending and delivering to Fayetteville Jail, my Negro man<BR>SIMON,<BR>Known as SIMON BLUE, who absconded on Thursday, the 19th of July last, without any provocation. It is supposed, he is lurking about Mr. Daniel McMillan's, who owns his wife, and lives about 14 miles South-west of this place. SIMON is about 40 years old, six feet high, with a very long foot; has relations, (the property of Mr. Wilkison Robeson, Robeson county, and Mr. Kelly's, in Moore county,) which places he may probably visit.<BR>H. CAMPBELL.<BR>Fayetteville, N. C. Nov. 13.<BR>6 3m
