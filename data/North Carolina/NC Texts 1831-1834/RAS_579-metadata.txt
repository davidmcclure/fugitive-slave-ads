Transcript: Twenty-five Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, living in Johnston county, on Swift Creek, 8 miles above Smithfield, on Saturday the 13th instant, a Negro Man, named JERRY.  Said Negro is about 30 years of age, middle size, very active, has a pleasing countenance when spoken to and has a remarkable fine set of teeth.  He has a very peculiar flesh mould on his breast, which if observed, would lead at once to his identity.  He is branded with the letter M on one of his hands, though so long a time has elapsed since it was done, that the appearance of it is about effaced.  He is dark complected, about 5 feet 7 or 8 inches high, and has with him a variety of Homespun Clothing.<BR>The above reward will be paid for his apprehension and confinement in any Jail within the State, for his delivery at the Jail in Raleigh.<BR>JESSE HOLLAND.<BR>Johnston county, N.C. July 23, 1834
