Transcript: STOP THE RUNAWAY<BR>RAN AWAY, on the 5th of September last, my Negro Man, named THOMPSON-- He is about 24 or 25 years of age, of a bright yellow complexion, about 5 feet 8 inches high, rather slender made, has a down look & is slow spoken; had on when he went off a blue homespun coat pretty much worn--his other clothing not recollected.  It is probable he is in Raleigh or its vicinity.  All persons are forewarned harboring said slave.<BR>A liberal Reward will be paid to any person who will deliver the above Negro to me, or secure him in any Jail so that I get him again.<BR>JOHN WHITE.<BR>Granville county, Oct. 5, 1834.  49 3t
