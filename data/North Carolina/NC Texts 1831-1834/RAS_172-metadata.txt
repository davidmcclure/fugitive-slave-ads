Transcript: $10 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, living near Laurel Hill, a Mulatto Girl near five feet high, 39 years old, (with Child,) has a scar on the right arm, below the elbow. The above reward will be paid to any person who will bring her to myself at my plantation near Laurel Hill, or to Hugh McLaurin of Fayetteville, or lodge her in any Jail in the State, so that I can get her again.<BR>January 11,<BR>JOHN McLEAN.
