Transcript: $100 REWARD<BR>RANAWAY about the 15th December last negro, JOE, his wife TEMPY and son NEEDHAM.<BR>JOE is a Blacksmith, about 33 years old, not very dark, with a heavy head, has a down look when spoken to and is about 6 feet high, inclined to be slender.<BR>TEMPY is a small woman rather dark and is about 25 years old, she is quite an intelligent and genteel looking negro.<BR>NEEDHAM is a smart boy about 9 or 10 years old and can read and write.<BR>JOE left the person to whom he was hired, with an intention, as he said, to visit his brother, in Nash, county, who is called Bish and formerly belonged to Mrs. Adkins.<BR>I will give $100 dollars for the above named negroes, if taken out of the State, and fifty dollars if taken in the State.<BR>E. HAYWOOD<BR>Raleigh Jan. 25.<BR>44--3w
