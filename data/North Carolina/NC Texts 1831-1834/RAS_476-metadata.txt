Transcript: 25 DOLLARS REWARD.<BR> RANAWAY from the subscriber, at his residence in Randolph County, N.C. on the 8th instant, Two young negro men, viz:<BR>JESSE & ELIJAH;<BR> Jesse is about twenty two years old, weighs about 140 pounds, of low stature but well made, is of a dark mulatto colour, and can read print tolerably well. Elijah is about eighteen years old, spare made, is rather inclined to be slender built and his lips project rather beyond the proper length. He had on when he left a half worn chiphat[?] and thin homespun apparel.<BR> They are intelligent smart fellows and will no doubt try to pass as free men, under the name of Waldon, Evans or Chonlor; they are both mixed with Indian.<BR>The above reward will be given for the apprehension and confinement of said negroes in any jail so that I may get them again, or twelve dollars fifty cents for either.    6t77<BR>J.K. ARMISTEAD.<BR> Moffit's Mills, P.O.<BR>Randolph County, N.C. Apl. 8, 1833.
