Transcript: $20<BR>Reward. <BR> Ran away from the Subscriber, living in Montgomery County, on Monday the 30th of December last,<BR>A Negro Man, named Peter, And his Wife, named Nan.<BR> Peter is about 6 feet high, 30 years old, spare made, of a dark complexion, and a pleasant countenance; he has a scar on his right foot. <BR> Nan is about the middle size, about 28 years old, of a yellow complexion, and has a scar on her nose.<BR> They have been heard of in Randolph County, under false names: Peter called himself Walter, and Nan took the name of Polly. It is supposed they are aiming for a free State, in company with two other negroes.<BR> I will give the above reward to any person who will lodge them in any jail, and notify me of the same. <BR> WILLIAM HARRIS. Montgomery County, N.C.} <BR> January 27, 1834.}
