Transcript: NOTICE.<BR>RANAWAY on 20th February, my man DERRY, about thirty-five or six years old, near six feet high, very black complected, high cheek bones, and large feet.--Had on when he left me, a Liney round about coat, Coperas[sic] colored overhaul's, and an old wool hat. Any person returning said negro to me, at my residence, at the Mills formerly owned by Col. David Caldwell, in Iredell county, North Carolina, or confining him in any Jail so that I get him, shall be amply compensated for all trouble and expense.<BR>S. GLASSCOCK, Sen.<BR>March 29--4w36
