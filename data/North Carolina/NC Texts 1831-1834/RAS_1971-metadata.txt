Transcript: $5 REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, on Saturday evening last, a mulatto boy named OWEN. He is about 17 years of age, 5 feet 7 or 8 inches high. Masters of vessels and other[s] are cautioned against harbouring or carrying him away, under the penalty in such case made and provided. The above reward will be given for his apprehension and delivery to the subscriber, in Newbern, or for securing him in any jail so that he shall get him again.<BR>EDWARD C. O. TINKER.<BR>15th Nov., 1833.
