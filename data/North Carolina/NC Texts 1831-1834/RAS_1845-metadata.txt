Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward. <BR> RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, living 14 miles West of Raleigh, on the Chapel Hill road, on the 23d inst. his Negro Boy JOHN.--Said negro is about 16 years old, very well grown for one of his age, jetty black complexion, well built and would readily pass for a year or two older. He has a scar on one of his ancles, occasioned by a scald, which is white about the centre[sic]. <BR> The above reward  will be given for his apprehension and confinement in any Jail, so that I get him again. <BR> ASHWELL McGEBEE.<BR>Sept. 30, 1834<BR>48
