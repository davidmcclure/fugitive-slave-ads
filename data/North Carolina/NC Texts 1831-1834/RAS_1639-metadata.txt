Transcript: RANAWAY<BR>FROM the subscriber, on the 14th ult. a negro fellow named BOB, about 22 years of age, very black complexion, and about 5 feet high. He is well known about Wilmington, and is probably lurking about Mr. David Thally's plantation, on the North East river, where his father and mother live.<BR>Any person who will deliver the above fellow, to the Jailer; or secure him, so that I can get him into my possession, shall be liberally rewarded.<BR>W.S. WILLKINGS.<BR>May 7th.<BR>70-tf.
