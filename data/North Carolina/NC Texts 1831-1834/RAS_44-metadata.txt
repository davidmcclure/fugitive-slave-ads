Transcript: $10 REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, six miles N.W. of Ashboro', Randolph county, NC. on Wednesday the 23d of May inst. my Negro Boy STEPHEN. Said Stephen is about 27 years old, about 5 feet 3 inches high, is a remakably square, heavy thickset negro, thick projecting lips, and short spread nose; squeaks quick and promptly when spoken to.  He carried with him a blue broad cloth coat, velvet collar, half worn; two pair of pantaloons, one of purple bangup cord, the other of brown holland : a black velvet waistcoat, also one of home-made blue striped : two shirts, one with a plaited bosom, and a black fur hat nearly new: he had also a hickory staff with a buckhorn handle.  It is supposed that he has procured a passport to enable himself to reach a free State.  I will give a reward of Ten Dollars for his apprehension and confinement in any Jail so that I get him again.<BR>JOHN B. MOSS<BR>Ashboro',N.C. May 25, 1833.    30 3
