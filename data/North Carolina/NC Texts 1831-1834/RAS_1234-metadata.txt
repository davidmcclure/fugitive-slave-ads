Transcript: Five Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on the 30th December last, a negro man named BILL, about 60 years of age, five feet 6 inches high, stout made and black complexion. He formerly belonged to a Mr. Williams, of Onslow County, and lately to Mr. Elijah Clark, and is probably lurking about New River, in Onslow County, where he has relations. All persons are forewarned from harbouring, employing, or carrying him away, under the penalty of the law.<BR>The above reward will be given to any person who will deliver him to the subscriber, or secure him in jail, so that he gets him.<BR>THOMAS CUTERELL.<BR>Broad Creek, Craven County, 17th Jan. 1834. 83[?]kt
