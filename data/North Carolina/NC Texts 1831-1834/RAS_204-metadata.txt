Transcript: $20 Reward.<BR>A REWARD of Twenty Dollars will be paid by the Subscriber, for the apprehension and delivery to him of a[?] negro named<BR>RANDALL,<BR>who absconded on the 3d January. A description is deemed unnecessary, as he is well known in this place and vicinity. FIFTY DOLLARS will be given, for proof sufficient to convict any white person of harboring him. Masters of vessels are cautioned against employing, or carrying away the said fellow, as the Law will be rigidly enforced.<BR>T. COWAN.<BR>January 9, 1833.<BR>tf 1
