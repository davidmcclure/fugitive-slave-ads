Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY, in January last, a negro woman, well known in Wilmington, as<BR>HANNAH HOLLMAN.<BR>A description of her is unnecessary; and as she has many acquaintances in the country, as well as in town, she is probably in the neighborhood, and might easily be apprehended. All persons are cautioned against harboring said negro. I will pay the above reward for her delivery to me in this place.<BR>F.A. LUMSDEN.<BR>Wilmington, May 15th, 1833.<BR>19 2t.
