Slave name(s): Jno.[John] Turner

Transcript: $10 REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, a few days since, a mulatto man named JNO.[JOHN] TURNER, about 19 years of age, and 6 feet 2 inches high. He stoops a little in walking, and when spoken to has a downcast look. It is probable that he will attempt to pass as a free man. All persons are forewarned from harbouring, employing, or carrying him away under the penalty of the law. The above reward will be paid for his delivery to the subscriber, or for securing him in jail.<BR>DONUM MONTFORD.<BR>8th June, 1832.
