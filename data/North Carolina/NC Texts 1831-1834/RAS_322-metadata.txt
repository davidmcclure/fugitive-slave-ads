Transcript: $25 Reward.  <BR>THE above reward will be given by the subscriber for the apprehension and confinement in Jail of his Negro girl MARY, who was, it is believed, enticed away by another Negro on the 8th of September last. She is twelve years of age, of light complexion, well grown and likely. She is probably lurking in or about Newbern, or she may have gone to Hillsborough or Washington, at all of which places she has connexions.  <BR>JOHN E. FOSCUE.  <BR>Jones County, March 14th, 1834.
