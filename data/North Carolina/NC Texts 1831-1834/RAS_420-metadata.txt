Transcript: $80 Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber on 19th inst. a negro man known by the name of<BR>SAM USHER or SAM PIGFORD.<BR>formerly belonged to a Mr. Pigford, in the neighborhood of South Washington--about 30 years of age, 6 feet high, spare built, black complexion, has a little blink in one eye, thin visage, and speaks slow. Also, three boys,<BR>ANTHONY, REDICK and SANDY,<BR>all about 19 or 20 years old.--Redick, square built, a little bow legged, and speaks slow--black, about 5 feet 8 or 10 inches. Anthony, about same height and complexion--speaks quick and stutters a little--well built. Sandy, black, very spare, and speaks plainly, but slowly.<BR>Twenty Dollars will be given for the delivery of either of the above negroes to the subscriber or to the Jailer in Wilmington.<BR>SAM'L[Samuel] POTTER.<BR>Wilmington, N C 21st July, 1834. 81-tf.
