Transcript: $10 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, six miles north west of Ashboro' Randolph Co. N.C. on Wednesday the 23. of May inst. my negro boy STEPHEN.<BR> Said Stephen is about 27 years old, about 5 feet 3 inches high, is a remarkably square, heavy, thick set negro; thick projecting lips, and short spread nose: speaks quick and promptly when spoken to. He carried with him a blue broad cloth coat, halfworn with a velvet waistcoat, also one of home made blue stripe, a black fur hat, nearly new: he had also a hickory staff with a buck horn handle. It is supposed he had procured a passport to establish himself to reach a free state. I will give the above reward of ten dollars for his apprehension and and confinement in any jail so that I get him again.<BR>3t70    JOHN B. MOSS.<BR> Ashboro' N.C. May 25, 1833.
