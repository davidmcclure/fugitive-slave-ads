Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN away from the subscriber on Tuesday the 13th inst. a negro man, well known by the name of Scott's JACK, about 5 feet 6 inches high, 35 years of age; the little finger on one of his hands is crooked--He is supposed to be lurking about Wilmington. The above reward will be paid for delivering the said Negro to me in Sampson County, or securing him in jail, so that I get him. Masters of vessels and others are hereby forwarned from harbouring, employing or carrying him away.<BR>DAN WILLIAMS.<BR>Sampson county, Nov. 27.--1w
