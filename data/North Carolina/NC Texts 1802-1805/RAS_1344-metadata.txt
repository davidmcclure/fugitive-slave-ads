Slave name(s): Sarah;Ned;John
Slaveholder: C. Massenburg
Transcript: THIRTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the Subscriber, living  near the Falls of Neuse, Wake county, three Negroes, viz.<BR>Sarah 30 years old, copper complexion, of the smaller size and spare made.<BR>Ned, a negro boy, black, 18 or 19 years old, 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, square built, narrow Face with small Eyes.<BR>John, a Boy, 26 years old, about 5 feet 7 inches high, black and slender built, a large Mouth with thick Lips, and very knock-kneed.<BR>I expect they will get into the neighbourhood of Windsor, Bertie county.  The above Reward and all reasonable Charges, will be given for delivering them to me in Wake county.<BR>C. MASSENBURG<BR>February 13.
