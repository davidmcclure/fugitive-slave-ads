Transcript: RAN AWAY<BR>FROM the Subscriber, living on Mark's Creek in Wake County, on the 23d of December last<BR>A MULATTO WOMAN<BR>About 17 years of age, stout and well made, named Cherry : has a considerable Welt quite across her Breast occasioned by Whipping ; she has also a Mark on one of her Legs, from the bite of a dog.<BR>She had on when she went off a coarse Muslin Dress.  She also took with her two Homespun Handkerhiefs[sic] and a Bonnet.<BR>It is understood she has past[sic] for a free Woman by the name of Amey.<BR>Whoever shall apprehend the above Woman and lodge her in any Goal so that she may be had again, shall receive Five Dollars Reward.<BR>WM.[William] SHAW.<BR>Feb 2, 1805
