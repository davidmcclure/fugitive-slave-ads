Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, during the Holydays[sic], a Negro Man named Jacob, about 24 or 25 years of age, 5 feet 4 or 5 inches high and well made, yellow complected, and has a scar on his forehead. The above reward will be paid for confining him in any jail on the continent so that I get him, or delivering him to me on Stump Sound.<BR>ALL masters of vessels and others are forewarned from harbouring, employing or carrying him away.<BR>ROBERT WILLIAMS.<BR>Wilmington, Jan 9. 1804.     366
