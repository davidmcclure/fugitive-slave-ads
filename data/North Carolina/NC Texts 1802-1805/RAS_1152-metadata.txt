Transcript: Negro Woman run away.<BR>RUN away from the City of Raleigh, on Monday last, a Negro Woman, 28 Years of Age, a low, stout made Wench; she has a Scar in her Forehead, and has lost her fore Teeth.  Her Name is Silvia.  She had with her a spotted Calico Habit, a flowered Muslin do. and a red Silk Petticoat.  She is supposed to have made for Halifax or Chapel-Hill, at each of which Places she has Relations.<BR>Whoever shall apprehend the said Negro, and lodge her in any Goal, or bring her to her Owner, Green Hill, of Raleigh aforesaid, shall receive a Reward of Ten Dollars.<BR>Aug. 18.
