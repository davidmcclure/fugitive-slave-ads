Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward,<BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 4th inst. a bright mulatto fellow by the name of Ben who was raised by Col Jas Wall of Greensville county, Virginia.  He is about 29 years of age, about 5 feet 10 inches high.  Had on and carried away with him the following clothes  a blue broad cloth coat, a mixt homespun do a white Marseilles waistcoat, and a blue negro cotten jacket, a pair of buff cassimere and a pair of mixt homespun breeches, and an [illegible] strip great coat -- He plays the fiddle very well and formerly played for a black dancing manner by the name of Hardy Artise.  His mother lives in Warren county.  A reward of ten dollars will be given for securing him in any gaol so that he may be had or the above reward for his head, and no questions asked.<BR>Thomas Hudson.<BR>Halifax county, Feb. 6.    36 4
