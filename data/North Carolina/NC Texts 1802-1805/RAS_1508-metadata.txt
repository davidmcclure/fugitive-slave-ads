Transcript: RAN away from the Plantation of the subscriber, about the 12 of August, a negro Woman, named SARAH-She is about 5 feet 3 or 4 inches high, talks good English, and is very free spoken. I expect she is aiming towards Fayetteville.<BR>A reward of Ten Dollars will be given to any person who will apprehend said negro and bring her to me.<BR>All masters of vessels are forbid harbouring or carrying her away.<BR>Mary Moor.<BR>September 5, 1804.      2w
