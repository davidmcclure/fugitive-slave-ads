Transcript: RAN AWAY<BR>FROM the subscriber on Tuesday the 20th April, a Negro Man named SAM, about 33 years old, dark complected, about six feet high, and spare made; has very thick ankles, and a lump on his breast; he took with him a variety of clothing; was raised in Virgina. Five dollars will be given to any one that will apprehend him in this county, and out of the county ten dollars.<BR>ROBERT FLEMING.<BR> Wake County,Neuse River,<BR>April 26, 1801.
