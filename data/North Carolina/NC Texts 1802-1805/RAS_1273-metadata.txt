Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN away from the subscriber's Plantation, at Colly Swamp, Bladen County, on Friday the 15th inst. a Negro man, named<BR>CATO,<BR>about five feet teen inches high, well made, has a scar on his face resembling the new moon, about twenty-five years of age. Whoever will apprehend the said Negro and put him in goal, or give notice to the owner so that he may get him again, shall be entitled to the above reward and all reasonable charges. All persons are cautioned against harbouring, employing, or carrying off the above mentioned Negro at their peril.<BR>Hugh Murphey.<BR>June 25, 1804          391--eow at.
