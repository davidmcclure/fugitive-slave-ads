Transcript: RAN AWAY from the subscriber's plantation on the Sound on the 8th instant, a Negro Women named FANNY, between 25 and 30 years of age, about 5 feet two or three inches high, of a yellowish complection[sic]--She was formerly the property of Doctor Fergus, deceased, and lately of Mr. L.A. Dorsey, from whom I purchased her--She has a husband belonging to Mr. Peter Maxwell named Ben. A reward of Ten Dollars will be given to any person who will bring her to me or deliver her to Mr. L.A. Dorsey in Wilmington.<BR>I will give a further reward of Fifty Dollars for information, which may lead to conviction, of her being harboured by a white person ; and twenty-five dollars if by a negro.<BR>WILLIAM G. BERRY.<BR>Sept. 20th.   tf.
