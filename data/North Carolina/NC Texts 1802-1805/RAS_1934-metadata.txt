Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, at the mouth of New-River, about the first of May last, a likely young Negro Woman named RACHAEL of middle size, very black, down look and speaks slow. She is the daughter of Spicer's Sue, who lives in Wilmington, and very probably is harboured about there.--I will give the above reward for having the said ran-away delivered to me or secured in jail so that I get her.--I will give a further reward of twenty dollars for information which may lead to conviction of her being harboured by a white person.<BR>GEOGRE W. NOBLE.<BR>July 12, 1803. - 3w.
