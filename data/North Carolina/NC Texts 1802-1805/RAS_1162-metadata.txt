Transcript: Forty Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY or stolen from the subscriber on the 12th inst. a Negro Man named STEPHEN, about 5 feet 9 or 10 inches high, 22 or 23 years old, of a common complexion, has no beard, thin lips very white teeth, nose sharp for a negro, with a pleasing countenance. He carried off with a him a negro cotton short coat, a pair of mixed overalls, one do. mixed with Turkey cotton, a cassimere jacket, a blue broad cloth coat, which has many large old fashioned buttons on it; also a mixed cloth coat and breeches, a quantity of thin clothing, a Dutch blanket, and two felt hats.<BR>I will give fifteen dollars for the Negro, or if confined in gaol that I get him again, and twenty five dollars if he has been stolen for the person who [illegible] him away.<BR>SOLOMON THOMPSON.<BR>Wake County, Jan. 17.
