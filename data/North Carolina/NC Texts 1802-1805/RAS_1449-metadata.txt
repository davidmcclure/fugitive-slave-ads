Transcript: 10 Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY on Tuesday the 13th November, a negroman known by the name of Scot's Jack, sometimes calls himself SIMON or ISAAC, about 5 feet 6 inches high, a black fellow, the little finger of one hand crooked, some marks of the whip on his back.  It is supposed he is about Roanoke, as he once belonged to old Col Thomas Pugh.  The above reward will be paid on delivering him to me, or securing him in gaol so that I get him again.  Masters of vessels and others are hereby forewarned from harbouring, employing or carrying him away.<BR>Daniel Williams.<BR>Sampson county, January 15.    33---38
