Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away on the 7th inst. a negro fellow named Prince, well known in the town of Wilmington, and its vicinity. For several years he has been in the service of Mr. John Lord, plays the Fiddle tolerably well, wears brown cloth pantaloons, with a short coat of the same. Whoever will deliver the said negro to me or secure him in jail, shall receive the above reward.<BR>JOHN D. TOOMER.<BR>Wilmington, April 16, 1805.--tf.
