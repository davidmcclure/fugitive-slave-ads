Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from Capt. Thomas Peyton, living in Cumberland County, on or about the 14th of October last,<BR>FIVE NEGRO SLAVES, viz.<BR>Charles, a well set fellow, about 27 years of age, has a scar on his head occasioned by a burn.<BR>Tryal, a small fellow, 22 years of age, slim made, 5 feet 6 inches high, has a scar on one leg occasioned by a hurt. <BR>Charlotte, a yellow wench, 21 years of age, and has a very woolly head.<BR>Hetty, a mulatto wench, 19 years of age, small and handsome-and a<BR>Girl Child about one year old, almost white.<BR>The above reward will be given for securing said slaves so that the owner get them, and all resonable charges paid; or five dollars for each--And a reward of twenty-five dollars will be paid for the discovery of any white person that may have concealed or conducted them away, upon proof sufficient to convict him.<BR>P. HODGES, for<BR>Capt. T. Peyten.<BR>November 16, 1804.    411--3w
