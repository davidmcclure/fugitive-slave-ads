Transcript: RANAWAY<BR>From the Subscriber on the 29th of October last,<BR>A MULATTO Fellow named Guy, about 36 Years of Age, 5 Feet 9 or 10 Inches high.  Both his Ears have been cropped, and he is branded on both Cheeks on one with an M. and the other an A. not very visible.  His Feet are small, but his big Toes uncommonly large, occasioned by Rheumatism, which makes him limp in his Walk.  he speaks rather short and rough, and his Countenance rather frowning and the White of his Eyes has a reddish Cast, He had on a Rorum Hat lined with black, an old Negro Cotton Jacket and Trowsers, and blue Surtout Coat, and a pair of old Shoe Boots.  he carried with him also some Homespun Cloaths.<BR>It is supposed his Wife is with him, who is of a copper black: her fore-finger on her right hand is stiff and bent,<BR>Whoever shall apprehend the said Mulatto, or his Wife, so that they be restored to their Owner, shall receive Ten Dollars Reward for either of them, if taken in the County of Wake, or more if taken at a distance and secured in any Goal, and Notice given to the owner.<BR>ISHAM HENDON.<BR>Near Raleigh, Nov. 16th.
