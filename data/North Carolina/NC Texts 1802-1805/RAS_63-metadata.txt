I purchased him last August of a Mr. Aaron Moore of Cumberland county, where he may at this time be lurking.<BR>I will give ten Dollars for said Fellow delivered to me, or lodged in Wake Jail, and a larger sum in proportion to a further distance.<BR>SAMUEL HIGH.<BR>Wake, March 20
