Transcript: THIRTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>Runaway from Col. Hamilton's, North Carolina, Guilford County, on the 7th Aug. last,<BR>Negro Isaac and his Wife Hannah,<BR>THE Fellow is very stout, weight nearly 200; his Wife has a Scar over her left Eye, they are both very black.  I expect they will endeavour to get back to Maryland, as they were lately brought from there.  Any Person securing said Negroes so that I get them, shall receive the above reward.<BR>JOSEPH ERWIN.<BR>September 12, 1802.
