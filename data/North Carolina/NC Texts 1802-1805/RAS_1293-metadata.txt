Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber on the 9th inst. a negro man named CAROLINA, 5 feet 6 or 7 inches high, 22 years of age, well made and of common complexion, and an artful, plausible fellow. It is probable he is gone to Wilmington, or to Lilliput, and may be harbored by the negroes in that quarter or at Town-Creek. Masters of vessels and others are hereby forewarned employing, or carrying him away. The above reward and all reasonable charges will be paid to any person who will apprehend said negro and deliver him to me, or lodge him any gaol in this state, so that I get him.<BR>JAMES FLEMING.<BR>Bladen County,<BR>Belmount, April 16, 1804.   381--tf.
