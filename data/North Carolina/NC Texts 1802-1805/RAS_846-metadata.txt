Transcript: Fifteen Dollars Reward,<BR>WILL be given for apprehending and committing to the nearest gaol, so that I may get them, a negro man and woman, by the names of BILLY and PATTY, between thirty and forty years of age, the man of a yellow complexion, a very down look ; the woman black and lusty made they ranaway on the night of the 26th at April last.  It is supposed they will make for Hanly county, Virginia, near the town of Morefiled, or one of the lower counties (the name not known) near Richmond, as they ahve been in such places.<BR>JOHN WALLIS.<BR>Iredell county, N. Carolina, August 11.    29 6p
