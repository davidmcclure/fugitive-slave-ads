Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward. <BR>RAN-AWAY from the Subscriber living in Moore County, about the 26th Oct. last, a Negro Fellow named Jim, about thirty years of age, five feet eight inches high, stout and well set, of rather a brow complexion--he is turning gray, and has lost the fore finger of his left hand, and the middle finger has been much hurt and stands crooken--he wore when he went off a ruffled shirt, a striped coat and overalls-he is an artful fellow, can write a tolerable hand, and will no doubt forge passes for himself or attempt to pass for a freeman. The above reward will be paid to any person that will deliver him to me in Moore County, or a reasonable reward for apprehending and securing him in any jail so that I get him again .<BR>BRADLY GARNER.<BR>Moore County, Nov. 1.
