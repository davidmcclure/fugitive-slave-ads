Slaveholder: John MacAlister
Transcript: RAN AWAY<BR>FROM the Subscriber living in Richmond County, near Mr. Gilrist's Bridge, on Drowning Creek the 22d of December last, a Negro Woman named Milly, black Complexion, large Mouth, her right Leg somewhat shorter than her left, which causes her to limp, when she walks. She will aim for the State of Virginia, by the way of Petersburg. She had a Calico Gown, with some white homespun Cloaths with her. A Reward of five dollars will be given to any Person than will confine her in Goal, so that I get her, besides all reasonable expence paid if brought to my house.<BR>JOHN MACALISTER.
