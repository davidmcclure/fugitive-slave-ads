Transcript: RAN-AWAY from the subscriber, on the 1st inst, a mulatto boy named TOM, about 15 years old, but small for his age-he was formerly the property of Mr. John Levingston, dec.--had on when went away a brownish great coat, striped homespun pantaloons and jacket, and white hat. He is well known in Town and and the neighbourhood, which renders a further description unnecessary.<BR>I will give a generous reward to any person who will bring him to me. <BR>Masters of vessels and others are cautioned against harbouring, employing or carrying said Boy away, at their peril.<BR>PETER CARPENTER.<BR>May 13.--3w.
