Transcript: NOTICE.<BR> RANAWAY from the subscriber living in Wake County and about nine miles north of Raleigh, a Mulatto Man named POMPEY, about 28 years old, though he has passed by the name of COOK EVANS at the University and its vicinity, he is about six feet high, of a thin vissage, Roman nose, also with a scar across the bridge of his nose, also a scar on the back of his left hand. I had him in custody at the University on the night of the 15th instant, but he makes his escape. I cannot describe his clothing as he will in all probability change his dress.--Any person delivering said mulatto to me in Wake county shall receive  fifteen dollars, and ten dollars if committed to any jail.<BR> WILLIAM SIMMS.<BR> January 17, 1805.
