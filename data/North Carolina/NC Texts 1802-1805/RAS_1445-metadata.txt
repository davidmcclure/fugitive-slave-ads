Transcript: 10 Dollars Reward.<BR>RUN-AWAY from the subscriber living in Wilkesborough, on Monday the 10th inst. a likely Mulatto Woman named Surry, about 30 or 31 years of age, remarkably well made, tolerably dark complexion, and when she walks steps remarkably short.  Had on when she went away nothing but a shift and petticoat, took two Indian blankets, and had either seven or eight dollars in money.  Any person or persons that will take up said mulatto woman, and return her to me, shall receive the above reward, and all necessary charges paid by me.<BR>JAS.[James} PATTON.<BR>Wilkesborough, Sept. 25. 1804.    41 3m.
