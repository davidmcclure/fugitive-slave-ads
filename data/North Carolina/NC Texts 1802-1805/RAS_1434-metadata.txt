Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUN away from the subscriber about the 15th September last a negro man named HALLEY, 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, 23 or 24 years of age, well made and of common complexion. He is well known in Wilmington and its neighbourhood. I will give the above reward for having him delivered to me or to the jailor of this town.<BR>Masters of vessels and all other persons are forbid harbouring, employing or carrying him away.<BR>ANTHONY B. TOOMER.<BR>Wilmington, October 18.
