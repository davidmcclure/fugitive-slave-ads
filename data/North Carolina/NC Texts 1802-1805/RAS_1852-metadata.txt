Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward. <BR>RAN away from the subscriber on the 28th December last, a Negro man named TOM. He is about 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high--country born--speaks slow and plain--is of a black complexion, and has a scar on the side of his nose. It is supposed he is about Mr. Swan's, on Catfish, or in Wilmington. All masters of vessels, and others, are hereby forewarned harbouring, employing or shipping said slave Tom.<BR>I will pay any person who will apprehend said negro and deliver him to me, or lodge him in Wilmington gaol so that I get him, the above reward and all reasonable charges.<BR>Aaron Morgan.<BR>Morgan Creek, Feb. 7 1804.    70tf
