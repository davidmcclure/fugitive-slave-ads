Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, about three months ago, a Negro Man named NED, about six feet high, twenty-eight or thirty years of age, is of a yellow complexion, and has thick lips.--I lately purchased him of Benjamin Sutton of this county, in which and likewise in Onslow he is well known--and I have reason to suppose he is now lurking about the Southwest branch of New-River.<BR>I will give the above reward for apprehending said Negro and delivering him to me or securing him in jail so that I get him.<BR>All persons are forbid harboring, employing or carrying him away.<BR>James Bludworth.<BR>New-Hanover, Sept. 25.
