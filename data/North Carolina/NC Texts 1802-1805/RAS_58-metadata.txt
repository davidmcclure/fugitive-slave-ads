Transcript: RAN AWAY,<BR>ABOUT the 20th of last January, from the Subscriber, living in Wake County, 7 Miles east of the Falls of Neuse, a Mulatto Man named Isham, 19 Years old, very well grown, whose Parents live with Philip Alston, of Warren, where it is likely he is lurking.  A generous Reward will be given for said Mulatto Man, if delivered to me, or secured in Jail, so that I get him again.<BR>THOMAS ALSTON.<BR>Wake County, April 1, 1803.
