Has a wife at Mrs. Hunleys.<BR>I will given[sic] Ten Dollars for his delivery, if taken within the State; and the above reward with reasonable expences, if taken out of the state.<BR>All masters of vessels and others, are forwarned from harbouring, employing, or carrying the said fellow away.<BR>John M. P. Crispin.<BR>Newbern, 8th, February, 1804.
