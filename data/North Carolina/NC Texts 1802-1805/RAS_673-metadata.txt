Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber, in the Month of September last, a NEGRO WOMAN, about 25 Years old, named BETTY.  She is very black, tolerably stout, and speaks pert.  She was raised by Archibaled M'Kay, Cumberland County, and was since owned by Neill M'Kay, near Fayetteville, and it is supposed she is lurking about that Place, having many Relations thereabout.<BR>Any Person who will apprehend the said Wench, and secure her in some Jail, shall receive the above Reward.<BR>JOSEPH PICKETT:<BR>Anson County, Oct. 28.
