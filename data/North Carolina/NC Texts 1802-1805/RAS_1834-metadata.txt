Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward,<BR>AND ALL REASONABLE CHARGES<BR>WILL be paid for taking up and delivering to me, or securing in Jail so that I get him, my negro man SAM, who ran away on the 13th inst. He is about 6 feet 2 inches high, very black, slim made, and all his toes were burnt off when a child. He had on when he ran away, a homespun shirt and mixed homespun overalls.<BR>All masters of vessels and others are cautioned against harbouring, employing or carrying him away.<BR>R.C. FARR.<BR>New-Hanover County, Sept. 20, 1804.   403 tf
