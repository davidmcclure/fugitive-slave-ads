Headline: Twenty-five dollars reward

Time period (decade): 1820-1829

Slave name(s): Elleck
Transcript: TWENTY-FIVE DOLLARS<BR>REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, about the first of January, without the least provocation, a negro fellow by the name of ELLECK, belonging to the estate of J. Wingate, deceased. I suppose him to be about 45 or 50 years of age; about 5 feet 7 or 8 inches high.--The fellow is well known in and about Wilmington.--I will give the above reward to any person that will deliver the said runaway to me, in Wilmington, or lodge him in any jail within the state so that I can get him. <BR>CHARLES NIXON. <BR>February 2.      tf
