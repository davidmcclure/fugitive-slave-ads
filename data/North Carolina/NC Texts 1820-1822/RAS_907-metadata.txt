Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber in Hillsborough, on Wednesday evening last, the 17th instant, a negro girl by the name of PRISCILLA, formerly the property of Wm.[William] Clifton, of said town.  She is about eighteen or nineteen years of age, black, and stout built; was brought from Wilmington, and will probably aim to get there again. The above reward, with all reasonable charges, will be given to any person who will deliver said negro to Thomas Dancy, sheriff of Orange county, N.C. or secure her in any jail so that I get her again.<BR>Benjamin Ellis.<BR>April 19  15--dv.
