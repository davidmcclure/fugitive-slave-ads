Transcript: $25 REWARD,<BR>BROKE JAIL on the night of the 15th inst; a white man by the name of WILLIAM STRONG, thin vissage, light complexion, and tolerable large whiskers, He had on a blue surtout coat, which reached down to his ancles[sic]. Also a negro man named PARIS, who is about 40 years old, 5 feet, 8 inches high, large face, and high cheek bones and very bushy hair. The above persons were confined in the Jail of this County for capital offences. I will give a reward of twenty-five dollars, for their apprehension and delivery to me in Wilmington.<BR>CHARLES B. MORRIS, Jailor.<BR>July 21.--(J. 21.)        tf.
