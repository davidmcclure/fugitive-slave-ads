Transcript: Twenty-Five Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber, on the 18th instant, a Mulatto woman named<BR>PENNY.<BR>She is about 23 years of age, 5 feet 2 or 3 inches high, has a very light complexion, straight hair, and a small scar on her chin.  It is believed that she was enticed away by her husband, a Negro man named RUNNY, who absconded at the same time.  Said Runny belongs to Jonathan Rouse; and is between five and six feet high, stout made, weighs about 180, has a scar on his breast about an inch and a half long, and a very thick beard; he can read and write tolerably well, and it is probable that they will attempt to pass for free persons.<BR>The above reward will be given to any person who will apprehend and confine said PENNY in jail so that I get her.<BR>JAMES WIGGINS.<BR>Falling Creek, Lenoir County,<BR>December 23, 1820.     145 2w
