Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber in August last, a Negro Woman named LUCY--she is thick set, and has a full face. She formerly belonged to Thomas Hill, deceased, and lately to Mr. Green. I have understood that she is generally in the neighborhood of Long Creek and the Big Bridge. I will give the above reward for her delivery to me in Wilmington, or to the jailor of New Hanover county.<BR>LOUIS PAGET.<BR>March 24             tf.
