Transcript: 100 Dollars Reward<BR>WILL be given for apprehending and delivering to me, or confine in jail so that I get him, my Negro Fellow<BR>HANSE.<BR>who ranaway on the 3d inst. without any provocation whatever. He formerly belonged to the estate of Col. Joshua Creecy, dec'd; and is about 5 feet 10 or 11 inches high, pretty black, very stout, and about 24 years of age. I will give the above reward if taken up without this State, and secured as aforesaid; or 25 Dollars if taken up within this State.<BR>Masters of vessels and others, are hereby forwarned from harboring, employing, or carrying him off, at their peril.<BR>James Coffield.<BR>Chowan, Nov. 7, 1821.
