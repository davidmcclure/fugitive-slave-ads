Transcript: 100 Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY on the 1st inst. my Negro Man<BR>ANTHONY, about five feet 8 or 9 inches high, a carpenter by trade, of a black complexion, smooth skin, round shouldered, and about 22 years of age,<BR>Also<BR>my Mulatto Man<BR>STEPHEN,<BR>about five feet 8 or 10 inches high, of a yellow complexion, well made, has a fine look, and generally keeps his hair well combed and plaited, about 28 or 30 years of age, has worked for some time past as a carpenter, and it is probable he may pass himself as a free man and make for the North: their dress not recollected.<BR>The above reward will be paid for the apprehension of the above Negroes, or $50 for either of them if delivered to me, or secured in any jail so that I get them again.<BR>All persons are hereby cautioned from harboring or employing them: and masters of vessels are strictly forbidden from harboring or carrying them off at their peril.<BR>Snoad B. Skinner<BR>Lake Phelps, Washington County}<BR>N.C. 20th Jan, 1821.}
