Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward<BR>WILL be given for apprehending and delivering to me, or confining in jail so that I get her again, my Negro Woman<BR>FLORA,<BR>late the property of John Lanston, and formerly belonging to Dr. Beasley. She is about 33 or 34 years of age, tall and very slender, and of a yellowish cast. She is no doubt lurking in or about Edenton. I will give the above reward if delivered or secured as aforesaid, and all reasonable expences paid.<BR>Josiah Skinner.<BR>Perquimans, Dec. 20, 1820.
