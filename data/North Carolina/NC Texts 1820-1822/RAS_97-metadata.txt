Transcript: RAN AWAY<BR>FROM the MISSES PULLIAM, on the 20th instant, their Negro Woman LYDIA.-- She carried off with her a homespun checked light-ground frock, a striped muslin do. a white cambric do. frilled, a light calico do. one bed, two sheets and a blanket.  If any one will be kind enough to take her up they will be rewarded for thier trouble.<BR>Lydia is tall, yellow complected, rather a dead-looking eye, stoops in her shoulders, her teeth not good and has lost part of her back ones, as may be seen when she laughs-- she is very artful, and can tell a good tale, so as to be believed by those who are not well acquainted with her.  She is about 30 years of age.  All persons are forwarned from harboring her.<BR>January 26.    66tf
