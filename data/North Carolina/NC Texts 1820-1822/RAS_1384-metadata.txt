He has a wife at Mr. Gaston's Mills, where he is probably lurking.  The above reward will be given for his delivery to me in Newbern.  Masters of vessels and others are cautioned from carrying away or harbouring said Negro, under the penalty of the law.<BR>RACHEL M'CABE.<BR>Newbern, Dec. [December] 8, 1821--194tf.
