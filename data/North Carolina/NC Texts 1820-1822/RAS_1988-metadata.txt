Transcript: 10 Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from me in Pitt County, the 18th of August last, my Negro Woman PEGGY, whom I purchased of Josiah M'Keil, near Edenton. She is far advanced into pregnancy, and will in all probability, try to get back to the neighborhood of Edenton. She she be taken up and committed to jail, application must be made to James Skinner, Esq. of Chowan, who pay the reward and jail fees.<BR>Lewis Bond.<BR>September 12, 1821.
