Transcript: 40 dollars reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber on the 11th January last a negro man named<BR>Abram,<BR>about five feet 5 inches high; rather knock kneed, has a scar on the right side of his neck and a little turn in his right eye, about 25 years old--He formerly belonged to James Green of Greenville, and was sold by M. Mordecai to me.<BR>Also, on the 1st Sept. last a negro man named<BR>Jim,<BR>about 37 years old, yellow complexion, spare made, has a little scar under his right eye, is about five feet 10 inches high-He was raised in the Hollaway family in Hyde county, & sold by John Hollaway to Weeks Chapman, and by W.C. to me.<BR>I will give the above reward to any person who will them to me, or twenty dollars for either, or $20 each, for securing them in any of the jails in Pitt, Beaufort, Hyde, or any of the adjoining county jails.<BR>JOHN BOYD.<BR>BOYD'S FERBY,<BR>Pit county, Oct. 11.     tf279
