Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR> RUNAWAY or carried off by some person unknown to me from my plantation on the 22d. of December last, two Negro Men named Sam and Isaac.--Sam about 34 years old dark Complexion and gray hair supposed to be five feet eleven inches high his teeth stands Scattering, lisps in talking. Isaac aged 22 or 23 years 5 feet 9 inches high, dark complexion has a mark on one of his ears occasioned by a knife, a scar under his jaw by a rising. The above  reward will be given if said negroes is delivered to me at my plantation in Cumberland county, North Carolina, if carried off by an person fifty dollars will given for apprehending the said person or persons so that I bring them to justice.<BR> JAMES BATTLE.<BR>Cumberland County, Jan. 1. 1821
