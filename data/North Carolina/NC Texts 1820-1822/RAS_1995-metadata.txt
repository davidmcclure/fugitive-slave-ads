[William] Littlejohn, of Edenton. He has a wife at Mr. John Hobbell's, where it is likely he is lurking. I will give the above reward if delivered to me, or confined in jail so that I get him, and all reasonable expences paid.<BR>John M. Bond.<BR>Chowan, Oct. 11, 1820.
