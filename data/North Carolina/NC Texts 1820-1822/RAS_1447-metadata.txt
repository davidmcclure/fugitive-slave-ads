Transcript: 10 dollars reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber's plantation on the 23d[?] ult. a negro boy named<BR>AYRS;<BR>He is 19 years old; five feet 4 or 5 inches high; thick set; yellow complected; and has a down look. It is supposed that he is lurking about Washington or its neighborhood. The above reward will be paid to any person who will deliver said boy to me at Newport, Tyrrel County, or secure him in any jail so that I get him again.<BR>All persons and particularly masters of vessels, are hereby forwarned from harboring, employing, or carrying him away, under the penalty of the law.<BR>JOHN MARRINER.<BR>Tyrrel county, May 1, 1821-tf36[?]
