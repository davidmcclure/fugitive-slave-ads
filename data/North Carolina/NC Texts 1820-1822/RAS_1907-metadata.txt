Slave alias: Sam Overton

Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber in Person County on the 19th inst a negro man named SAM, about 20 years of age, five feet, ten inches high, slender made, and very active.  He is a very light mulatto, not more than one-fourth negro blood.  There is no doubt but that he will attempt to pass for a free man; he is a Hatter by trade, and makes very good Hats.  His eye lashes appear very full and falls over his eyes as if they were puffed.  The above Reward will be given to any person taking up said boy and securing him in any Jail, or delivering him to me.<BR>S. COCHRAN.<BR>Sept 28, 1821    51--4
