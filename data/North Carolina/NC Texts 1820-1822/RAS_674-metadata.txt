Transcript: Twenty Dollars reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber, living in Currituck, Hyde County, on the 17th inst. a dark mulatto boy named<BR>Bill Morris alias<BR>Bill Kurdet.<BR>He is nearly or quite six feet high; slender built; has large and black eyebrows, stammers in his speech; about 22 years of age; has a mother living in Washington, and his father (Richard Morris) lives on Clubfoot's Creek, below Newbern. He may possibly attempt to pass as free.<BR>The above reward will be paid to any person who will deliver said boy to me, or secure him in any jail so that I get him again.<BR>All Masters of vessels and others, are cautioned against, harboring, employing, or carrying him off under the penalty of the law.    ROBERT SILVERTHORN<BR>Washington, Sept. 21, 1821--3w326.<BR>The Editors of the Newbern Centinel will please insert the above 5 weeks in their paper, and send their bill to this Office for payment.
