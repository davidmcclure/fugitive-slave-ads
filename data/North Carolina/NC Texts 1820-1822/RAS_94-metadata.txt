Transcript: RAN AWAY<BR>FROM Governor Branch's plantation in Wake county, on the evening of the 25th ultimo, JOHN, a mulatto lad about 19 years of age. He is about 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, fair complexion, slender made and moves awkwardly; he had on when he went away a dark brown cloth coat, blue cassimere pantaloons, light waistcoat and black hat; but has probably changed his dress, as he carried other clothes with him, tied up in a red handkerchief.<BR>Five Dollars reward will be paid to any person who will deliver him to the Subscriber, 11 miles west of Raleigh; or lodge him in Jail.<BR>JOHN ELLIS, jr.[junior] Overseer.<BR>March 1, 1821.
