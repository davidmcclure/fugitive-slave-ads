Transcript: 35 Dollars Reward.<BR>WILL be given for apprehending and delivering to me, or securing in jail so that I get them, my two Negro Fellows named<BR>Peter and Sam.<BR>Peter is about 40 years of age, somewhere about 6 feet high, slim, and pretty black, and well known in the neighborhood by the name of Peter Halsey. Sam is about 5 feet high, pretty thick set, and much pock-broken, and about 30 years of age. Peter has a wife at Mr. Bozmin's in Edenton, where he is probably is frequently lurking. Sam has a wife belonging to  Mrs. Leary, who is now living with Mr. Joseph Norcom, sen. -I will give 20 dollars for the apprehension and delivery of Peter as above; and 15 Dollars for Sam, if delivered or secured as aforesaid.<BR>Edmund Benbury<BR>Chowan, Nov. 14, 1820.
