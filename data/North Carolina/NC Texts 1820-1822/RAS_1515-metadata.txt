Transcript: RAN AWAY from the Subscriber on the 16th inst. a negro man named<BR>Ceasar,<BR>formerly the property of H. Smaw, dec'd. He is about 26 years old, quite black, 5 feet 5 or 6 inches high and tolerably well made. He is so well known, that a more particular description of him is deemed unnecessary. It is supposed he is lurking about Washington where he has many relatives, or about the plantation of Wm. Vines, Esq. at the mouth of Bath Creek, at which place he was reared. A suitable reward will be paid to any person who will deliver said fellow to me, or secure him in jail so that I get him again.<BR>Masters of vessels and others are forwarned from harboring, employing, or carrying him off under the penalty of the law.<BR>REBECCA SMAW.<BR>Jan. 18        tf 342
