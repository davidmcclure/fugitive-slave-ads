Transcript: Fifty Dollars Reward.<BR> RAN away from the subscriber, at Charlotte Court-House, Mecklenburg county, N.C. a Negro Boy by the name of SIMON; dark complexion, stout made, and 5 feet 7 or 8 inches high. He speaks low when spoken to. It is supposed that he will make towards the county of Prince William, Virginia, as he was purchased in that county.  I will give the above reward if the said negro is delivered to Isaac Wilie, Concord, Cabarrus county, or 25 dollars if secured in any jail, and information given, so that I get him again.<BR> March 24, 1821.<BR>EVAN WILIE.
