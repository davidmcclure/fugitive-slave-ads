Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR> RUNAWAY from the subscriber, living in Granville county on the 15th December last a negro man by the name of DICK, formerly the property of Mr. Marmaduke Jefferys of Franklin county; supposed to be about forty five or fifty years of age; dark complexion, and about six feet high. He was raised in Halifax county, North Carolina, he is no doubt lurking about the plantation of Samuel Wester of said county. I will give the above reward and pay all reasonable expences for his delivery home.<BR> DAVID S. GOODLOE.<BR>Jan. 3, 1821.
