Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>Ran away from the subscriber on Monday, the 16th inst. a Negro Boy named Harry; about 25 years of age, 5 feet 9 inches high, stout made, very black complexion, handsome features, his clothing not recollected.  I suspect he is lurking about the confines of Mecklenburg and Cabarrus.  I will give any person ten dollars who will deliver him to me in Cabarrus county, or confine him in Concord jail, so that I get him again.<BR>SAMUEL W. BURNS.<BR>Rocky River, Cabarrus Co.<BR>July 12, 1821.    3wt62
