Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward<BR>FOR apprehending and delivering to us, twelve miles above Raleigh, on the Haywood road, George, a wel[sic] known Blacksmith belonging to Massinburg's estate.--George went off on the 23 instant, and has probably gone to Franklin, where he has many acquaintances. Or we will give five dollars if secured in any jail within the State, so that we get him again. We forwarn all persons from harboring or dealing with him as we shall put the law in force against any thus offending.<BR>H. C. WIATT & Co.<BR>Oct 26th, 1820<BR>40--tf.
