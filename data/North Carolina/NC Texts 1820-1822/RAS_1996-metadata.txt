Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward<BR>WILL be given for apprehending and delivering to me, or confining in jail so that I get him again a Negro Fellow named<BR>MINGO,<BR>the property of Josiah Coffield. He went off without the smallest shadow of provocation that I know of. He is about 5 feet 10 or 11 inches high, pretty stout, tolerable black, and about 45 years of age. He is a blacksmith by trade, and has brothers living at Josiah Hurdel's, in this County, where it is probable he is lurking. I will give the above reward if taken up within this County, or 50 Dollars if taken up without and delivered or secured as above.<BR>James Coffield.<BR>Chowan, Oct. 21, 1820.
