Transcript: 200 Dollars Reward<BR>WILL be given for delivering to me, or confining in jail so that I get them again, my two Negro Fellows.<BR>Harry & Jack,<BR>who ran away upwards of six months ago. Harry is about 5 feet 6 inches high, light complexion, pretty well made, and about 18 or 19 years of age. He is supposed to be lurking in the County of Pasquotank. Jack is about 5 feet 8 or 10 inches high, pretty black thin visage, and about 23 years of age. He is supposed to be lurking in Durant's Neck, where he has a wife belonging to Mr. Jacock's. The above reward will be given for both if delivered or secured as aforesaid, or 100 Dollars for either, and all reasonable expences paid.<BR>Richard H. Blount<BR>Bluff-Point, Dec. 22, 1820.
