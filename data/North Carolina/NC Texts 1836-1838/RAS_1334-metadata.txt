Time period (decade): 1830-1839

Date of departure: 1836-12-06
Presumed or intended destination: Slocomb's Creek (N.C.);New Bern (N.C.)
Slave name(s): Dempsey
Slaveholder: Dexter Gibson
Transcript: THIRTY DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RANWAY from the Subscriber on the 6th of October last, a negro boy named DEMPSEY, aged 26 years, yellowish complexion,--5 feet 4 or five inches--stutters in his speech, is well set and likely. He has a wife on Slocomb's Creek, is well acquainted in Newbern, and may probably be concealed at one or other of those places. The above reward will be paid for his confinement in any jail in this State, so that I get him. All persons are cautioned against harbouring or carrying off said negro under the penalty of the law. <BR>DEXTER GIBSON.  <BR>December 21st 1836.
