Transcript: $20 REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber on the 13th instant a likely NEGRO MAN, aged 22 years, named EDMOND. He is a bright Mulatto speaks quick, and is about 5 feet 6 or 7 inches high. I will give the above reward to any person who will deliver him to me, or confine him in any jail within the State, so that I get him. His wife left with him. She is a free bound Mulatto, named GATSEY PITTMAN. I will give TEN DOLLARS for her apprehension or delivery to me at this place.<BR>DEXTER BURNS.<BR>Onslow Court House, Dec. 14th, 1837.<BR>101 3m
