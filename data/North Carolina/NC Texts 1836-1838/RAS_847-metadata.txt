Transcript: Fifteen Dollars Reward,<BR>WILL be given for the apprehension and delivery to me in this place, of a negro man named SAMPSON. He formerly belonged to Angus Ray, Esq.[Esquire], of this County, and is now the property of Captain Christian Bringle of Rowan. Said Negro is about 40 years old, 5 feet 10 inches high, copper colour, supposed to be lurking about the residence of Angus Ray, Esq.[Esquire], or the neighborhood, where he has a wife.<BR>R. COCHRAN, Jr.<BR>Fayetteville, March 27, 1838.<BR>86 2w.
