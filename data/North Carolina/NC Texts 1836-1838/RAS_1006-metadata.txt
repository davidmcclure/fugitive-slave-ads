Transcript: Fifty Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber's Plantation, ten miles above Elizabeth-town, on the 25th of September, a negro fellow named ALICK.<BR>Said negro is about twenty-five years of age, well grown, and has light complection[sic], with broad flat features. He is about the usual stature, and strong built. He carried or wore away, a blue cloth coat and pantaloons, and fur hat, besides some other negro clothing. He has heretofore been hired ten miles below Fayetteville, and in different parts of Bladen County. The above reward will be paid for his apprehension and delivery to me if found without the limits of the County, or Twenty five Dollars if found within it. Any information respecting him will be thankfully received.<BR>HEMAN H ROBINSON.<BR>Elizabeth-town, Bladen County, Oct 13, 1837<BR>63-2w.
