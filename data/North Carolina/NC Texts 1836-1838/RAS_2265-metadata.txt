Transcript: $20 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on Sunday 25th inst. my Negro Woman Betsey Merrick, with her three children, Edward, Margaret Ann and Caroline. Said Betsey is of dark complection[sic], low stature, speaks very slow, and has a downcast look; her children are Mulattoes, formerly the property of Charles Nixon. Her youngest is an infant.<BR>The above reward will be given on her delivery to me, or being lodged in any jail where I can get her and her children.<BR>W. A. LANGDON.<BR>June 30th, 1837.<BR>25--t-f.
