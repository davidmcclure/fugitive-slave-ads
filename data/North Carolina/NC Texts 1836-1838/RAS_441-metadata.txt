Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward,<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber a Negro Boy Jim, aged about 22 years, five feet 6 or 8 inches high, of Black complexion, with thick lips, and weighs about 165, and has a little lameness in his walk. The last time he was heard of, was near Smithfield, on his way to Raleigh.<BR>The above Reward will be given for his apprehensioa[sic] and conflinement[sic] in any Jail in the State, so that I can get him again.<BR>JAMES A. KELLY.<BR>Cumberland County, March 2, 1837.--526w<BR>The Raleigh Standard and Newbern Sentinel will insert the above for 6 weeks and forward their accounts to this office for payment.
