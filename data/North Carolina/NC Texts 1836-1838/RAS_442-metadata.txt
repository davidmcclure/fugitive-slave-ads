Transcript: 20 Dollars Reward. <BR>    RAN AWAY from the subscriber, some time in the month of June last, his negro man SAM, about five feet six or eight inches high, slender made, quite black, has a down look, red eyes, and thin jaws; said slave is between thirty-five years and forty years of age, and is expected to spend a part of his time on Beaver Creek, in the neighbourhood of the Reynolds, and part in Onslow County, about John M. Francks's. The above reward will be given to any person who will deliver him to me at my residence, or secure him in any jail so that I get him. <BR>HENRY RHODES.<BR>Tuckahole, Jones Co. 28th Oct. 1836.
