Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber, in July last, his negro man TOBY about 23 years of age, little yellow in his complexion, and about 6 feet high.  He has a small scar on his forehead, which may be discovered on examination, tho' he has nearly outgrown it.  When agitated or alarmed, he is inclined to stutter.  No other marks recollected.<BR>The above Reward will be paid for his delivery to me, or his confinement in Jail, so that I get him again.<BR>WILLIAM HODGE.<BR>Wake County, Nov. 21, 1836.    2 tf
