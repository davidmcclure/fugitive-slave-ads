Transcript: $20 REWARD<BR>RAN AWAWY from the Subscriber living in Nash county, 18 miles South of Court House, on the Smithfield Road, his Negro man<BR>HARRY,<BR>About 20 years of age, a little inclined to yellow complexion, and when he walks steps with one foot at right angles to the other.  He stoops when walking, and has a full flaring eye, the white of which is very conspicuous. He went away sometime last February, and has been probably carried out of the State.<BR>I will give $20 for his apprehension, if taken out of the State, and $10, if caught within the limits thereof.<BR>SION SANDERS.<BR>Nash county, June 6.    31
