Transcript: $100 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, on Tuesday night the 6th of September last, my negro man named<BR>TOM,<BR>Aged about 56 years, 6 feet high and thin visaged. Tom is no doubt lurking in the neighborhood, as he has killed and injured my cattle, hogs, sheep, &c. As Tom is a malicious negro and will certainly resist an attempt to take him, I will give the above reward for his apprehension if taken in any manner and confined in jail.<BR>Elizabeth Cromwell.<BR>October 17, 1836.<BR>41
