JINSEY is a stout black negro girl, with large white eyes, about 22 years old--she is so well known that a further description is deemed unnecessary.  She is probably lurking in the neighborhood of Hinton's Mills.<BR>ABRAHAM HESTER.<BR>N.B. I will give the highest cash prices for young negroes. A.H.<BR>Wake county, Nov 3    105-4tp
