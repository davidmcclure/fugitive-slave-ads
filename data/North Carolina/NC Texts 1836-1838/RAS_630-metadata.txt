Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>Ranaway from the Subscriber, a negro boy JIM, aged about 22 years, 5 feet 6 or 8 inches high, of black complexion, with thick lips, and weighs about 165, and has a little lameness in his walk.  The last time he was heard of, was near Smithfield, on his way to Raleigh.<BR>The above reward will be given for his apprehension and confinement, in any Jail in this State, so that I can get him again.<BR>JAMES A. KELLY.<BR>Cumberland Co. March 2, 1837.    123 5t.
