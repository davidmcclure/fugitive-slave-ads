Slave alias: Ben Fox

Transcript: 200 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, about three years ago, a certain negro man named BEN, commonly known by the name of Ben Fox. He is about 5 feet 5 or 6 inches high, chunkey made, yellow complexion, and has but one eye. Also, one other negro, by the name of RIGDON, who ran away on the 8th of this month. He is stout made, tall, and very black, wih large lips.<BR>I will give the above reward of one hundred dollars for each of the above negroes to be delivered to me or confined in the jail of Lenoir or Jones county, or for the killing of them, so that I see them. Masters of vessels and all others, are cautioned against harbouring, employing, or carrying them away under the penalty of the law.<BR>W.D. COBB. <BR>Nov. [November] 12, 1836.
