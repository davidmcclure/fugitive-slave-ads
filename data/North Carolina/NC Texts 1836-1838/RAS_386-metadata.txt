Transcript: $50 Reward,<BR>WILL be paid for the apprehension and confinement in Jail so that I get him again, of my negro man BRISTER, who is supposed to have been conveyed away to the South by one Joseph Jeanerette. Brister is a dark mulatto, about 50 years of age, about 5 feet 8 or 9 inches high, stout built, has a scar on his left temple, plays on the fiddle. Jeanerette is about 35 or 40 years of age, dark complexion and dark hair, very stout about 5 feet 10 inches high. It is expected that Jeanerette would avail himself of the first opportunity of selling the negro. They went off on the 29th of January, 1838. Any information will be thankfully received by the Subscriber at Meare's Post Office, Columbus county, N. C.<BR>MARGARET JEANERETTE.<BR>February 1, 1838.<BR>79-3wpd
