Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber on the 21st inst., a negro boy named SCOTT, yellow complected, about 25 years old, 5 feet 10 inches high, and a very active fellow--he took a number of good clothes with him and a fine fur hat. He was raised by Dr. Green Porter, and will no doubt make either for that neighborhood, the Steel Creek neighborhood, or to Chester District, S.C. where he has relations. Any person taking up said boy and lodging him in any jail, so that I can get him, shall receive the above reward.<BR>ROBT.[Robert] WILSON.<BR>April 25, 1838.<BR>t97
