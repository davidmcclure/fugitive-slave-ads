Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR>THE above reward will be paid for the delivery to me of my negro man BOB who ran away from me nearly twelve months ago. He is believed to be lurking in the neighborhood or about Green Spring, where he has a wife. BOB, is about five feet high, black complexion, stout made and about twenty eight years of age. <BR>  All persons are hereby forewarned against harbouring, employing, or carrying away said negro, under the penalties of the law made and provided in such cases.<BR>GRAVES FONVILLE. <BR>Bachelor's Creek, 8th Jan. 1838.
