Transcript: 100 Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, on Friday the 16th December last, his Negro man JACK, or as he calls himself, John Cox. Jack is about 34 years old, stout made, 5 feet 10 or 11 inches high, black complexion, and generally wore large whiskers. Having been employed for a number of years, as a servant in the subscriber's Washington Hotel, in New Bern, he is generally well known. The above reward will be given to any person who will confine him in jail in New Bern, or seventy five dollars, if confined in any other jail in North Carolina.<BR>Masters of vessels and all others are cautioned against harbouring or carrying him away, under the penalty of the law.<BR>JOSEPH BELL.<BR>24th March, 1837. 447tf.<BR>Mr. George Banders, of New Bern, is authorised, and will pay the above reward to any person who will apprehend and secure in jail the above named negro man.
