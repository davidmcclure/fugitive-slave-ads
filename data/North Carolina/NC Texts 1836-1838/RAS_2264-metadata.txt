Transcript: $10 Reward<BR>WILL be given for the delivery (to me in Whitesville, N.C.) of a certain negro girl, by the name of Celia, formerly the property of Hugh Murphy, deceased. Said girl was hired by the Subscriber at February Term of the County Court of Bladen County, N.C. since which time she has not been heard of. I will give a reward of 50 Dollars for proof to convict any white person or harbouring the said negro. She is supposed to be lurking about Elizabeth, Bladen County.<BR>CHESTER ROCKWELL.<BR>Whitesville, April 15th, 1837. 15 3-t.
