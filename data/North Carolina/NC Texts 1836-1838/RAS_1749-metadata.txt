Transcript: Runaway.<BR>FROM the subscriber in this county on the 7th inst., a negro man by the name of Wilie. Said Wilie is of a light yellow complexion, about 5 feet 6 inches high, and about twenty six years of age, had on when he went away a wollen[sic] round about, cotton pantalloons[sic] and a white fur hat. Said negro man is supposed to be lurking about the lower end of this county, on the east side of Cape Fear River, and also occasionally about the Town of Fayetteville. I will give a reward of Twenty Dollars for delivering him to me.<BR>M. EDWARDS.<BR>September 27.<BR>30--aw.
