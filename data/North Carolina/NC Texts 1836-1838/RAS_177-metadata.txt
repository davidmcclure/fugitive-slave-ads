Transcript: $10 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY, on the 17th March last, a negro boy named BOB, who belongs to Mr. Robeson of Bladen County, and was hired to me for one year. He is about 20 years of age, 5 feet 5 or six inches high, stout built, and of a dark mulatto complexion. He was raised in Bladen, and has lived in Fayetteville. I will give the above reward for his apprehension and confinement in jail, if information is given to me so that I get him again.<BR>ROBERT MATHERS.<BR>Wilmington, N C., April 7, 1837.<BR>36 3w
