Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber a negro man known as FRANK PILOT; he formerly belonged to the late William Blackledge, Esq.[Esquire],  was raised in Craven county, and lived for some time in Newbern, where he is no doubt now lurking. He is five feet eight inches high, dark complexion, and about 50 years old, has rather been free since 1829--is now my property, as heir at law of his last owner, Samuel Ralston, dec. I will give the above reward if he is taken and confined in any jail so that I get him. <BR>SAMUEL RALSTON. <BR> Pactolus, Pitt County, 13th Dec. 1837.  485tf
