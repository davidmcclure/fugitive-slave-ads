Transcript: 5 Dollars Reward<BR>RAN away, on the fifth of September from the residence of Paisley Nelson, a NEGRO WOMAN, with an infant Girl in the arms, the property of John Hart.  Barbary is a very black woman, quite tall and slender, with very white teeth, and shows a good deal of the white of her eyes.  A reward of five dollars will be given by the subscriber for the apprehension of said slave and child, and all reasonable charges paid.<BR>John M. Paul.<BR>September 21.  33--
