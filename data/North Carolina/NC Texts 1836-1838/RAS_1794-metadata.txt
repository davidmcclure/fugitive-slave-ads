Transcript: Stop the Runaway!<BR>RAN away from the subscriber, on Friday the 16th instant, a negro boy by the name of GRIFFIN.  He was about 21 years of age, five feet nine or ten inches high, and of a dark complexion; has a full face and projecting lips, and speaks very quick.  No prominent mark recollected except a scar on one of his legs.  He was raised in Virginia, and purchased of Godwin & Templeman.  A liberal compsenation will be given to any one who will apprehend said boy and deliver him to me, or confine him in any jail so that I can get him.<BR>JOHN A. FAUCETT.<BR>Hillsborough, March 23  13--3w
