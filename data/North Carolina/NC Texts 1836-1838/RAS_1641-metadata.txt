Transcript: RANAWAY<BR>FROM the Subscriber, on the 24th inst a negro girl named DILLY, about 18 or 20 years old, yellow complected, & somewhat freckled.  She took her clothes and bed-clothes with her, and I have no doubt of the fact that she is concealed in or about Raleigh.  I will give a handsome Reward for her confinement in Wake Jail.  She left without provocation, having been always well treated.<BR>LEWEY HUTCHINS.<BR>Wake Co., Jan. 29, 1838.     13
