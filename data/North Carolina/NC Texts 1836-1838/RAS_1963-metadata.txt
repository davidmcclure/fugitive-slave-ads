Transcript: Ten Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY on the 10th inst., from the subscriber, a negro man named Ned. Said negro is about 24 or 25 years old, and is about 5 feet 10 inches high; copper color; has snaggle teeth, and a pleasant countenance; remarkably quick spoken; clothing not recollected. Said negro is doubtless lurking about in this county, or one of the adjacent counties. I will give the above reward to any person who will apprehend Ned, and deliver him to the rubscriber[sic] (residing 8 miles south of Greensboro') or commit him to any jail.<BR>WM.[William] HANNER.<BR>Guilford county, Jan. 20, 1937[1837]. 1tf.
