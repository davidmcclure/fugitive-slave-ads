Transcript: $20 REWARD.<BR>RAN away from the subscriber on the 18th of November last, my negro man ABSALOM, formerly the property of Mrs. Will Davis, on the Sound, and where it is supposed he may be lurking a part of the time. It is presumed that he is a part of the time on the Northeast River, in the neighborhood of Rocky Point, and Spring Garden. Said fellow is about 55 or 60 years of age, about five feet seven or eight inches in height, thick set, of yellow complexion, wears his hair long and bushy, and speaks rather low and thick.<BR>I will give the above reward for his apprehension and delivery to me, or to the jailer in this place.<BR>ISAAC NORTHROP.<BR>Wilmington, Jan. 15th, 1838.<BR>165[?] tf
