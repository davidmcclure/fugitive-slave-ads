Transcript: $20 REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on Monday the 24th of April, a negro man by the name of JACOB and known about Fayetteville as McAlister's Jacob, he has for the last two or three years lived about Fayetteville, and worked mostly at the Carpenter's trade. As it is believed that he will range where he is known, it is not necessary to give any further description of him, but say in conclusion that the above reward will be cheerfully paid to any person that will deliver him to the subscriber one mile from Glarednon Bridge, where he left without any cause whatever. All persons are cautioned against harboring said negro.<BR>WILLIAM CADE.<BR>May 20, 1837.<BR>42 3w.
