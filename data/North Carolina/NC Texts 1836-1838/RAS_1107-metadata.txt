Slave name(s): Ulysses

Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RANWAY from the subscriber about the 5th December last, a negro man named ULYSSES, about 5 feet, six inches high, light complexion and by trade a Plaisterer. As he can read and write he may have forged a pass or free papers, and perhaps attempt to get to the north. I will give a reward of Twenty  Dollars for his apprehension within the limits of this State, and confined so that I get him, or Fifty Dollars if taken out of the State. <BR>Masters of vessles and all other persons are forewarned from harboring or employing said negro under the penalty of the law. <BR>O.S. DEWEY. <BR>January 18, 1837.   3tf
