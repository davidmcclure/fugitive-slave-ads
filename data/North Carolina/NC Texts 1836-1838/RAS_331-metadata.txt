Transcript: $25 Reward.<BR>RANAWAY in April last, a negro man named JACOB, belonging to the estate of Alex'r[Alexander] McAlister, dec'd[deceased]. He is about 42 or 43 years of age, about 5 feet 8 inches high, stout built, light complexion, his right foot turns out considerably in walking. The above reward will be paid on his apprehension and delivery to me, or in any Jail so that I get him again.<BR>ALEX'R[Alexander] McALISTER, Guardian.<BR>Cumberland County, near Fayetteville,}<BR>February 7, 1838.}<BR>80-3w.
