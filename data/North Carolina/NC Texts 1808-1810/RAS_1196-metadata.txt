Transcript: NOTICE<BR> RANAWAY from the subscriber, a negro fellow named TAFF, about 6 feet 1 or 2 inches high, very black, has long thick lips; he stammers when talking. Perhaps he may have a pass, as he can write himself. I will give twenty dollars reward to any person that will deliver him to me or secure him in goal so that I get him again, and pay all reasonable charges.<BR>The above negro is thirty year[s] of age.<BR> JOHN KOEN.<BR> July 25.
