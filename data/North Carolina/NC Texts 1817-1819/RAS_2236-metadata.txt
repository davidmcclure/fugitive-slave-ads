Transcript: RANAWAY or stolen from the subscriber the last day of June, a negro man named JIM, no doubt he has altered his name, he is about five feet seven or eight inches high, and when spoken to has a down look; he is going on 20 years of age, is very black, has a mark on his cheek, occasioned by a bite of another boy. Any person apprehending said fellow and lodging him in any Jail and giving information so that I get him again, shall receive Fifty dollars in this State, and if out of this State, one hundred dollars--he was raised by me in Mecklenburg County, N.C.<BR>PATRICK JOHNSTON.<BR>September 24.
