Transcript: Runaway<BR>From the Subscriber on the sixth Inst. two Negro Men by the name of CADER & SURRY. Cader is about twenty years old, about five feet nine inches , knock-kneed, stout belly, high rumpt, short upper lip, rather shews his teeth. Surry is about seventeen years, about five feet eight inches high, stout made, his under jaw long, shews his upper teeth, small voice. We have reason to believe they have met with a friend, who has given them a free pass. We rather suppose they will make for Roanoke. <BR>Any person who will apprehend and secure them in a jail so I get them shall be handsomely rewarded.<BR>M. COLLINS,<BR>For JOHN BREWER.<BR>October 15th, 1817.
