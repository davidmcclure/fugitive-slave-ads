Transcript: NOTICE.<BR>RUNAWAY from the Subscriber on the 17th inst. on Middle creek in Wake county, a negro Man named Lewis commonly called Luee, about fifty five years old about five feet ten inches high, proportionably [sic] made, rather round shouldered and bald headed. We suppose he will make towards Bridge's ferry upon Roanoke. Any person that will give themselves the trouble to take him up and confine him in Jail so that I get him, shall be handsomely rewarded for their trouble.<BR>U. COLLINS, for JOHN BREWER.<BR>May 21.
