Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, in Trenton, about the 1st of August last, a negro woman named SILVEY.  She is about 30 years of age, about 5 feet, 6 or 7 inches high, well proportioned and of a black complexion.  She was formerly owned by Mr. Solomon Collins of White Oak.  The above reward will be given to any person that will deliver said negro to me, or secure her in any jail in this district, so that I get her again.<BR>PETER ANDREWS.<BR>June 13th.--13tf
