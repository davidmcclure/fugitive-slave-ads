Transcript: 30 DOLLARS REWARD <BR>RAN-AWAY from the Subscriber sometime in Sept.[September] last, a Negro man by the name of <BR>DAVE, <BR>About 37 years of age, 5 feet 10 or 11 Inches high, slender made; somewhat Pock broken, has a scar on the breast cut with a knife, from appearance, and a load of shot above his knee,--he is sensible and cunning and it is thought he is lurking about in the neighborhood of Tarborough, having a mother in that place.<BR>I will give the above reward of $30 if delivered to me, or secured in any Jail so that I get him, and all reasonable chages paid.--All persons are forwarned from harboring employing or carrrying him out of the State, under the penalty of the law.<BR>Wm.[William] Palmer.<BR>Newbern Dec.[December] 11th, 1817.--6
