Transcript: 20 DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber a likely negro fellow called <BR>TUESDAY; <BR>of about the age of 20 years. Well set, small eyes, nose turns a little upwards, and face of an accute, sly appearance. He went off without provocation. The above reward will be given for him, if delivered to me, or secured in any jail so that I get him. <BR>Geo.[George] Wilson.<BR>Durhams Creek, Beaufort County, Feb.[February] 28th, 1818.
