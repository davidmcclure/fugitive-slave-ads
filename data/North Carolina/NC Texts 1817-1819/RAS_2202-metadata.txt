Transcript: Five Dollars Reward.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscriber about 15 days ago, a large likely Negro Fellow named Stephen, said fellow was hired by me in January last, has remarkable thick lips, is otherwise stout and well made, and is inclined to be rather talkative; it is supposed that he is lurking about the city of Raleigh, as he has been seen there, and where he is well known as Goodwin's Stephen. The above reward will be paid to any person who will deliver said fellow to me 9 miles south of Raleigh, or to the Jailer of Wake County.<BR>ALBERT UTLEY.<BR>Wake County, Dec. 10, 1817.
