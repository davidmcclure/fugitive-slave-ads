Transcript: Twenty Five Dollars <BR>REWARD. <BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on the 6th Inst. a Negro Man named <BR>BEN, <BR>about 42 years of age, five feet 5 inches high, yellow complexion, thick hair, and a little grey on the top of his head, teeth wide apart, very large hands and feet, a Scar across his nose, when spoken to he smiles before he speaks. All persons are forwarned from harboring, employing or carrying him out of this State, under the penalty of the law. <BR>I will give the above reward for him to be delivered to me or secured in Jail so that I get him, and all reasonable expences shall be paid. <BR>EDMUND HATCH, Sen'r.<BR>Craven County, May 10, 1817.
