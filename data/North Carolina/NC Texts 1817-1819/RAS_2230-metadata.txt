Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR> RUNAWAY from the Subscriber on the 23d instant, my  negro man LION, about thirty years of age of a dark complexion, had on when he left home Trowsers of tow cloth and a Cotton shirt; carried with him other clothes--a blue broadcloth coat, about half worn, the rest not recollected--He was raised in Chatham county, N.C. and I expect he will aim that way. Any person who will deliver said negro to me in Pitt county, or secure him in any Jail, so that I get him agian shall receive the above reward and all other reasonable expences paid.<BR>OLIVER PRINCE.<BR>July 25th.
