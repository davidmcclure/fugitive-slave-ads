Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward. <BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 1st of September last, a likely Mullatto[sic] Girl by the name of <BR>MARY<BR>she is about 16 years of age, thick built, generally wears a pleasing countenance, and is very likely.--I understand she is lurking about Newbern and its vicinity, and passes by the name of free CHARLOTTE; she formerly belonged to the estate of Anthony Hatch, dec'd.--I will give the above reward to any person who will secure her in Jail, so that I get her agian, or deliver her to me in Onslow County. <BR>DURANT HATCH. <BR>Onslow County, N.C. February 14, 1818 -4w. (PAID.)--76.
