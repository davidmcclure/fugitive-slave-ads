Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward. RANAWAY from the subscriber in Randolph county, on the 1st inst. a negro fellow by the name of DAVE, about 22 years of age, black complected, about 5 feet 10 inches high, strong and well built;  he rather lisps when he speaks, is tolerably well clothed. It is expected he will try to get to Gates county. The above reward will be given to any person who will confine him in any jail so that I get him again.<BR>WM.[WILLIAM] BROWN.<BR>Randolph county, Nov. 5, 1817.   45-3w-pd.
