Transcript: 10 Dollars Reward <BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber on the 13th inst. a Negro fellow by the name of CAESAR. <BR>About 35 years of age, dark complexion, 5 feet 5 inches high, said fellow is well known in Newbern, has a sister at Mr. Joseph Oliver's, and it is likely that he is lurking about that place or neighborhood.--I will give the above reward to any person that will deliver him to me, or secure him in Jail so that I get him and all reasonable expences shall be paid. <BR>Masters of vessels and all others are forwarned from harboring, employing or carrying him away, under the penalty of the law. <BR>Wm.[William] P. Moore.<BR>Craven County, Sept. 20, 1817.
