Transcript: Twenty Dollars Reward.<BR>RAN AWAY from the subscriber, some time in the month of November last, a negro man named ISAAC, about 22 years of age, six feet high, tolerably stout made, and of a black complexion.  It is supposed that he is lurking about Little or Big Swift creek, Craven county.<BR>I will give the above reward to any person who will bring said negro to me, or secure him in any jail so that I get him; and will pay all reasonable expences.<BR>JOHN REEL.<BR>Swift Creek, May 2, 1818.--tf.--7.
