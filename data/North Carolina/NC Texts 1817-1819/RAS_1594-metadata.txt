Transcript: RANAWAY from the subscriber, on the night of the 17th inst. a negro man named<BR>ARTHUR<BR>about 27 years of age, five feet nine or ten inches high, slender made, of a yellowish complexion and has a blemish in his left eye--he had on homespun clothes when he left home.  It is expected that he will make for Newbern, where his father, whose name is Arthur, the property of Mr. Shackelford lives.<BR>A liberal and satisfactory reward will be given to any person who will apprehend and secure said fellow so that the subscriber gets him again.<BR>N.B. All masters and owners of vessels are hereby forwarned from employing or harbruring[harbouring] said fellow.<BR>JOHN ROBERTS.<BR>Carteret co. June 18th, 1818 -- 15tf
