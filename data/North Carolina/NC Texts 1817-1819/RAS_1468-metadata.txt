Transcript: 100 Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY on the first of September last, a negro man named JOE, dark complexioned, his legs small, feet large, has an impediment in his speech, and stammers when he speaks; he is about five feet nine inches high, & straight in his form.  Joe was formerly owned by Joseph Wynn.  I will give the above reward to any person who will deliver said negro to me, or secure him in any jail so that I get him.<BR>WM.[WILLIAM] PUGH.<BR>Pitt County, N.C. <BR>May 27, 1818.    5w11
