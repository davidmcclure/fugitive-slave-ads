Transcript: Ran-away from the Roanoke Navigation Company, about the [illegible] instant, FOUR NEGRO MEN,<BR>Joe, Dempsey, March & Willoughby,<BR>JOE is about 25 years of age, was purchased of Wilson & Webster, Camden county, North Carolina.<BR>DEMPSEY, 30 years of age, of Joseph W. Downes, Currituck.<BR>MARCH, 23 years of age, of Gardener Watson, Camden; which three are of common size.<BR>WILLOUGHBY, 28 years of age, purchased of John Mackey, Currituck; and is upwards of 6 feet high.--Joe is black.--the others are somewhat brighter.--TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD will be given for apprehending each of them, so they can be brought into the service of the Company again.<BR>James R. Allen, Agent,<BR>April 16, 1818.     tf609
