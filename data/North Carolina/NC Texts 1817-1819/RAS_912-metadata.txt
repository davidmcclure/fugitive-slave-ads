Transcript: TWENTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN AWAY from the Subscriber living in Rutherford county, on the first of September last, a Negro Woman named ROSS, about twenty four years of age, of a black complexion, five feet 2 inches high, with a scar on one of her arms occasioned in Innoculation of Kine Pock. The above reward will be paid to any person who will deliver said negro to me in Rutherford county, North Carolina, with all reasonable expences paid, or ten Dollars for securing her in a jail so that I get her again.<BR> 100 3p JOHN MOORE.
