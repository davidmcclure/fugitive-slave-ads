Slave name(s): Isome

Transcript: 25 DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 11th ult a negro man named ISOME, 23 years old about 5 feet 11 Inches high, well built, weighing 170 or 180 lbs. has a very good countenance smoth[smooth] skin, & a little inclinded to be yellow speaks with a droaning voice, no marks recollected expect a knot on his neck near the collar bone, about the size of a large rifle ball, occasioned by some wound; he was raised on Roanoke and it was expected he would make for that place, but he has told some negroes that he intended to go to Newbern and get on board of some vessel. I forwarn all masters of vessels from taking him, he may also have obtained a free pass. <BR>The above reward will be given for securing him in any Jail in this state, so I get him. And information given to me living in Cumberland County, N.C. little River. <BR>John Murchison<BR>July 5.
