Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward,<BR>WILL be given for apprehending or delivering to me, or securing in  jail so that I get him, a Negro Fellow, named JACK, formerly the property of Miss Eleanor Houghton, well known in Chowan County. He is about 5 feet 2 or 3 inches high, stout made, and yellow complexion, and has a mother living at T. Charleston's farm about 1 1/2  miles below Edenton; at which place it is probable he occasionally resorts. The above reward and reasonable expences will be paid for securing him as above.<BR>John Bond.<BR>Bertie County, June 8, 1818.   4t616
