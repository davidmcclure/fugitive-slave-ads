Transcript: 100 Dollars Reward<BR>WILL be given to any person who will apprehend and deliver to the subscriber, the following negroes, viz:<BR>Adam, his wife Patience, and their three children,<BR>the two eldest, females, and the youngest four months old a male.  Most likely they will be about Little Swift Creek, or More Creek.<BR>The above reward will be given for the whole, or $50 for the fellow alone, thirty for the wench, and ten for each, or either, of the eldest children.<BR>All persons are hereby warned, not to employ any of them; but him in particular, in getting shingles; as the law will be strictly enforced against offenders in this case.<BR>Fifty dollars will be given to any person on his producing proof of Adam's being thus, or in any other manner employed.<BR>E. PASTEUR.<BR>Farmville, June 13--tf13.
