Transcript: 20 DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN-AWAY from the subscriber on the 8th inst a negro fellow by the name of<BR>MANUEL,<BR>formerly the Property of Frederick Foy Sen'r, he is about five feet eight or ten Inches high, dark complexion, had on when he went away a woolen Round Jacket and homespun pantaloons--he has a wife at Mrs. Margaret Foys, on Trent River, also he has some relations at William S. Hills on Whiteoak which it is very likely he will be lurking about one place or the other.--I will give the above reward of twenty dollars if any person will deliver him to me or secure him in Newbern Jail so that I get him again and all reasonable Charges paid. <BR>All persons are forwarned harboring employing or carrying him away under the Penalty of the law.<BR>Edward Mumford. <BR>Dec[December] 20 1817.
