Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RUNAWAY from the subscribers living near Salisbury Rowan county N.C. on the 27th inst. a negro boy named Tom, dark complexion, spare made, about nineteen years old, one of the middle toes of his right foot much crooked, had on when he went away a pair of pantaloons of blue cloth, with red welts, and tow shirt. Any person who will deliver said negro to me or secure him in jail and write home so that I get him again shall have the above reward.<BR>JOHN POOL.<BR>June 29th, 1818.
