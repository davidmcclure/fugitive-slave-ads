Transcript: 20 DOLLARS REWARD<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber some time in June last a negro man named ISAM, about 25 years of age, dark complexion, stout built, and looks fierce out of his eyes ; he is supposed to be lurking about Washington, N.C. or Lenoir County : he has a pass, which was given him by his former owner, John Hooten, to look him a master, which he uses as a free pass.<BR>The above reward will be given  & all reasonable expences paid, to any person who will apprehend the said negro and deliver him to me or secure him in jail so that I get him.<BR>Masters of vessels, and all others, are forwarned from harbouring, employing or carrying him away, under penalty of the law.<BR>WILEY MOSELY.<BR>Lenoir County July 5th 1818.<BR>19 4w
