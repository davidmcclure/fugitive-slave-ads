Transcript: 25 Dollars Reward.<BR>RANAWAY from the subscriber, on Tuesday the 22d instant, a negro man named<BR>SAM,<BR>formerly owned by George Morse, of Beaufort.  He is about 35 years of age, five feet, seven or eight inches high, stout made, and is a good fiddler.  He sometimes calls himself Giles Miner, and commonly sailed in the packet between Newbern and Beaufort, with Hasty.<BR>The above reward, together with all reasonable expences, will be paid to any person who will apprehend and deliver said fellow to the subscriber, or secure him in any jail.<BR>Masters of vessels, and others, are forwarned from harbouring, employing, or carrying him away, under the penalty of the law.<BR>Libbeus Hunter.<BR>White-Oak,<BR>Carteret county,      Oct. 3d 1818.-tf
