Transcript: TEN DOLLARS REWARD. <BR>RUN AWAY on the 21st. inst. a negro fellow named BRUTUS about twenty three or four years of age, about six feet high, slim built, has a bad sore on one his ancles[sic], and speaks very broken english[sic].--It is supposed he will endeavour to get to Greensville where he has a wife, or to Beaufort, Washington, or Wilmington, where he may try to make his escape, as he has been brought up to the sea. <BR>I will give the above reward of TEN DOLLARS for him if delivered to me or secured in any Jail so that I get him, and all reasonable charges paid. <BR>All persons are forwarned from harboring, employing or carrying him out of the State, under the penalty of the law. <BR>George Reid. <BR>Newbern, September, 27, 1817.
