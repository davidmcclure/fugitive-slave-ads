Transcript: 60 Dollars Reward.<BR>WILL be given for apprehending & delivering to me, or confining in jail so that I get them again. Two Negro Fellows named GEORGE and HARRY, who ranaway on the 30th of August last.<BR>George is about 22 years of age, 5 feet 6 or 7 inches high, light complexion, high forehead, slim and well made.<BR>Harry is about 19 years of age, 5 feet 2 or 3 inches high, black complexion, stout and well made. I will give the above reward if taken up within this State, or $30 for either; or I will give $100 if taken up without this state, or $50 for either, and confined so that I get them.<BR>Masters of vessels are cautioned against carrying them off at their peril.<BR>James Williamson.<BR>Chowan, Sept. 25, 1818.
