Transcript: 20 DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RAN-AWAY From the Subscriber on, the 12th May, two Negroes, a man & boy, JACK BATTLE, and CHARLES EMERY.  Jack is a guinea Negro, about twenty-six years of age, 5 feet 4 inches high, dark complexion, full eyes and large whiskers, he pretends to be a barber, has a wife at Mrs. Sitgraves' in Newbern--had on when he went away, an olive coloured bear skin cloth Jacket & white woolen homespun Trowsers.  Charles is about sixteen years of age, 5 feet 3 or 4 inches high and of a light complexion--had on when he went away, a white cotton homespun jacket, and pale blue casinet Trowsers, patched in several places, a black leather hat &c. has a brother and sister living in Newbern, Bill Emery and Matilda; Matilda lives near Mr. A. Scott's distillery, it is supposed they are lurking about the neighbourhood of Newbern.  The above Reward will be given to any person who will deliver them to me, or secure them in any Jail so that I get them.<BR>All persons are hereby cautioned against harbouring, or carrying them away, under the penalty of the law.<BR>WM. [WILLIAM] P. MOOR.<BR>Craven County, May 12th--tf.
