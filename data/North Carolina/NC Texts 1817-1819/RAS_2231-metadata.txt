Transcript: FIFTY DOLLARS REWARD.<BR>RANAWAY from the Subscriber on the night of the 19th inst. my negro man, ARGIL, about 30 years old, 4 feet 9 or 10 inches high, the said fellow Argil curls his hair on his forehead, he is of a light complexion for a full blooded negro, walks quick and very proud for a slave, and talks short and quick when spoken to, he sometimes calls himself Hubbard, & may probably pass by that name--he formerly belonged to a Mrs. Collins of Norfolk, of Portsmouth Virginia, and purchased from her by the late Barnana Bulls, Dec. some years past. I will give a reward of $25 for his safe delivery in the jail of this country, or $50 for his delivery in any jail in the United States, so that I can get possession of him again.<BR>Johnston County Co. N.C. August 20, 1818 <BR>The editor or the Norfolk Herald will please Insert the above 3 weeks and forward his account to this Office.
